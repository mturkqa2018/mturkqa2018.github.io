The location of the state of Alabama in the United States of America

The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to the U.S. state of Alabama: 1

Alabama – 22nd U.S. state to be admitted to the Union, which is located in the South. It is bordered by Tennessee to the north, Georgia to the east, Florida and the Gulf of Mexico to the south, and Mississippi to the west. Alabama is a major producer of chickens, which accounts for almost half of the state's agriculture. 2
