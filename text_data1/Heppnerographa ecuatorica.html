Heppnerographa ecuatorica is a species of moth of the family Tortricidae. It is found in Ecuador. 2

The wingspan is about 12.5Â mm. The ground colour of the forewings is yellowish brown, but paler beyond the end of the median cell near a whitish streak. The hindwings are brownish cream, but more fuscous outwards. 3
