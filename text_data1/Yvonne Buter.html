Yvonne Buter (born 18 March 1959 in Schiedam, Zuid-Holland) is a former Dutch field hockey goalkeeper, who won the bronze medal with the National Women's Team at the 1988 Summer Olympics. 1

From 1985 to 1988 she played a total number of 29 international matches for Holland. Buter, a stand-in for first choice Det de Beus, retired after the 1988 Summer Olympics in South Korea. 2
