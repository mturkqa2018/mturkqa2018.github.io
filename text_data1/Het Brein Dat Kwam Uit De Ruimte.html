Het Brein Dat Kwam Uit De Ruimte ("The brain that came from space") is an experimental electronic Dutch band from Heemskerk. 1

Biography

Active since 2006, Het Brein won the IJmond Popprijs, a band competition, in 2007. In 2008, they also participated in the IJmond competition, and again in 2009, when they made the finals. As the 2007 winners of the Popmania competition, they were given the closing slot at the annual Popmania festival in Heerhugowaard. 2

In 2009, they played twice at Beeckestijn Pop, a festival in Velsen attended by 15,000. Their regular session was a "smashing" show, and they added an all-acoustic session which was filmed by VPRO's 3VOOR12. 3
