The End of Chaos is the thirteenth studio album by American thrash metal band Flotsam and Jetsam, released on January 18, 2019. It is the band's first album to feature drummer Ken Mary, replacing Jason Bittner who left Flotsam and Jetsam in 2017 to join Overkill. 1
