List of African records in Olympic weightlifting

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The following are the African records in Olympic weightlifting. Records are maintained in each weight class for the snatch lift, clean and jerk lift, and the total for both lifts by the Weightlifting Federation of Africa (WFA). 1
