Gorchakov Memorial School is a private boarding school that opened in 1999 in Pavlovsk, a suburb of Saint Petersburg, Russia. It was named after Russian diplomat Alexander Gorchakov, a classmate of Alexander Pushkin's. The main dormatory and dining area are in an historic, Alexander Brullov country house. 1
