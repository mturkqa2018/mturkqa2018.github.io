"Only One" is the fourth overall single and second British single released by Australian singer Peter Andre from his second studio album, Natural. The single was released in the United Kingdom only on 1 March 1996, as an alternative to the Australian-only Get Down on It, via Mushroom Records. The song peaked at No. 16 on the UK Singles Chart. 2
