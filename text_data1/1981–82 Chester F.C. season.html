The 1981â€“82 season was the 44th season of competitive association football in the Football League played by Chester, an English club based in Chester, Cheshire. 1

Also, it was the seventh season spent in the Third Division after the promotion from the Fourth Division in 1975. Alongside competing in the Football League the club also participated in the FA Cup and the Football League Cup. 2
