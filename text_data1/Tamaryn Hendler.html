Tamaryn Hendler (born 12 August 1992), better known as Tammy Hendler, is a Belgian tennis player. 2

Hendler reached the quarter-finals in the 2008 US Open Girls' singles tournament and the semi-finals at 2008 Junior Wimbledon. She has won 4 ITF Singles titles and 3 ITF Doubles titles in her career, as well as some of the biggest junior tournaments in the world, including Little Mo and Orange Bowl. She has represented Belgium in the Fed Cup competition in several ties since 2007. 3

Hendler currently resides in Boca Raton, Florida. 4

She has competed in all 4 Grand Slam qualifying events in 2012. At Roland Garros, she reached the second round of qualifying for the first time in her career. 5
