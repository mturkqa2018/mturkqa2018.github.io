South Mosman ferry wharf is located on the northern side of Sydney Harbour serving the Sydney suburb of Mosman. It is located at the end of Musgrave St, and hence is marked "Musgrave St. Wharf" on some publications. 2

Services

South Mosman wharf is served by Sydney Ferries Mosman Bay services operated by First Fleet class ferries. 3
