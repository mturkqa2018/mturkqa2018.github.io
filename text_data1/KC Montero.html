Casey Wyatt Montero Miller (born June 17, 1978 in Tacoma, Washington), better known as KC Montero is a Filipino American Radio DJ, MTV VJ, TV host and actor. 1

Personal life

He was born on June 17, 1978. He is the brother of Troy Montero and Colby Miller. He was born to an American father of German and Irish descent and a U.S. born Filipina mother. He started his career as an MTV VJ in 1999 after winning the MTV VJ Hunt along with Belinda Panelo. 2

He was a former host on GMA 7's Sunday variety show SOP (Sobrang Okey Pare) and now currently seen on Party Pilipinas, Studio 23's Usi. He co-produced and hosted the MTV Pilipinas Video Music Awards 2006 and is a radio personality on the early morning show called "The KC Show" on Wave 891. 3

He was married to singer-actress Geneva Cruz but now are separated. 4
