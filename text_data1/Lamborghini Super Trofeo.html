The Lamborghini Super Trofeo is an international motor racing series. 1

Lamborghini Super Trofeo drivers compete in identical Lamborghini Huracán Super Trofeo Evo cars, based on the Lamborghini Huracán LP 620-2 replacing the older Gallardo version. These championships races are held in North America, Europe, Middle East and Asia, and the World Final at the and of each year reunite all the drivers competing in each region. The drivers are also divided according to categories – Pro, Pro-Am, Am, and LB Cup. 2
