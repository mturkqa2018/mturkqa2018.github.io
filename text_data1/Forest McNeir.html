Forest Waldemar "Seluya" McNeir (August 16, 1875 â€“ May 9, 1957) was an American sport shooter who competed in the 1920 Summer Olympics. He was born in Washington, D.C. and died in Houston, Texas. 2

In 1920, McNeir won the gold medal as member of the American team in the team clay pigeons competition. 3
