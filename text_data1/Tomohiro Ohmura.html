Biography

  • Ohmura graduated from Numazu Higashi High School, after entering his first course, he entered to Meiji University School of Commerce.
  • He made double act along with Kensuke Fujita at NSC, in April 1997.
  • Ohmura announced his marriage at the same time as his comedy partner in April 2006. In the same year, their eldest son was born. Ohmura's eldest daughter was later born in 2014, and is currently a father of two children.
