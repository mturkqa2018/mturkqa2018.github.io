Baek Mok-Hwa (born (1989-08-30)30 August 1989) is a South Korean female volleyball player. She is part of the South Korea women's national volleyball team. 1

She participated in the 2014 FIVB Volleyball World Grand Prix. On club level she played for Korea Ginseng Corporation in 2014. 2
