DAV Public School, Kusmunda is located in Kusmunda Area of Korba, Chhattisgarh, India. Founded in 1983 by South Eastern Coalfields Limited and D.A.V. College Managing Committee. It is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education, New Delhi and is governed by South Eastern Coalfields Limited. 1
