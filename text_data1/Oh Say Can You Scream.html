Oh Say Can You Scream is a VHS video of live performances by Skid Row featuring songs from the band's self-titled debut album. It was released on December 4, 1990. Running time is 100 minutes. The video includes 12 live performances from all over the world plus uncensored clips of "18 and Life", "Youth Gone Wild", "I Remember You", and "Piece of Me". 1
