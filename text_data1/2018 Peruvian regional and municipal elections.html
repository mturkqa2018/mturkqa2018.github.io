Municipal and regional elections were held in Peru on 7 October 2018. In the election, Peruvians voted for governors, deputy governors and regional councilors at a regional level while at a municipal level, citizens voted for mayors and municipal councilors. Those elected take office 1 January 2019 and serve their term until 31 December 2022. 1
