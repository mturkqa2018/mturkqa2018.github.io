Pleun Strik (born 27 May 1944) is a Dutch former international footballer who played as a defender. 2

Career

He played two matches for the Netherlands national football team in the 1970 FIFA World Cup qualification tournament, and he made the Dutch squad for the 1974 FIFA World Cup, although he did not play in the tournament. 3

Strik played club football with PSV Eindhoven for over eight years in the late 1960s and 1970s, and made a total of 28 appearances for them in UEFA club competitions.
