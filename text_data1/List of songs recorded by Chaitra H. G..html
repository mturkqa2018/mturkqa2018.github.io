List of songs recorded by Chaitra H. G.

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Chaitra H. G. (born 18 June 1984) is an Indian playback singer who sings predominantly in Kannada films. She has also recorded songs in Telugu, Tamil and Malayalam films, with the number totalling to over 600. 1
