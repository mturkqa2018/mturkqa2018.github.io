"No Candle No Light" is a song by British singer Zayn featuring Trinidadian-American rapper Nicki Minaj. It was released on 15 November 2018 by RCA Records, as the sixth single from his second studio album Icarus Falls (2018). 1

Background and release

On 14 November 2018, Zayn shared on Twitter a teaser for the song with only the word "Thursday" spelled out on the screen and Minaj's signature "Young Money" in the background. 2

Music

The Fader called the song a "tropical house bop" and compared its synths to those in "Sorry" by Justin Bieber. Rolling Stone called the track an EDM-pop exploration of "romantic frustration". Exclaim! noted the song's "booming EDM beat with Zayn's trademark emotional croon", and pointed out that Minaj sings instead of rapping. 3

Cover art

Complex noted the song's "cigarette-assisted cover art", which shows Malik holding a cigarette to his mouth. 4
