Beechboro Road is a north-south road located in the northeastern suburbs of Perth, Western Australia, connecting Morley and Bayswater with areas further north. Until the construction of the nearby section of Tonkin Highway between 1984 and 1992, Beechboro Road was one of northeastern Perth's most important routes. The road is discontinuous at Tonkin Highway, with the section from Gnangara Road to Walter Road officially known as Beechboro Road North, and the section running through Bayswater as Beechboro Road South. 2

The location of the intersection with the Reid Highway route has required impact analysis and environmental review 3
