Karen Courtland Kelly (born October 12, 1970, in Orange, New Jersey) is a former American pair skater. With partner Todd Reynolds, she won the bronze medal at the United States Figure Skating Championships in 1993 and 1994 and finished 14th at the 1994 Winter Olympic Games. 1

Courtland married Patrick Kelly. She is a motivational speaker and has taught pilates. She is president of the World Figure Sport Society. 2
