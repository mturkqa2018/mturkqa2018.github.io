The men's 4 Ã— 400 metres relay event at the 2008 Summer Olympics took place on 22 and 23 August at the Beijing National Stadium. 1

There were 16 NOCs competing at this event. These 16 NOCs were selected by the average of the two best marks at the qualifying period. The final was won by the United States in the new Olympic record time 2:55.39. 2
