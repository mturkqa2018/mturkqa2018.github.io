Jadson Viera Castro or simply Jadson (born 4 August 1981) is an Uruguayan retired football defender born in Brazil and current football coach. He is currently the assistant coach of Nacional. 2

Biography

Jadson was born in Santana do Livramento, a city located in the south of the Brazilian state of Rio Grande do Sul, along the border with the Uruguayan city of Rivera. 3

Jadson started his professional career in 2001 with Danubio in Uruguay, during his time with the club he has helped them to win 2 Apertura, 3 Clausura and 2 overall Uruguayan league championships. 4

Jadson spent part of 2005 on loan to Atlante in Mexico before returning to Uruguay in 2006. 5

After helping Danubio to claim the overall league championship in 2007, Jadson moved to Argentina to join Club AtlÃ©tico LanÃºs where he helped the club to win the Apertura 2007 tournament, their first ever top flight league title. 6

In July 2010, he signed with Vasco da Gama. 7
