Stadium

Further information: Fazanerija City Stadium

Fazanerija City Stadium (Slovene: Mestni stadion Fazanerija) is a multi-use stadium in Murska Sobota, Slovenia. The stadium was built in 1983 and has a capacity of 3,782 covered seats. With the standing area included, the total capacity of the stadium is around 5,500. 4
