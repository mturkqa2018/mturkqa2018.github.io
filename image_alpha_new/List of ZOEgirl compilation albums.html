With All of My Heart â€“ The Greatest Hits is the first compilation album released by ZOEgirl on December 27, 2005. It introduced two new song which were previously unreleased: "Unchangeable" and "One Day". The album was reissued on October 28, 2008 as Greatest Hits. It retains the same songs and order, except for the removal of three songs: "Give Me One Reason", "Feel Alright" and "Different Kind Of Free". 2
