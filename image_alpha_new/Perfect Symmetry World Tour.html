The Perfect Symmetry World Tour was the third tour given by the British Band Keane, following the launch of the album Perfect Symmetry. 2

Set list

As was the case on previous tours, Keane's set list changed regularly. Among new cover songs, they played The Beatles's "Golden Slumbers", "Disco 2000" by Pulp and "Under Pressure" by Queen and David Bowie. 3
