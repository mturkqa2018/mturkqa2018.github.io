Shri Nawal Kishore Bharatiya Municipal Girls Postgraduate College (N.K.B.M.G Postgraduate College) is situated in Chandausi town of Moradabad district in Uttar Pradesh, India and it was founded on 6 October 1964, by the Late Nawal Kishore Bharatiya. It is affiliated from M.J.P. Rohilkhand University, Bareilly. 2

Initially N.K.B.M.G Postgraduate College started graduate classes in Hindi, English, Sanskrit, Economics, Education, Sociology, Political Science, Music Vocal and Music Instrumental. B Ed classes were started in 1973 and those in Home Science were started in 1984. With the beginning of MA classes in Sanskrit and Political Science, the institution became postgraduate college. 3
