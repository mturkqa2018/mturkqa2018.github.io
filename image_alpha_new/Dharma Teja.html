Dharma Teja is a 1989 Indian Telugu film directed by Perala and produced by Sainath. It stars Krishnam Raju, Sharada, Raadhika and Vani Viswanath in lead roles. The music was composed by Vidyasagar. The film was a remake of Tamil film Poonthotta Kaavalkaaran. 2
