It was created by international law students at Kiev National University's Institute of International Relations in 2005. The journal accepts material from all international law students at the university; students also prepare each issue for publication, supervised by Ukrainian legal scholars. Articles written in English or Russian are occasionally included, but most are written in Ukrainian. The editors state they hope to publish more English- and Russian-language articles in the future by recruiting more international students to the institute. 1
