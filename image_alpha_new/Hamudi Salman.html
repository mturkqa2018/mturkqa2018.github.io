Background

Salman has two brothers, Amer and Mussa, both of whom also became professional footballers with Hapoel Jerusalem. 3

Playing career

Midway through the Liga Leumit season of 2008/09, Salman made national news when he deserted the club and stated his intention to continue playing football for Jebel al-Mukaber of the Palestinian West Bank Premier League. 4
