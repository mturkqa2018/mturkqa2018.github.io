2000 Waveney District Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

1

The 2000 Waveney Council election took place on 4 May 2000 to elect members of Waveney District Council in Suffolk, England. One third of the council was up for election and the Labour party stayed in overall control of the council. 2

After the election, the composition of the council was 3

  • Labour 28
  • Conservative 13
  • Liberal Democrat 4
  • Independent 2
  • Others 1
