"Give It Up" is a song by English singer Nathan Sykes, featuring vocals from American rapper G-Eazy. The song was released in the United Kingdom on 29 April 2016 as the third single from his debut studio album Unfinished Business (2016). The song peaked at number 56 on the UK Singles Chart. 1
