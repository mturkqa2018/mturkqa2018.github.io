Seachanges is a piece of music written by Raymond Deane in 1993. 1

The piece is scored for Flute and Piccolo in G, Piano, Violin, Violoncello, and Percussion. The percussion includes Gong, maracas, rainstick, Crotales, Marimba, Cymbals, Guiro and Bass Drum. 2
