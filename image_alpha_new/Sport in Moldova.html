Sports

Basketball

See also: Moldova national basketball team

Moldova has an increasingly successful team at the FIBA European Championship for Small Countries. There, Team Moldova won two silver medals until today. (2008 and 2012) 1

Cycling

The most prestigious cycling race is the Moldova President's Cup, which was first run in 2004. 2

Football

See also: Moldova national football team

Football is the most popular sport in Moldova. 3

Rugby

Rugby union is popular as well. Registered players have doubled, and almost 10,000 spectators turn up at every European Nations Cup match. 4

Wrestling

Trânta (a form of wrestling) is the national sport in Moldova. 5
