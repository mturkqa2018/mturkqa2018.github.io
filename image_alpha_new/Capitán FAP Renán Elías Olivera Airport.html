The Capitán FAP Renán Elías Olivera Airport (IATA: PIO, ICAO: SPSO) is an airport serving Pisco, an oceanside city in the Ica Region of Peru. It is used by the Peruvian Army, but it can be also used by civil aircraft. 1

A new terminal building entered service in 2015. The new terminal is expected to help bring new airlines and destinations for the city, and plans to become a gateway for international passengers. 2

The Pisco VOR-DME (Ident: SCO) and non-directional beacon (Ident: SCO) are located on the field. 3
