Lines

Mejirodai Station is served by the Keio Takao Line, and is located 4.3 kilometers from the terminus of the line at Kitano and 40.4 kilometers from Shinjuku Station. 2

Station layout

This station consists of two opposed side platforms serving two tracks. The platforms and tracks are in a cutting, and the station building is located on the embankment above. 3
