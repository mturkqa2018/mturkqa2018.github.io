The 1995 AFL draft was held at the conclusion of the 1995 Australian Football League (AFL) season. 1

The AFL draft is the annual draft of new unsigned players by Australian rules football clubs that participate in the main competition of that sport, the Australian Football League. 2

Clubs receive picks based on the position in which they finish on the ladder during the season, although these picks can be swapped around by teams for trading players. 3
