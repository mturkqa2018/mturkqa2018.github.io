Description

The constituency is largely composed of the north of Mulhouse and its suburbs of Illzach and Wittenheim, however it also includes the canton of Sierentz to the south east. 3

In 2012 the constituency was unique among the constituencies of Haut-Rhin by being represented by someone other than the UMP, however the victorious party New Centre is closely allied to them. 4
