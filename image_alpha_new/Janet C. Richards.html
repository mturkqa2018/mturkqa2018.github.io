Janet C. Richards is a professor in the College of Education at the University of South Florida, Tampa. where she teaches courses in literacy theory and methods, writing, and qualitative research. 1

She is Senior Editor of the Journal of Reading Education [1] and a literacy scholar for the International Reading Association; she has worked with classroom teachers and higher education faculty in Thailand, Azerbaijan, Estonia, Pakistan and Romania. She is also a former elementary classroom teacher and writes extensively on preservice and inservice teachersâ€™ communication skills. 2

Selected Research

Richard's current research examines the connection between reading and writing. 3
