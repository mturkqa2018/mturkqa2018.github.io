Miss Indonesia 2010, the 6th edition of the Miss Indonesia beauty pageant, was held at the Central Park in Jakarta, Indonesia on June 1, 2010. Asyifa Latief of West Java was crowned as Miss Indonesia 2010 by outgoing titleholder, Karenina Sunny Halim of Jakarta SCR. She represented Indonesia in the Miss World 2010 pageant but she was unplaced. Representatives of the 33 provinces competed for the crown, which was broadcast live on RCTI. The event was hosted by Sandra Angelia and Uli Herdinansyah. The event was also attended by Miss World 2009, Kaiane Aldorino and Chairwoman of the Miss World Organization, Julia Morley. 1
