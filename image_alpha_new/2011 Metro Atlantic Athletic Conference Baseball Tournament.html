The 2011 Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference Baseball Tournament took place from May 24â€“27. The top four regular season finishers of the league's teams met in the double-elimination tournament held at Mercer County Waterfront Park in Trenton, New Jersey. Manhattan won their second tournament championship and earned the conference's automatic bid to the 2011 NCAA Division I Baseball Tournament. 1
