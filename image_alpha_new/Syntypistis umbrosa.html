Syntypistis umbrosa is a species of moth of the family Notodontidae. It is found in India, China (Fujian, Guangdong, Guangxi, Hainan, Sichuan, Yunnan), Taiwan, Myanmar, Malaysia, Indonesia and Vietnam. 2
