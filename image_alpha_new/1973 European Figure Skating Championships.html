The 1973 European Figure Skating Championships were the European Figure Skating Championships of the 1972-1973 season. Elite senior-level figure skaters from European ISU Member Nations competed for the title of European Champion. Skaters competed in the disciplines of ladies' singles, men's singles, pair skating, and ice dancing. 2

In 1973, the European Championships were held in Cologne, West Germany from 6 to 11 February 1973. 3
