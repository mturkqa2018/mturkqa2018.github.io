Haubourdin is a commune in the Nord department in northern France. 1

It is part of the Urban Community of Lille Métropole. 2

Heraldry

 The arms of Haubourdin are blazoned :

Gules, a lion Or, armed, langued and crowned azure. (Aix-en-Pévèle, Emmerin, and Haubourdin use the same arms.) 3
