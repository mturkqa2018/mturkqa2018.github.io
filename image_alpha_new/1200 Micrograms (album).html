1200 Micrograms is the eponymous debut full-length album of psychedelic trance artists 1200 Micrograms which was released in 2002. 1

Notes

  • The track "DMT" does not appear on the double-LP version.
  • The sample in the song "Mescaline" is from the movie Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas.
  • The sample in the song "DMT" is from the movie The Matrix.
  • The sample in the song "Salvia Divinorum" is from Terence McKenna.
