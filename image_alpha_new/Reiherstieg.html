The Reiherstieg (German pronunciation (help·info)) is an anabranch of the river Elbe in the Port of Hamburg, Germany. 1

It connects the Süderelbe and the Norderelbe. To avoid tideflow, its natural mouths are closed by locks, although the middle access through the Rethe basin isn't locked. 2
