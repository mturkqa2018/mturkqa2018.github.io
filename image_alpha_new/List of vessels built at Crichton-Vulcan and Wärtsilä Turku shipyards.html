List of vessels built at Crichton-Vulcan and Wärtsilä Turku shipyards covers all projects built during 1924−1989 (1990, 1991) at Crichton-Vulcan (from 1965 Wärtsilä Turku Shipyard) and Wärtsilä Perno shipyard. 1

  • Yard number is a project-specific running number. Unrealised projects: 802–807, 868–869, 1 015, 1 029–1 030, 1 032, 1 174, 1 253 and 1 299–1 300.
  • Name is the original name of the vessel.
  • Type, type of the vessel.
  • Shipowner shows the assigner and the country (flag).
  • Keel lay or order date is the date when the keel is laid on the dock, or the order has been signed off. Since project number 1 083 the date means the order date; that is marked in Italic.
  • Handover is the date when the vessel has been handed over to the customer. A vessel handed over by the estate following the bankruptcy of Wärtsilä Marine is marked with *. The ships finished by the follower Masa-Yards are marked with MY.
