Macau competed at the 2017 Asian Indoor and Martial Arts Games from 17 to 27 September, 2017.Macau sent a delegation consisting of 35 competitors for the multi-sport event. 1

Macau claimed its only medal (bronze medal) in the dancesport event. 2
