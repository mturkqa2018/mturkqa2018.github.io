Melanie Keil (born (1984-05-07)7 May 1984) is a German volleyball player. She is a member of the Germany women's national volleyball team. 1

She was part of the German national team at the 2013 Montreux Volley Masters. 2

She played for University of Nebraska, and Florida State University. 3
