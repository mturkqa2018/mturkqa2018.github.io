Sultana Daku is a 1972 Bollywood drama film directed by Mohammed Hussain. The film stars Helen Jairag Richardson and Dara Singh. This movie is based on a person named Sultana who fought against British imperialists who used to torture Indians. Sultana Daku ka quila is located in Najibabad city, Bijnor district (Shobhit). 2
