Uskallaqta (Quechua uska poor, llaqta place (village, town, city, country, nation), other spellings Juscallacta, Uscallacta, Uskallacta) is an archaeological complex with stone tombs (chullpa) and rooms in Peru. It is situated in the Arequipa Region, Caylloma Province, Chivay District, southwest of Chivay. 1
