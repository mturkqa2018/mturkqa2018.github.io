Early life

Shabarish was born on 30 December 1984 in Chennai, Tamil Nadu and had completed his schooling at Vadapalani Matriculation Higher Secondary School, Chennai. He graduated as an engineer from Sathyabama University, then persuaded his acting career by 2011. 3

Film career

Shabarish introduction into the film industry was by his father, stunt director FEFSI Vijayan, through Markandeyan, followed by opportunity knocked him through Rasu Madhuravan's Pandi Oliperukki Nilayam (2012). However, both films had low-key releases and went unnoticed. He is currently acting in Asurakulam, directed by Vinmeengal(2012) fame Vignesh Menon. 4
