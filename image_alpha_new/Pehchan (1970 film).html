Pehchan is a 1970 Filmfare Award winning Bollywood romance film produced & directed by Sohanlal Kanwar, & the story is by Sachin Bhowmick. The film stars Manoj Kumar, Babita, and Balraj Sahni. Music is by Shankar Jaikishan. 2

Awards and nominations

  • Filmfare Best Supporting Actress Award for Chand Usmani
  • Filmfare Best Music Director Award for Shankar Jaikishan
  • Filmfare Best Lyricist Award for Varma Malik's composition "Sabse Bada Nadaan"
  • Filmfare Best Male Playback Award for Mukesh for "Sabse Bada Nadaan"
  • Filmfare Nomination for Best Film
  • Filmfare Nomination for Best Director - Sohanlal Kanwar
  • Filmfare Nomination for Best Story - Sachin Bhowmick
  • Filmfare Nomination for Best Lyricist - Neeraj for the song "Bas Yehi Apradh"[1]
