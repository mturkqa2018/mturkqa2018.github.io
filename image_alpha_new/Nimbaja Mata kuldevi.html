Nimbaja Mata Kuldevi is a Gautam gotra temple of Shrimali Brahmins community. The temple is located in the small village Narta, near Bhinmal, Jalore district, in Rajasthan. 2

1

Kuldevi

Every year, Patoutsav (anniversaries of murti-consecration) and Navchandi Path are performed in the temple. The Chaturdashi of the Vaishakh Shukla Paksha is the day of kuldevi, when many people come to the temple to pray. 3
