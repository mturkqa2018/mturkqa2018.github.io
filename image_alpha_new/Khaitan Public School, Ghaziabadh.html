Khaitan Public School is a higher secondary school in Sector 5, Rajendra Nagar, Sahibabad, Dwarka Enclave, Sahibabad, Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh 201005, India, established in 2001. Khaitan Public School is set amid landscaped grounds covering 3.5 acres (14,000Â m). 2

School profile

Khaitan Public School, Ghaziabadh is a fully airconditioned Senior Secondary School at Sector - V, Rajendar Nagar, Ghaziabadh, Uttar Pradesh. It started in 2002. The school is divided into Pre-School, Primary School, Middle School and Higher Secondary School. 3
