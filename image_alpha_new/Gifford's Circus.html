Giffords Circus is a traditional English circus which tours the Cotswolds area every summer. 1

Established in 2000, Giffords Circus is a small circus company, founded and run by Nell and Toti Gifford, that tours market towns of the south west. When not on the road the founders concentrate on landscape architecture. Nell is the sister of the designer Emma Bridgewater. 2
