Athletics at the 2004 Summer Paralympics – Men's 100 metres T52–54

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Men's 100m races for wheelchair athletes at the 2004 Summer Paralympics were held in the Athens Olympic Stadium. Events were held in three disability classes. 1
