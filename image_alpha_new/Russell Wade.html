Life and career

Russell Wade was born on June 21, 1917 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, USA. He signed an RKO contract in 1942. Having appeared in 80 films, his last one was Beyond Glory in 1948. 2

With a boost from actor Pat O'Brien, Wade moved up from being an extra to having a role that was "expanded and tailored" for him in The Iron Major (1943). 3

Death

On December 9, 2006, Wade died in Palm Springs, California, at age 89. 4
