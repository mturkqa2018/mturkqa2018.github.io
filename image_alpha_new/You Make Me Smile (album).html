You Make Me Smile is an album by American flugelhornist Art Farmer's Quintet featuring saxophonist Clifford Jordan featuring performances recorded in 1984 and released on the Soul Note label. 1

Reception

The Allmusic review called the album "Creative bop-based music with Farmer's usual subtlety clearly in evidence". 2

  4. "Flashback" (Art Farmer) - 7:51

  6. "Have You Met Miss Jones?" (Lorenz Hart, Richard Rodgers) - 4:48

Personnel
