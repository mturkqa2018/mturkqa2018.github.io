Ian Sarfas (born 10 June 1970 in London) is a former English professional darts player who currently on British Darts Organisation from the 1990s. 3

Darts Career

Sarfas played in the 1994 BDO World Darts Championship, beating Dave Askew in the first round before losing to Denmark's Troels Rusel in the second round. Sarfas previously reached the quarter-finals of the 1993 British Open. 4
