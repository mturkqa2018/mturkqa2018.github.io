Neil Michael Knight Smith (born 27 July 1967) is a former English cricketer who played in 7 ODIs from 1986 to 1996. He then went on to work at Warwick School for boys, Myton Road, Warwick as the Groundsman but has recently semi retired. He is the son of the former England test captain, M J K Smith. 3
