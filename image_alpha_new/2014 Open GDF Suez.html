The 2014 Open GDF Suez was a women's professional tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 22nd edition of the Open GDF Suez (formerly known as the Open Gaz de France) and a Premier tournament on the 2014 WTA Tour. It took place at Stade Pierre de Coubertin in Paris, France from January 25 through February 2, 2014. 1
