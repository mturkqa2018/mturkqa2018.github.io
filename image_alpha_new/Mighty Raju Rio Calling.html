Mighty Raju: Rio Calling is an Indian animated feature film that released in 16 May 2014 in India. It was the only film based on the characters that released in theaters. The running time of the film is 98 minutes. The film was directed and produced by Rajiv Chilaka. 2

The trailer of the film was released by Kajol and at the same event she was awarded the title "Mighty Mom". 3
