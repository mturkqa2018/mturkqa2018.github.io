Error is the self-titled debut EP by Error, released by Epitaph Records in 2004 and their only recorded work as of 2019. 1

Although not an official member, Greg Puciato of the Dillinger Escape Plan was asked to record vocals for the EP, as the band was without a vocalist at the time. 2
