Anna-Lena Friedsam (born 1 February 1994) is a German tennis player. 2

She has won one singles title on the WTA 125K series as well as 12 singles and three doubles titles on the ITF circuit in her career. Friedsam played also in one final at the WTA Tour and in the 4th round of one Grand Slam. In 2016 she reached her best singles ranking of number 46 and peaked at world number 90 in the doubles. Friedsam won her first ITF tournament at the Infond Open. 3
