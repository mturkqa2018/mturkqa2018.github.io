This is the solo and group discography of the legendary underground hip hop collective Monsta Island Czars. Included are the solo studio albums, EPs and compilations of the various group members and affiliates. Although Monsta Island Czars aren't formed until after the death of Subroc, KMD's albums Mr. Hood and Black Bastards are stylistic precursors to what will appear in the Monsta Island Czars catalog. 1
