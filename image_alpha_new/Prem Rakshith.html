Prem Rakshith is an Indian choreographer who has won four Filmfare Awards for Best Choreography. In 2006 RB Choudry hired Rakshith for a film. In 2007 SS Rajamouli hired him to choreograph the film Yamadonga.Yamadonga was a blockbuster hit. Rakshith choreographed two songs in it. As of July 2015, Rakshith was engaged in various Telugu and Tamil movie projects. 2
