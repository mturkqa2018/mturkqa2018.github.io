Dig Deep is the fifth studio album by American progressive metal band After the Burial. The album was released on February 19, 2016 through Sumerian Records and is the band's first release since the death of guitarist Justin Lowe. It is also the final album by the band to feature founding bassist Lee Foral. 1

Release

The release of Dig Deep was announced on January 4, 2016, when the cover art and track listing was unveiled. The first single "Lost in the Static" was released on October 29, 2015. "Collapse" was the second song released off Dig Deep, it premiered on February 4, 2016. Two days before the official album release a full album stream was made available via Metal Injection. 2
