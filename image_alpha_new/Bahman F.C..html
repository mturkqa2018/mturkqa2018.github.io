In the early years, the bulk of players were from Pas Tehran F.C. winning squads of 1991 and 1992. 1

History

Bahman was founded in 1994 and operated until the end of the Azadegan League 1999/2000 season, after which their shares were bought by now Iran Pro League side Paykan. 2
