Promenades Cathédrale is a 135,495-square-foot retail complex on Saint Catherine Street in downtown Montreal, Quebec, Canada. 1

The complex is located beneath Montreal's Anglican Christ Church Cathedral. Constructed in 1987, the mall is integrated into the underground city. 2

The complex is connected to Henry Morgan Building across the street along Avenue Union and home to Hudson's Bay Company Montreal store. 3
