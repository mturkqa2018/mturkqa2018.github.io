"If Her Lovin' Don't Kill Me" is a song first recorded by American country music artist Aaron Tippin. It was released in August 2002 as the second single from the album Stars & Stripes. The song reached #40 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart. The song was written by John Rich, Vicky McGehee, and Tim Womack, then a member of Sons of the Desert. 1
