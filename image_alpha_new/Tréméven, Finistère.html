Tréméven (Breton: Tremeven-Kemperle) is a commune in the Finistère department of Brittany in north-western France. 1

Breton language

The municipality launched a linguistic plan through Ya d'ar brezhoneg on March 8, 2005. 3

In 2008, 24.11% of primary-school children attended bilingual schools. 4

International relations

Treméven is twinned with the village of Monivea in Ireland. 5
