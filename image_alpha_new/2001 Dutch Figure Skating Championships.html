In the 2001 Dutch Figure Skating Championships of the 2000-2001 figure skating season, skaters competed in the disciplines of men's singles, ladies singles, and ice dance for the title of national champion of the Netherlands. 1

The competition took place between 4 January and 6 January, 2001 in Eindhoven. 2
