History

Tokida Station opened on January 24, 1963 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR) SangÅ« Line. The station was absorbed into the JR Central network upon the privatization of the JNR on April 1, 1987. 2

Station layout

Tokida Station consists of one side platform serving bi-directional traffic. There is no station building, and the station is unattended. 3
