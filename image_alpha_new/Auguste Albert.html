Auguste Albert was a French sailor who competed in the 1900 Summer Olympics. 1

He was a crew member of the French boat Martha 1, which won a silver and a bronze medal in the races of the 1 to 2 ton class. He also participated in the Open class with the boat Martha 27, but did not finish the race. 2
