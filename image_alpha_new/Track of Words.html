Track of Words is a 1999 solo album by singer-songwriter Steve Knightley. 1

A lost album of sorts, which was re-recorded by Knightley and co-producer/engineer Mark Tucker. The re-recorded album was released as Track of Words - Retraced in 2009. Retraced was included in Mike Ganley's Crooked Road "Top 10 Albums 2009" list. 2
