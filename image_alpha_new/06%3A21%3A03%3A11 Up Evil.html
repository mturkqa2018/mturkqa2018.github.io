Album Title

Utilising a simple alphanumeric conversion from numbers to letters, i.e. 1=A, 2=B, 3=C, ..., 26=Z, the title can be interpreted as: 2

  • 06 = F
  • 21 = U
  • 03 = C
  • 11 = K

Which gives a full album title of Fuck Up Evil. 3
