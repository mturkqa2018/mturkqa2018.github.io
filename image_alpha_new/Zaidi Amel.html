Zaidi Amel (born (1995-03-03)3 March 1995) is an Algerian female volleyball player. She is part of the Algeria women's national volleyball team. 1

She participated in the 2015 FIVB Volleyball World Grand Prix. On club level she played for R. C. Bejala in 2014. 2
