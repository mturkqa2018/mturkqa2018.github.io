The 2011 Macedonian Handball Cup was the 19th edition of the Macedonian Handball Cup. It took place at the Sports Hall Mladost in Bitola, Republic of Macedonia, on 14 and 15 May 2011. The cup was won by RK Metalurg for the fourth time. 1
