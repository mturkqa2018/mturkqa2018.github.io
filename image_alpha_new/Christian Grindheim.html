Christian Grindheim (born 17 July 1983) is a Norwegian football midfielder who currently plays for the Tippeligaen club FK Haugesund. Grindheim is known as a hardworking midfielder with tough tackling and powerful shots. He has made over 50 appearances for the Norway national team. 2
