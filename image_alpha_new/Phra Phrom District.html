Geography

Neighboring districts are (from the north clockwise) Mueang Nakhon Si Thammarat, Chaloem Phra Kiat, Ron Phibun, and Lan Saka. 2

History

The district was created as a minor district (king amphoe) on 30 April 1994 by splitting off four tambon from Mueang Nakhon Si Thammarat district. On 11 October 1997 the minor district was upgraded to a full district. 3

Administration

The district is divided into four sub-districts (tambon), which are further subdivided into 38 villages (muban). There are no municipal (thesaban) areas, and four tambon administrative organizations (TAO). 4
