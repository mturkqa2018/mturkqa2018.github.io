Kelvin Amian Adou (born 8 February 1998) is a French footballer who currently plays for Ligue 1 side Toulouse. He plays as a defender. 1

Club career

Amian Adou is a youth exponent from Toulouse. He made his Ligue 1 debut on 14 August 2016 against Marseille playing the full game. 2

Personal life

Amian is of Ivorian descent. 3
