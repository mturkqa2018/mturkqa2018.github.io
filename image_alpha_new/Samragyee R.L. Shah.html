Biography

1995â€“2015: Early life and career

Samragyee RL Shah was born on November 18, 1995, in Kathmandu. Her parents moved to Birgunj. She studied there up to grade 3 and she was sent to study abroad for further studies. She completed her higher secondary education from Royal Academy, India. She joined NIMS college for her Bachelors Degree in Social Work, while also pursued modelling. She was Second runner-up at Face of Classic Diamond Jewellers Competition in 2014. She also appeared in singer Fuba Tamang's music video "Dami". 2

2016-present: Actress Debut

She debuted as lead actress in the film Dreams opposite Anmol K.C. in 2016 which was a commercial hit. Her second film A Mero Hajur 2 which was an enormous success at box office, which helped her gain a foothold in Nepali Film Industry. After a brief role in Mangalam, she then in 2075 BS(2018) starred in Kaira opposite Aaryan Sigdel which was below average grosser but her next film Timi Sanga and Intu Mintu Londonma opposite youtube sensation, Dhiraj Magar(thatdudevlogs) was blockbuster at box office and thus, established herself as leading contemporary actress in Nepalese Cinema. 3

She has then appeared various Nepali music videos and on the cover pages of various magazines like M&S. 4
