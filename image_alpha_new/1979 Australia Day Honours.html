The Australia Day Honours 1979 were announced on 26 January 1979 by the Governor General of Australia, Sir Zelman Cowen. 1

The Australia Day Honours are the first of the two major annual honours lists, announced on Australia Day (26 January), with the other being the Queen's Birthday Honours which are announced on the second Monday in June. 2
