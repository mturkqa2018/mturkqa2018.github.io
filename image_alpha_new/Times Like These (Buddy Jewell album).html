Times Like These is the fourth studio album by American country music singer Buddy Jewell. The album was released on April 26, 2005 by Columbia Records. Two singles were released from the project, "If She Were Any Other Woman" and "So Gone." While the former peaked in the Top 30 of Billboard's Hot Country Songs chart, the latter failed to chart entirely. 1

Sales of Times Like These failed to meet those of Jewell's debut, and he was dropped by Columbia in September 2005. 2
