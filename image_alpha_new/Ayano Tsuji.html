Biography

In 1993, Tsuji attended Kyoto City Dohda Senior High School of Arts where, during an activities class, she started to learn the ukulele after finding her hands were too small to correctly hold and play the guitar. 2

In 1996, Tsuji attended Ryukoku University as a student under the literature department. 3

She released a mini-album entitled Urara from LD & K Records, making this her independent debut as a musician. 4

She graduated as a historian from Ryukoku University's Department of Oriental History. 5
