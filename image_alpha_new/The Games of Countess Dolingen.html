The Games of Countess Dolingen (French: Les Jeux de la comtesse Dolingen de Gratz) is a 1981 French fantasy-drama film written and directed by Catherine Binet and starring Carol Kane. 1

The film was entered into the main competition at the 38th edition of the Venice Film Festival. 2
