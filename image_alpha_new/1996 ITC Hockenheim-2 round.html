The 1996 ITC Hockenheim-2 round was the eleventh round of the 1996 International Touring Car Championship season. It took place on 13 October at the Hockenheimring. 1

Klaus Ludwig won the first race, starting from pole position, and Manuel Reuter gained the second one, both driving a Opel Calibra V6 4x4. 2
