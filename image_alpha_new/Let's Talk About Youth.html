Let's Talk About Youth is a 1976 South Korean dramatic film. 1

Plot

When Sun-a becomes pregnant, she does not know who is the father of her unborn child, and asks Jeong-su to help her care for the baby. She later finds out that Seok-ku is the father and abandons the child. Sun-a and Seok-ku decide to start a new life with the help of Jeong-su. 2
