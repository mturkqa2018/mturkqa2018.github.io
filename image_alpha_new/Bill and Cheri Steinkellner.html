Bill Steinkellner and Cheri Steinkellner are a husband and wife screenwriting duo, known for their work on Cheers, Teacher's Pet, its film adaptation of the same name, and Bob. Bill and Cheri Steinkellner were also originally writers of the canceled Circle 7 Animation version of Toy Story 3, but their script was rejected. 1
