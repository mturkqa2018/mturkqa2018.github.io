Lines

Kita-Yoshida Station is served by the Echigo Line, and is 51.7 kilometers from terminus of the line at Kashiwazaki. 2

Station layout

The station consists of one ground-level side platform serving a single bi-directional track. 3

The station is unattended. Suica cards, Niigata City's travel scheme, can be used at this station. Rail tickets can be purchased from automatic ticket machines. 4

History

The station opened on 8 April 1984 in Yoshida which is now part of the city of Tsubame. With the privatization of Japanese National Railways (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East. 5
