Norazlan bin Razali (born 19 December 1985) is a Malaysian football player who plays for Malaysia Super League side FELDA United as a goalkeeper. 2

Club career

Johor, Kuala Lumpur

Norazlan began his career with home town team Johor before moving to Kuala Lumpur in 2010, making 52 appearances and earning a national team call up. He made three appearances against Iraq, Saudi Arabia and Palestine but none of the match listed as FIFA 'A' international match. 3

Selangor

In 2015, Norazlan returned to Selangor. 4

Felda United

In the 2019 season, Norazlan was transferred to Felda United under the wings of Mohd Nidzam Jamil. 5
