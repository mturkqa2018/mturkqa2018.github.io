Sheho, Saskatchewan is a village in the Canadian province of Saskatchewan. Sheho is located on Saskatchewan Highway 16, the Yellowhead in south east Saskatchewan between Foam Lake and the city of Yorkton. Sheho Lake post office first opened in 1891 at the legal land description of Sec.28, Twp.30, R.9, W2 before moving slightly and changing name to Sheho. Sheho is located east of Foam Lake, Saskatchewan and west of Yorkton, Saskatchewan 1
