Acraea asema, the speckled orange acraea, is a butterfly in the family Nymphalidae. It is found in Angola, Zimbabwe, central and south-eastern Zambia, Malawi, Mozambique and southern Tanzania. The habitat consists of dry deciduous woodland. 2

Adults are on wing year round. 3

The larvae feed on Tricliceras species. 4
