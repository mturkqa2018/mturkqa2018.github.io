Lines

Higashi-Mikkaichi Station is served by the Toyama ChihÅ Railway Main Line, and is 37.8 kilometers from the starting point of the line at Dentetsu-Toyama. 2

Station layout

The station has one side platform serving a single bi-directional track. The station is attended. 3

History

Higashi-Mikkaichi Station was opened on 5 November 1922. 4
