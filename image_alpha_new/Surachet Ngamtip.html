International career

He debuted for U-19 team in 2010 AFC U-19 Championship. Surachet was called up to the national team by Bryan Robson. In 2013, he was called up to the national team by Surachai Jaturapattarapong to the 2015 AFC Asian Cup qualification. In October, 2013 he played a friendly match against Bahrain. In October 15, 2013 he played against Iran in the 2015 AFC Asian Cup qualification. 2
