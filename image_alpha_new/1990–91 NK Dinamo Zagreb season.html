The 1990â€“91 season was Dinamo Zagreb's 45th season in the Yugoslav First League. It proved to be their last season played in the Yugoslav league system. Following the season's completion and due to the breakup of Yugoslavia, Croatian clubs, including Dinamo, decided to leave the league to form Prva HNL. 1

Dinamo Zagreb finished runners-up in the league, with ten points behind champions Red Star Belgrade.
