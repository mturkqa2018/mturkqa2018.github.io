Q Radio is a network of radio stations in Northern Ireland. Originally independent in their own right, after a number of mergers and acquisitions the stations in the network broadcast entirely from their studios 'QHQ' in Belfast's Fountain Centre, with local opt outs for news, traffic and advertising. 2

The network is the second most listened to radio station in Northern Ireland with a combined figure of over 276,000 listeners across Northern Ireland. 3

Network

The stations currently making up the Q Radio Network are: 4

  • Belfast - 96.7 & 102.5 FM & DAB
  • North West - 102.9 FM
  • North Coast - 97.2 & 97.6 FM
  • Mid Antrim - 107.0 & 107.6 FM
  • Mid Ulster - 106.0, 106.3 & 107.2 FM
  • Newry & Mourne - 100.5 FM & 101.1 FM
  • Tyrone & Fermanagh - 101.2 & 102.1 FM

The original Q Network stations were Q102.9, Q97.2 & Q101.2. Five, Six and Seven FM were rebranded on air to the new Q Radio brand as of 2011 and Belfast CityBeat as of 2015. 5
