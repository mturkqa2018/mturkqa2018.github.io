Beşiktaş Women's Football (Turkish: beşiktaş Kadın Futbol Takımı) is the women football section of Beşiktaş JK, a major sports club in Istanbul, Turkey. 1

History

In the 2014–15 season, Beşiktaş women's team was allocated to the bottom league, the Turkish Women's Third Football League. They finished the league as the division leader, and after play-offs, they were promoted to the Women's Second League. 2

In the 2015–16 season, the team played in the Women's Second League, and secured promotion to the First League already four matches before the season's finish. They became league champion two matches prior to the end of the league. The team completed the 2015–16 season undefeated, and were promoted to the Turkish Women's First Football League. 3

The team finished the regular season of 2017–18 even on points with Konak Belediyespor behind the champion Ataşehir Belediyespor. The regular time of the play-off match between the two teams ended with 1–1 draw. In the extension time , Beşiktaş J.K. scored three penalty goals, finished the match by 4–1, and became runners-up. 4

Beşiktaş J.K. finished the 2018–19 Women's First League season as champion defeating Kireçburnu Spor 9–0 in the away match of the league's last round. The high score helped them to take the first place befor ALG Spor, which led the league in two previous round by goal average. It was the team's first champion title in the Women's First League. 5
