Ballyduff Lower GAA is a Gaelic Athletic Association club based in Ballyduff Lower, County Waterford, Ireland. The club enters teams in both the Waterford Intermediate Hurling Championship and the Waterford Intermediate Football Championship. 2

Underage

Ballyduff reformed an underage club in 2008 and fielded teams for the first time since 1992. 3
