Vivaagarathu (English:Divorce) is a 2014 Tamil language drama film from Malaysia. This film were directed by famous THR Raaga radio dj, Revathy Bawadas Kumar (also known as THR Revthy among her fan). The film is apparently based on a number of true stories plucked from the young urban Indian society Malaysia. The storyline revolves around seven married couples who are on the verge of divorce. 1

The film consists of an ensemble cast led by Revathy, Haridass, Aghonderan Sahadevan, Bala Ganapathi William, Nithya Shree, Pearlija Jraj,Tresa Lazaroo,Nookaladevi and Ben G.Vivaagaarathu was released nationwide in 23 local theatres on June 26, 2014 with a mixed review. 2
