Neocoenyra jordani is a butterfly in the family Nymphalidae. It is found in Tanzania. 2

Subspecies

  • Neocoenyra jordani jordani (eastern Tanzania)
  • Neocoenyra jordani septentrionalis Kielland, 1990 (eastern Tanzania)
