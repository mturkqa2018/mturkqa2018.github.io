The 1987 U.S. Clay Court Championships was a Grand Prix tennis tournament held in Indianapolis, Indiana in the United States. It was the 19th edition held in the open era and the last in Indianapolis. 1

The tournament was moved to a summer date (July 13â€“19) after the previous year's spring event failed to attract top male players. The revised date clashed with the scheduled Virginia Slims of Newport so there was no women's event in 1987. 2
