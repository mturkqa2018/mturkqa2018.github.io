Remains of the 16th century friary church.

Greyfriars, Gloucester, England, was a medieval monastic house founded about 1231. 3

In about 1518 a prominent local family, the Berkeleys of Berkeley Castle, paid for the church to be rebuilt in Perpendicular Gothic style. The rest of the friary complex was later demolished. 4
