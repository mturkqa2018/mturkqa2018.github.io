Mini Tsai Huang-ju (born 15 November 1987) is a Taiwanese actress, singer and television host. She made her television debut as a host of the long-running video gaming show GameGX. She has since appeared in several films and television series, including K Song Lover (2013), Crime Scene Investigation Center (2015), Back to 1989 (2016) and The Masked Lover (2017). 1
