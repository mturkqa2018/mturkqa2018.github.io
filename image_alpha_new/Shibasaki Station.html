Lines

Shibasaki Station is served by the Keio Line, and is located 13.3 kilometers from the starting point of the line at Shinjuku Station. 2

Station layout

This station consists of two ground-level opposed side platforms serving two tracks, connected to the station building by an underground passage. 3
