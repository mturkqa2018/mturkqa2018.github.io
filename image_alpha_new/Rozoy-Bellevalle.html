Rozoy-Bellevalle is a commune in the Aisne department in Hauts-de-France in northern France. It is part of the canton of Essômes-sur-Marne and the arrondissement of Château-Thierry. 1

Population and housing

The population of Rozoy-Bellevalle was 100 in 1999, 112 in 2006 and 110 in 2007. The population density of Rozoy-Bellevalle is 16.20 inhabitants per km². The number of housing of Rozoy-Bellevalle was 65 in 2007. These homes of Rozoy-Bellevalle consist of 44 main residences, 16 second or occasional homes and 6 vacant homes. 3
