Brussels Basketball, for sponsorship reasons called Basic-Fit Brussels, is a professional basketball club based in Brussels, Belgium. The team has played in the top tier Pro Basketball League (PBL) since 2013. 1

History

The club was founded in 1957. In 1999 Excelsior merged with AERA Castor and renamed itself AERA Excelsior Brussel VZW. After 7 years in the 3e Nationale, the Belgian third division, Excelsior promoted to the 2e Nationale in 2009. In the first two years, Excelsior finished 4th and 5th and was defeated in the quarterfinals. In the 2011â€“12 season Excelsior reached the semifinals and in the 2012â€“13 season, it finished 10th while missing the Playoffs. 2

The club moved to the professional Scooore League, the first tier in Belgium, for the 2013â€“14 season. Excelsior received a C-licence, which allowed them to play with a lower budget in the league. The club also got a new main sponsor in Basic-Fit, and was renamed Basic-Fit Brussels. 3

During the 2016â€“17 season, Brussels participated in a European competition for the first time by entering the 2016â€“17 FIBA Europe Cup season. Along with this, the club finished in the third place in the Belgian League and reached the Finals for the first time in club history by beating Antwerp Giants in the semi-finals. 4
