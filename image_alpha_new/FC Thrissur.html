FC Thrissur is a professional football club founded by Mr Jaison Muttichukaran and based in Thrissur, Kerala, India. The club began competing in the Kerala Premier League 2017, a professional league organised by Kerala Football Association, and FCT made a remarkable achievement in league as finalist 1

History

FC Thrissur, the Football Club is 3 years old and were champions 2015 C division 2016, A Division and runner up of KPL 2017. Founded by Mr. Jaison Muttichukaran and promoted by a group of enthusiastic football lovers, FCT is planning to launch an international sports culture in Thrissur to promote football It is also planning to develop a facility which will have modern amenities to train and get trained by professional footballers. FCT will promote itself as the "CLUB OF THRISSURIANS". 2
