Regional elections in Italy took place during 2018 in 7 regions out of 20 including Lazio and Lombardy (4 March), Molise (22 April), Friuli-Venezia Giulia (29 April), Aosta Valley (20 May) and Trentino-Alto Adige/Südtirol (21 October). 1
