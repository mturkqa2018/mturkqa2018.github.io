Odd Future Records is an American record label founded by rapper and producer Tyler, the Creator of Odd Future in 2007. It operates as a division of Sony Music Entertainment. 1

History

Odd Future Records was founded by Tyler, The Creator in April 2007 as a means to release material from the Odd Future collective. Despite this, members Earl Sweatshirt and Frank Ocean did not sign. Hardcore punk band Trash Talk signed in 2012. 2
