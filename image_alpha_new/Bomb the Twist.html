Bomb the Twist is a studio album that was released on January 11, 1996 by The 5.6.7.8's. "Woo Hoo" was featured on Kill Bill Volume 1, a film by Quentin Tarantino 1

Track listing

  1. "Bomb the Twist"
  2. "Jane in the Jungle"
  3. "Three Cool Chicks"
  4. "Guitar Date"
  5. "Woo Hoo"
  6. "Dream Boy"

Personnel

  • Ronnie Fujiyama - guitar, vocals, voice
  • Screaming "Omo" Chellio Panther - bass, backing vocals, voice
  • Sachiko "Geisha-Girl" Fujii - drums, backing vocals
