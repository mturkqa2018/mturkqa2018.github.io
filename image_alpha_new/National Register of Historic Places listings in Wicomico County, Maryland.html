Location of Wicomico County in Maryland

This is a list of the National Register of Historic Places listings in Wicomico County, Maryland. 1

This is intended to be a complete list of the properties and districts on the National Register of Historic Places in Wicomico County, Maryland, United States. Latitude and longitude coordinates are provided for many National Register properties and districts; these locations may be seen together in a map. 2

There are 23 properties and districts listed on the National Register in the county. 3
