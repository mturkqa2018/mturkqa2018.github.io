The 2017 World Draughts-64 Championship held in Saint Peterburg, Russia. 64 players from Europe, Asia, Africa, North and South America competed in the tournament, which started on October 20, 2017 and will end on October 28, 2017. Tournament played in hotel Â«MoscowÂ». The winning prize for the tournament was about 52,000 $ (three million rubles). At the same time the Women's World Draughts Championship was held. Championship played in classic (at the Russian draughts), rapid (at the Brazilian draughts) and blitz (at the Russian draughts) formats. 1

Rules and Regulations

In the first stage participants played Swiss-system tournament with 8 rounds. To define the places with equal points used of Buchholz system. The first 16 participated in the final. The final was in the play-off form. For the victory was given - 2 points for a draw â€“ one point, for loss â€“ 0 points. Players from 17 to 64 played additionally Swiss-system tournament with 2 rounds. 2

The final classification is based on the total points obtained. If two or more players will have same total points to define the places: 3

2. Number of points scored by opponents, excluding the lowest result (reduced Solkof coefficient) 5
