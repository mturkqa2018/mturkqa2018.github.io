Daku Rani Ganga is a Gujarati Film released in 1976 which was also dubbed and released as a Bhojpuri film , directed by Marathi film director Datta Keshav. The film was based on a gujrati novel - " Pravah Paltayo" by popular Gujarati novelist Harkisan Mehta and was produced by his son Tushar Mehta. 1
