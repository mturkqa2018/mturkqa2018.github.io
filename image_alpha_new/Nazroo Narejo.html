Early life

Nazroo Narejo studied sociology at the University of Sindh, Jamshoro in the 1970s. He was decent and intellectual student but due to his father's encounter in the shadow of feudal system he turned into Crime World and made a stand against the feudalism. His Father had a master's degree in sociology as well as L.L.B. 2

Death

Nazroo, along with his accomplices was killed in the Garhi Yaseen area of Shaikarpur in an encounter with Tanveer Ahmed Tunio, SSP of the Sukkur Region Sindh Police. His brother-in-law Sarwar, also known as Saroo Narejo and son Rab Rakhio Narejo were also killed during this operation. The operation claimed the lives of policemen Mujeeb Chachar and Haji Ismi Chachar. 3
