Family

Resh Marhatta belongs to family of Parashar Gotri Maratha immigrants into Gorkha Kingdom. His father is Shakti Marhatta, who served as an economist in the Finance Ministry of Nepal and was one of the key bureaucrat while designing and implementing value towards modernizing and reforming revenue administration of Nepal and his mother is Bina Marhatta, a housewife. Resh is the youngest of six siblings. He went to MAV Boarding School in early days. Resh got engaged with Ashmii Adhikari in 2015 who is a graduate from Manipal University. 4

Resh graduated from Weber State University. 5
