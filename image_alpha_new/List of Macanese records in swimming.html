List of Macanese records in swimming

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Macau records in swimming are the fastest ever performances of swimmers from Macao, which are recognised and ratified by the Associação de Natação de Macau. 1

All records were set in finals unless noted otherwise. 2
