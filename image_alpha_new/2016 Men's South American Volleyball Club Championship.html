The 2016 Men's South American Volleyball Club Championship was the eighth official edition of the men's volleyball tournament, played by eight teams from 17 to 21 February 2016 in Taubate, Brazil. 1

Sada Cruzeiro defeated the local team Funvic TaubatÃ© in the final match, and qualified for the 2016 Club World Championship. 2
