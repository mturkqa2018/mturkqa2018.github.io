1999 Runnymede Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Elections to Runnymede Council were held on 6 May 1999. One third of the council was up for election and the Conservative party stayed in overall control of the council. 1

After the election, the composition of the council was 2

  • Conservative 25
  • Labour 9
  • Independent 6
  • Liberal Democrat 2
