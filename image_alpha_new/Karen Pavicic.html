Karen Pavicic (born 29 April 1971 in Vancouver, B.C.) is a Canadian-born Croatian dressage rider. She represented Canada at the 2014 World Equestrian Games, where she placed 9th in the team competition and 41st in the individual dressage competition onboard Don Daiquiri. 1

Pavicic won a silver medal in team dressage at the 2007 Pan American Games in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. 2

In 2018, Pavicic switched her sports allegiance to Croatia after obtaining dual citizenship. 3
