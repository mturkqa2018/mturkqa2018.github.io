Electoral history of David Cameron

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This is a summary of the electoral history of David Cameron, who served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 2010 to 2016 and Leader of the Conservative Party from 2005 to 2016. He was the Member of Parliament (MP) for Witney from 2001 to 2016. 2
