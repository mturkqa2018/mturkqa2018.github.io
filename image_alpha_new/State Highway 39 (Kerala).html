State Highway 39 (SH 39) is a State Highway in Kerala, India that starts in Perumbilavu and ends in Nilambur. The highway is 107.112 km long. 3

Route

Perumbilavu – Koottanad - Pattambi – Perinthalmanna - Pattikkadu–Karuvarakundu – Kalikavu – Nilambur Road 4
