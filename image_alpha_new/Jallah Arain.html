Jallah Arain is a town city of Lodhran District in the South side of the Punjab province of Pakistan. It is located 3 kilometers away on the side of Multan-Bahawalpur main road. This town has a fertile land with beautiful villages around it. This area is famous for the ARAIN family with some native castes. 1

Shops and Markets

There are several shops and markets, including a cloth house, several electronic shops, a medical store, and restaurant as well as several other businesses. A private hospital run by a Docter Wahid, Docter Saeed and Docter Sajjid is located in the town. 2

Infrastructure

There is a road starting at the Bus stop Jallah Moor on the Multan-Bahawalpur road, which leads to Jallah Arain. 3

Education

There are two government schools in the region: 4

  1. Govt. Girls Higher Secondary School, Jallah Arain
  2. Govt. High School (for Boys), Jallah Arain. Govt. Boys High School is now upgraded to the Govt. Higher Secondary School (Boys) Jallah Arain.

Other than the government schools, there are few private schools and tuition institutes as well in the area, providing mostly the primary to Matric level education only. 5

Health

There is one government hospital in the area that provides the health services. However, it only fulfills the basic health needs of people and there is lack of proper equipment and treatment facilities. There are also a couple of doctors doing the private practice. People have to go to nearby district hospital in Lodhran or some other city if they need proper medical treatment. 6

MEPCO

MEPCO Sub Division office is also established in Jallah Arain Which is situated near the Union Council office Jallah Arain. 7

Water Supply

Water Supply scheme is operated by its users committee. Under ground water of Jallah Arain is brackish(Karwa). Water Supply Scheme comprise "4" Turbines which are fixed near the Mahmood Canal that provide sweet water in the city area of Jallah Arain. A project of Filtration Plant of water was started in the premises of Basic Health Unit in Jallah Arain but that was not completed only structure was constructed which is not in good condition at this time. 8

Graveyard

Two main graveyards are situated in city Jallah Arain and one in Basti Gareeb Abad(5 Marla scheme). Only some portion of one Graveyard namely Western side was provided Boundary wall but major portion of graveyard is out of Boundary wall. Other North side graveyard which is famous for the Buzarg Peer Budhan Lal is totally out of Boundary wall which is situated in the populated area. The area of graveyard is encroached due to non availability of Boundary wall. 9

=SOHAN HALWA,SAMOSA & JALEBI

If you vist Jallah Arain, Dont't forget to taste Abuzar's Sohan Halwa at Jallah Morre. One of the famous soghaat of this Town village. An other thing that is very famous in Jallah Arain is samosa(yummmi). Two smosaa Shops at Ghani Choke are well known that are Altaf and Asghar Samosy Wala. There is rush all the day because of the college's student and villagers as well 10

PEOPLE

People from Jallah Arain are very sweet,loving caring and hospitable. Mian Shafique and Mian Rafique Arain are well known personalities of this town. They are really great people. Kabaddi,kushti and volley ball are liked by people 11

5 Marla Scheme

5 Marla scheme is also situated in the east side of Jallah Arain namely Basti Gareb Abad. . 12
