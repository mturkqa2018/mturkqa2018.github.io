Brian Wayne Koelling (born June 11, 1969 in Cincinnati, Ohio) is a former Major League Baseball player who played 7 games for the Cincinnati Reds in 1993. He was drafted by the Reds in the 14th round of the 1991 amateur draft. His ML debut came on August 21, 1993 against the Montreal Expos. 1
