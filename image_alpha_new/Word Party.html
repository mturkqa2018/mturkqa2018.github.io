Word Party (also known as Jim Henson's Word Party) is an American animated children's television series that began airing on Netflix, Playhouse Disney, and Nick Jr in 2016. The series was created by The Jim Henson Company, using Henson Digital Puppetry Studio. The series premiered on July 8, 2016. Season 2 premiered on October 21, 2016. In season 2 of Word Party Episodes, big kids help the babies call Word Wally to help the babies learn the word as fastest as Word Wally can. with Season 3. 1

On January 30, 2019, it was announced that it was renewed for a fourth season by Netflix. The Jim Henson Company has an official press release confirming the renewal and addition of season four, which will introduce a new character named Tilly the Tortoise. 2

Overview

The vocabulary-building program features four young animals, Kip the Wallaby (John Tartaglia), Bailey the Elephant (Victor Yerrid), Franny the Cheetah (Donna Kimball), and Lulu the Panda (Dorien Davies), "who love to sing, dance and play." 3
