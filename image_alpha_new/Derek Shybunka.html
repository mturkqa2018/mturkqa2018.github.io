Derek Shybunka (born September 25, 1970) is a Canadian former professional ice hockey and inline hockey goaltender. 2

Shybunka played with the Canadian men's national team at the 2008 IIHF Men's InLine Hockey World Championship where he selected as the tournament's best goaltender. 3
