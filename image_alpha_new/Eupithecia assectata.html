Eupithecia assectata is a moth in the family Geometridae. It is found in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Kyrgyzstan, Afghanistan and Kashmir. It is found at altitudes between 2,350 and 3,750 meters. 2

It is a very variable species. The forewings vary from pale grey without a pattern to dark grey with very distinct blackish transverse lines on the forewings. 3
