"Sex, Love & Money" is a single by rapper Mos Def, issued as the lead single from his second album, The New Danger. The video was directed by Paul Hunter. It was nominated for a Grammy Award, and reached Billboard's the Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart. 1
