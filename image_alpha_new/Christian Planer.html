Christian Planer (born 15 May 1975) is an Austrian sport shooter. He competed at the 2004, 2008 and 2012 Olympics in two individual events, 10 m air rifle and 50 m small-bore rifle, and won a bronze medal in the small-bore rifle in 2004. 2
