Le Républicain Lorrain (founded in 1919) is a daily regional French newspaper based in Metz. As of 2012, its daily circulation was 123,357. 1

The newspaper has its primary market in the région of Lorraine. 2

It belongs to the French bank Crédit Mutuel, who also owns the newspapers L'Alsace and Le Pays. 3
