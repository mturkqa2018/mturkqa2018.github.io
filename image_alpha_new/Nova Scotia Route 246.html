NS Route 246 in Tatamagouche, Nova Scotia

Route 246 is a collector road in the Canadian province of Nova Scotia. 2

It is located in the northern part of the province and connects Tatamagouche at Trunk 6 with Wentworth, Nova Scotia at Trunk 4. 3
