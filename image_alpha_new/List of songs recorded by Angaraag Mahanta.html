Angaraag Mahanta in 2014

Angaraag "Papon" Mahanta is an Indian singer and composer. He lent his voice in numerous songs of different languages including Hindi, Assamese, Bengali, Tamil, Kannada, and Marathi. This article lists songs recorded by Angaraag Mahanta. 1
