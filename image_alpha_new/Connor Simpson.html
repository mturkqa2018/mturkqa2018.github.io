Connor Mark Simpson (born 24 January 2000) is an English professional footballer who plays as a forward for League Two side Carlisle United on loan from Preston North End. 1

Playing career

Simpson made his senior debut for Hartlepool as a substitute for Lewis Hawkins in a 1â€“0 defeat to Cheltenham Town on 29 April 2017. The goal meant that he was the first player born in the 21st century to have scored for Hartlepool United. 2

Simpson left Hartlepool to join Preston North End in January 2018 for a fee of Â£50,000. Simpson made his Preston debut in January 2018 coming on as a late substitute against Aston Villa. 3

Simpson joined Northern Premier League club Hyde United on a short-term loan deal in October 2018 and scored in his second appearance for the club against South Shields. After scoring five goals in nine games for Hyde, Simpson's loan deal was extended until January 2019. 4
