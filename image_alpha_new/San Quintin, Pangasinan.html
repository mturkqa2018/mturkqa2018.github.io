San Quintin, officially the Municipality of San Quintin, (Pangasinan: Baley na San Quintin; Ilokano: Ili ti San Quintin; Tagalog: Bayan ng San Quintin), is a 3rd class municipality in the province of Pangasinan, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 32,945 people. 1
