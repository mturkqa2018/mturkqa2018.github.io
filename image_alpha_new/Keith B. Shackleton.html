Keith B. Shackleton (born 1929) served as Head of Merchandising for AP Films (later Century 21). 1

Shackleton met Gerry Anderson while they both served as a non-commissioned officers in the Royal Air Force during 1948. Twelve years later, he was persuaded by Anderson to join AP Films. Shackleton became the managing director of A.P. Films, Merchandising, Ltd. 2
