Juan Aceres (died 1549) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Ciudad Rodrigo (1546–1549). 1

Biography

Juan Aceres was born in Spain. On 8 January 1546, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Paul III as Bishop of Ciudad Rodrigo. He served as Bishop of Ciudad Rodrigo until his death on 31 July 1549. 2
