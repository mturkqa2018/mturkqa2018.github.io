Highway 395 is a short cross-border spur in the Regional District of Kootenay Boundary in British Columbia. It connects with U.S. Route 395 (from which it derives its number) at the Canada-U.S. border crossing near Laurier, WA to the Crowsnest Highway (Highway 3) near Cascade, about 20Â km (12Â mi) east of Grand Forks. 2
