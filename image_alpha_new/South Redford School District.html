The South Redford School District is in Redford, a suburb of Detroit. In May 2005, residents of the South Redford School District passed the 2005 Bond Facilities Proposal. The $32.65 million bond allowed the District to make substantial up grades, repairs and improvements to the facilities across the entire school district. 1

Pierce Middle School was built in 1959. 2

Mission Statement: Staff, students, parents and the community working together to ensure continuous learning and success. 3
