Dhall Kakka is a medium size village in the tehsil of Kharian, Pakistan. 1

People of many tribes and clans live in the village mostly Muslim Jat and Muhajir. More than 60 percent of people depend on money which comes from working abroad in Europe and America. Dhall Kakka is known for its Bull racing, Wrestling (kabaddi), Football, Cricket, Islamic education and female education. 2
