Crew Dragon Demo-2, officially known as SpaceX Demo-2 and Crew Demo-2, will be the first crewed test flight of SpaceX's Crew Dragon spacecraft. It is scheduled for launch in July 2019 with a crew of two on a 14-day test mission to the International Space Station (ISS). If the schedule holds, it will be the first crewed flight of an American spacecraft into orbit since STS-135 in July 2011. 1
