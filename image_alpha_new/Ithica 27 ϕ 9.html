"Ithica 27 Ï• 9" is a song by Scottish post-rock band Mogwai. 1

"Ithica 27 Ï• 9" was originally released as a double A-side single with "Summer" on 4 November 1996 through Love Train. It was later included on the 1997 compilation album, Ten Rapid (Collected Recordings 1996-1997). 2

Release and reception
