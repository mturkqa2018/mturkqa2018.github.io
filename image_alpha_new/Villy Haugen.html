Villy Haugen (born 27 September 1944) is a former speed skater from Norway. 1

Haugen earned a bronze medal in 1500 m. at the 1964 Winter Olympics in Innsbruck. He came on eighth place on 500 m at the same Olympics. Haugen represented SK Falken in Trondheim. 2
