Alejandro Saravia is a Peruvian chef who was born in Lima, Peru on June 13, 1983, and has lived in Sydney, Australia since 2006. He is the co-owner and Head Chef of South American fine dining restaurant, Morena [1] in Surry Hills, Sydney. 1

Saravia studied marketing at university and worked as a brand manager before following his passion for food to train as a chef.
