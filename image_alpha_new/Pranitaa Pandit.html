Pranitaa Pandit is an Indian actress and film producer who has appeared and starred in television shows such as Uttaran, Kuch Is Tarah, Jamai Raja and Kasam. 2

Early life and career

Pranitaa started with her career working as an actress in the Star Plus serial called Kasturi and later got a slew of other Television shows. These included Kuch Is Tarah, Kasautii Zindagi Kay, Kitani Mohabbat Hai, Uttaran and Kaali, Laagi Tujhse Lagan. In 2016 she left the Colors TV show Uttaran and then started her own venture in the form of Parikatha Entertainment, which dealt with making corporate ads, films, 3D animation and VFX films. Pranitaa had turned an entrepreneur in 2014 and had to give up on her acting career for about 2 to 3 years. 3
