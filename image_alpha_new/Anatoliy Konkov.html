Career

During his playing career Konkov played in defense as a stopper. He won four Soviet championships, once was a holder of the Soviet Cup, UEFA Supercup, and UEFA Cup Winners' Cup 1974-75. He is a vice-champion of Europe'72, and received an Olympic bronze medal in 1976. 2

In 1979 Konkov played couple of games for Ukraine at the Spartakiad of the Peoples of the USSR. 3

After retiring as a player Konkov became a coach, coaching clubs of the Soviet Union, Ukraine, and Azerbaijan. On 2 September 2012 he was elected the President of the Football Federation of Ukraine, becoming the second president after Viktor Bannikov who had a professional player career. 4
