FC Reading Revolution was an American soccer team based in Reading, Pennsylvania, United States. Founded in 2009, the team played in the National Premier Soccer League (NPSL), a national amateur league at the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid, in the Northeast Keystone Division. 1

The team played its home games at Shirk Stadium on the campus of Albright College. The team's colors were red, black and silver. 2
