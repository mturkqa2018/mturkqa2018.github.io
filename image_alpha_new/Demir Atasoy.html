Demir Atasoy (also Dmitry Cherkasov, born May 26, 1987 in Dnipropetrovsk, Ukrainian SSR) is a Ukrainian-born Turkish swimmer, who specialized in the breaststroke events. 1

Atasoy qualified for the men's 100 m breaststroke at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing, by clearing a FINA B-standard time of 1:02.43 from the Croatian Open Championships in Dubrovnik. He challenged seven other swimmers on the fourth heat, including three-time Olympian Jakob JÃ³hann Sveinsson of Iceland. Atasoy failed to advance into the semifinals, as he placed thirty-ninth overall on the first night of preliminaries. 2

At the 2013 Mediterranean Games held in Mersin, Turkey, he won the bronze medal in the men's 4 Ã— 100 m medley relay. 3

He won four gold medals and one silver medal at the 2013 Islamic Solidarity Games held in Palembang, Indonesia. 4
