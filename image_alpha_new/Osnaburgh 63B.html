Osnaburgh 63B is a First Nations reserve in Kenora District, Ontario. It is one of the reserves of the Mishkeegogamang First Nation, alongside Osnaburgh 63A. 1

Osnaburgh 63B is made up of five separate areas known as Dog Hole Bay, Sandy Road, Bottle Hill, Trailer Park, Main Reserve.. 2
