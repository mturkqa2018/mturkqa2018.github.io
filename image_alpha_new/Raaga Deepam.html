Raaga Deepam (English: Musical Light) is a 1982 Telugu drama film, produced by Kodali Bosu Babu under the Veera Rani Enterprises banner and directed by Dasari Narayana Rao. It stars Akkineni Nageswara Rao, Jayasudha in the lead roles and music composed by Chakravarthy. 2
