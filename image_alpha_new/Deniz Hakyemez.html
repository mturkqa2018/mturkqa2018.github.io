Deniz Hakyemez Çetin Saraç (born 3 February 1983) is a Turkish volleyball player. She is 1.88 m (6 ft 2 in) tall and plays as an outside hitter. She studied at Marmara University. 1

Hakyemez plays for Galatasaray Medical Park. She signed 1-year contract with the team on July 2009. 2
