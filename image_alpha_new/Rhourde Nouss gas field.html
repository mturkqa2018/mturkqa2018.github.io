The Rhourde Nouss gas field is a natural gas field located in the Ouargla Province. It was discovered in 1980 and developed by Sonatrach. It began production in 1980 and produces natural gas and condensates. The total proven reserves of the Rhourde Nouss gas field are around 13 trillion cubic feet (370Ã—10mÂ³), and production is slated to be around 1.69 billion cubic feet/day (48.3Ã—10mÂ³). 1
