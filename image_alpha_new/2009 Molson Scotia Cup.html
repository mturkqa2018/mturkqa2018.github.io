The 2009 Molson Scotia Cup (Nova Scotia's men's provincial curling championship) was held February 10-15 at the Chester Curling Club in Chester, Nova Scotia. The winning team represented Nova Scotia at the 2009 Tim Hortons Brier in Calgary. 1
