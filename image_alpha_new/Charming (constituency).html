The constituency returns one district councillor to the Yau Tsim Mong District Council, with an election every four years. The seat is currently held by Chung Kong-mo of the DAB. 3

Provident constituency is loosely based on the Charming Garden and southwestern part of Mongkok with estimated population of 18,029. 4
