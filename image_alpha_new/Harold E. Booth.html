Harold E. Booth (October 30, 1911â€“1971) was a Democratic member of the California State Assembly from the 4th district from 1963 to 1965. 1

Early life

Booth was born in 1911 in Soldier, Kansas. He lived in California for most of his life. 2

Political career

Booth won the 4th State Assembly district election in 1962, narrowly beating Republican candidate Ray E. Johnson. He lost to Johnson two years later, in 1964. 3
