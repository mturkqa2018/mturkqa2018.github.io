Nelly Tchayem (born 4 August 1983 at Lille) is a French athlete, who specializes in the triple jump. 1

Biography

She won two French National Championships titles in the triple jump: one in outdoor in 2010 and one indoors in 2003. 2

Prize list

  • French Championships in Athletics:
    • winner of the triple jump 2010
  • French Indoor Athletics Championships:
    • winner of the triple jump 2003
