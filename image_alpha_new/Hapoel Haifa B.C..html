Hapoel Haifa Basketball Club is a professional basketball team based in Haifa, Israel. the club is owned by Hapoel Haifa Supporters Trust. 2009-10 was their last season as an active club when they played in Liga Leumit and finished fourth. 3

The club had a rich history with being a part of the Israeli Basketball Super League for many years and finishing as runners up to Maccabi Tel Aviv in 1962 and 1965. 4

In the 2011/12 season, the fans formed the club again. The team currently plays in the Liga Leumit; the second league in israel. 5
