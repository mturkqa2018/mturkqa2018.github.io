Season summary

Swansea had a good season in League One, finishing in sixth place to take the last play-off spot. After beating Brentford 3-1 on aggregate in the semi-final, the Swans faced Barnsley in the final. After a 2-2 draw at the end of extra time, Barnsley won the penalty shoot-out 4-3. This form did not translate to the cup competitions, being knocked out in the first round of both. 2
