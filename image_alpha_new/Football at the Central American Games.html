The Central American Games football tournament is a football competition organised by UNCAF and ORDECA. It is scheduled to be held every four years. 1

The men's tournament is only open to players who are aged 21 and below. 2

In 2001 a women's tournament was played for the first time. The women's event is contested by the senior national squads. 3
