Neetu David was a left-arm spinner who played for the India national women's cricket team. 2

David is India's third highest wicket taker in Tests and their leading wicket-taker in One Day Internationals. She also has the best innings bowling analysis in women's Test matches, having taken 8 for 53 versus England Women in 1995. 3

David retired from international cricket in 2006, but reversed that decision in 2008. 4
