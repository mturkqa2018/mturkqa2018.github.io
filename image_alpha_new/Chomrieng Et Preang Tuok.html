Jomrieng Et Preang Tuk (Songs to Remember) is a 1974 Khmer musical film directed and produced by the King of Khmer Music, Sinn Si Samouth, himself. The film star Kong Som Eun and Vichara Dany.It also features singer Ros Serey Sothear. 1
