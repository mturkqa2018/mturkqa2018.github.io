Artyom Savatyugin (born 9 July 1994) is a Uzbekistani male badminton player. In 2014, he reach the semi-finals round in the men's and mixed doubles events at the Bahrain International tournament. In 2015, he reach the final round in the mixed doubles event at the same tournament and became the runner-up with his partner Alesia Zaitsava of Belarus. 1
