Lost River 1 Airport (FAA LID: LSR) is an airport located at Lost River, in the Nome Census Area of the U.S. state of Alaska. 1

Facilities and aircraft

Lost River 1 Airport has one runway designated 18/36 with a gravel surface measuring 3,650 by 100 feet (1,113 by 30 metres). For the 12-month period ending August 16, 1983, the airport had 500 aircraft operations, an average of 41 per month: 80% air taxi and 20% general aviation. 2
