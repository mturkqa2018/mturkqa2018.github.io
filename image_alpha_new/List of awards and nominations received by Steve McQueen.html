The following is a list of awards and nominations received by British film director, producer, screenwriter, and video artist Steve McQueen. For his work on the 2008 film Hunger, he won a BAFTA Award for Outstanding Debut by a British Writer, Director or Producer, and he won an Academy Award, BAFTA Award and a Golden Globe for Best Film for his work on the 2013 film 12 Years a Slave. 2
