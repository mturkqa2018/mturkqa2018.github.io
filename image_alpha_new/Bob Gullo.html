Robert Gullo (born March 16, 1965) is the president and founder of Electronics Design Group (EDG) a private company that specializes in the design, integration, and installation of home theaters and home automation systems for private residences and businesses. 1

Gullo, an alumnus of Kean University is President of the Home Technology Specialists of America (HTSA), a buying group for custom installers of which EDG is a member. He has been a board member since 2006. 2
