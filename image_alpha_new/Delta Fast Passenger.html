Delta Express is an Express Train in India which runs between Kacheguda of Telangana and Repalle of Andhra Pradesh. The South Central Railway Zone of the Indian Railways administers this train. Train Number 17625 runs from Kacheguda to Repalle while 17626 runs from Repalle to Secunderabad Junction. 2

Initially this was ran as Fast passenger service, from 19 October 2017 upgraded to Express. 3

Features

The Delta Express is usually hauled by a WDM2/WDM3A. The 18 coach composition contains – 5 Sleeper Class, 1 3-tier AC, 11 Unreserved and 2 SLR. 4
