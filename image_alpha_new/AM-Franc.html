The "flag ticket" franc (French: Billet drapeau) was a currency issued by the United States for use in Allied-occupied France in the wake of the Battle of Normandy. With the swift take-over of sovereignty by General Charles de Gaulle, who considered the US occupation franc as â€œcounterfeit moneyâ€, the currency rapidly faded out of use in favour of the pre-war French franc.
