Santosh Mishra (born 24 August 1978) is a Bhojpuri film director, writer and music composer, known for his works in Hindi, Bhojpuri and Gujarati films. He is known for directing the Bhojpuri movies Patna Se Pakistan, Bam Bam Bol Raha Hai Kashi, Mokama 0Â km,Border, Nirahua Rickshawala 2 and more than 40 other films. 2
