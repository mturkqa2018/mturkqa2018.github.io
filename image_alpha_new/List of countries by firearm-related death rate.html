This is a historical list of countries by firearm-related death rate per 100,000 population in one year. 1

The following list includes justifiable homicides, suicides, and accidental fatalities. 2

Not included are homicides by other means, suicides by other means, accidental deaths by other means, and/or justifiable deaths by other means. 3
