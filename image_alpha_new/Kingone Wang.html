Early life

On 5 May 1980, Wang was born in Taipei. He is the only child in his family. His given name "ä¼ ä¸€" means "the one to inherit". 2

His father is a senior doctor in dermatological department. His mother died of cancer when he was only 17. 3

During his years of Affiliated Senior High School of National Taiwan Normal University, his mark was always very first-class. So the school recommended him for admission to National Chiao Tung University. 4

Kingone is able to speak English, Mandarin and Taiwanese Hokkien. He is currently completing Graduate degrees in both Civil Engineering at National Chiao Tung University and Philosophy at Chinese Culture University. 5

In his spare time, Wang likes music and sport, especially golf and darts. 6
