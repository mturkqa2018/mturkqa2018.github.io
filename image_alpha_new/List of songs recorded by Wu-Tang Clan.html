Wu-Tang Clan is an American hip hop group from Staten Island, New York City originally composed of East Coast rappers RZA, Method Man, Inspectah Deck, Ghostface Killah, GZA, Ol' Dirty Bastard, Masta Killa, Raekwon, and U-God. Longtime collaborator Cappadonna became an official member in 2007. 1

Below is a list of songs recorded by the group. 2
