The 1956 United States presidential election in Michigan was held on November 6, 1956. Michigan voters chose twenty electors to the Electoral College, who voted for president and vice president. 7

Republican Party candidate Dwight D. Eisenhower won the state with 56% of the popular vote, winning Michigan's twenty electoral votes. 8
