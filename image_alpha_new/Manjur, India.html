Manjur is a village in Bogalur block, Paramakudi Taluk, Ramanathapuram district, Tamil Nadu. There is a District Institute of Teacher Education. The village has a veterinary hospital, a primary health center (PHC) and a library. The village is situated on NH 49, Madurai Mandapam Highway. 2

This village is very popular for Lord Shiva temple which is recently renovated and the god in this temple is very powerful. This village is now developed lot and it has got all the facilities. Agriculture plays an important role in Manjur. Every year, the Rice, Chilli and Cotton are cultivated by the farmers. This place is also famous for the bricks and the products manufactured from Manjur are getting distributed all over south. Almost seven to eight brick industries are there. 3

Other attractions of this place are, well connected roads to NH-49, Primary & Higher Secondary school, District Teacher Training School, Veterinary hospital, Primary Health Center, Library with all new books, Canara Bank, Post Office, Panchayat Office, VAO Guest House, good water facilities, well maintained pond, Ayyanar Temple, Karuppannaswamy Temple, rice Mills and multiple hotels and shops. 4
