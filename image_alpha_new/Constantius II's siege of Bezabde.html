Constantius II besieged the fortress city of Bezabde in Zabdicene in 360, held by the Sasanians. The Sasanians successfully defended the fortress city against the Roman attack. 2

The Romans had lost Bezabde earlier that year to the Sasanians following a siege led by Shapur II. 3
