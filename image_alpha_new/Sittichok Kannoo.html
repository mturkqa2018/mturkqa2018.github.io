Personal life

Sittichok's older brother Attapon is also a footballer who plays for Army United. 2

Club career

Kannoo scored his first ever goal for Buriram United in the 75th minute with a great effort to equalize against Nakhon Ratchasima in an eventual 1-1 draw. 3

International career

He won the 2011 AFF U-16 Youth Championship with Thailand U17. In August 2017, He won the Football at the 2017 Southeast Asian Games with Thailand U23. 4
