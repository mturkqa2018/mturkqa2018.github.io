SandÃ© first became known to the public eye after she featured on rapper Chipmunk's debut single, "Diamond Rings", which gained herself and Chipmunk a top 10 single on the UK Singles Chart. In 2010, she appeared on Wiley's "Never Be Your Woman", which became another top ten hit. She has written for a number of artists, including Cher Lloyd, Susan Boyle, Preeya Kalidas, Leona Lewis, Cheryl Cole, and Tinie Tempah. 3

It was succeeded by the release of the singer's debut studio album, Our Version of Events, which debuted at number-one in the United Kingdom â€“ selling 113,319 copies in its first week.
