On 16 August 1950, another division reform was implemented. The southern part of the County was separated and established Pingtung County. The remaining Kaohsiung County has territory equivalent to the HÅzan (Fengshan), Okayama (Kangshan), and Kizan (Chishan) in the Japanese era. In addition, districts in the remaining part of Kaohsiung County was defunct. All townships were directly controlled by the County Government. On 25 December 2010, the county merged with Kaohsiung City to form a larger single special municipality. 4
