Carpatolechia aenigma is a moth of the family Gelechiidae. It is found in France, Germany, the Netherlands, Italy, Austria, the Czech Republic, Slovakia, Poland, Hungary, Romania, Greece, Ukraine and Russia (the Ural Mountains and Lower Volga). 2

The wingspan is 10â€“13Â mm. Adults have been recorded on wing from May to July. 3

The larvae have been recorded feeding on Tilia cordata and Quercus robur. 4
