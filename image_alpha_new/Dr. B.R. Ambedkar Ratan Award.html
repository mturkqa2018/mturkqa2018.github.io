The Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Ratan Award (also called as the Ambedkar Ratan Award) is a award presented by the Government of Delhi (India) in honour of B. R. Ambedkar, the country's first law minister, father of the Indian Constitution and champion of human rights. 1

History

The Dr. B.R. Ambedkar Ratan award is given for: 2

  • outstanding contribution to the promotion of social understanding, socio-economic or educational upliftment of the Scheduled Castes in Delhi
  • efforts towards Integration of the Scheduled Castes into the mainstream of

society and work done for their socio-economic and educational upliftment 3

  • A public work or a mass movement which has made considerable positive impact on

the quality of life of the Scheduled Castes. 4
