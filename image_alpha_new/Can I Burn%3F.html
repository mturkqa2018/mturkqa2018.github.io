Can I Burn? is the fourth album released by the rapper Fiend. It was released on September 19, 2000 for Fiend's own label, Fiend Entertainment and featured production from Fiend and David Banner. After two successful albums for No Limit Records, Can I Burn? was his first independent release since 1995 and found mild chart success. 1
