Directo En Tus Manos is the first live album released by Venezuelan singer-songwriter Carlos Baute. The album was released by Warner Music on September 8, 2009. It was filmed on the live show Baute presented on the Palacio de Vistalegre in Madrid on June 25, 2009. The show included guest appearances of Nek, Pastora Soler, Alex Ubago and Marta SÃ¡nchez. 1
