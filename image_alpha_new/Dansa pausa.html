"Dansa pausa" is single by Swedish multi-ethnic group Panetoz. It was released on Warner Music Sweden in February 2012, reaching the top of Sverigetopplistan, the official Swedish Singles Chart on week 19/2012 dated 11 May 2012. The single was certified gold on 8 May 2012. An English-language version "Dance Pause" was also released. 1

Cover versions

Jessy Matador, the Democratic Republic of the Congo singer, currently residing in France has recorded a cover of the song with added French lyrics. The track credited to "Jessy Matador featuring Panetoz" appears as track number three on Matador's third album Authentik on Wagram Music released on 28 June 2013. 7
