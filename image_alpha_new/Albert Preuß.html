Albert Preu√ü (born 29 January 1864, date of death unknown) was a German sports shooter who competed in the 1912 Summer Olympics. 2

He won the bronze medal in the clay pigeons team event. 3

He also competed in the running deer, single shots event and finished 23rd. In the running deer, double shots event he finished 17th and in the trap competition he finished 4th. 4
