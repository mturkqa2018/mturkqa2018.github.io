KBRU-HD2 (94.7-2 FM is the HD2 digital subchannel of radio station KBRU/Oklahoma City. Broadcasting a Regional Mexican format, the feed also simulcasts over translator K253BV (98.5 FM) and K253AY (98.5 FM). 1

Translator

This K253BV translator was assigned its own call sign on December 6, 2013. KBRU-HD2 is relayed by a single FM translator to extend its broadcast area. 2
