Bizet is a station on the Brussels Metro, served by the western branch of line 5. It opened on 10 January 1992 and is named after Place Bizet, under which it is located in the municipality of Anderlecht. 1

From its opening until 2003, Bizet was the western terminus of line 1B. In 2003 the western extension from Bizet to Erasme/Erasmus was opened. 2
