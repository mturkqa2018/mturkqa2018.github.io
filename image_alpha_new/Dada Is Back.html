Dada is Back is a 2017 Indian Kannada crime gangster drama film directed by Santhosh produced by Ajay Raj Urs and Dr Shankar under the Apple Films & Dayanand Sounds production The film features Arun Kumar, Ajay Raj Urs, Shravya along with the Tamil actor R. Parthiepan, making his debut in Kannada cinema, in the lead roles. The film's score and soundtrack are composed by Anoop Seelin whilst the cinematography is by Nagesh Acharya. 2

The film's trailer was officially released on 1 April 2017 by actor Sudeep in Bangalore. The film was released across Karnataka on 21 July 2017. 3

Review

The Times of India reviewed the film giving 3 stars out of 5 and remarked that the film is for "those who like tales revolving crime and underworld. Though, it doesn't provide as much action one would expect from an underworld film". 5
