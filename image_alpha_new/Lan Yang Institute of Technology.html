Lan Yang Institute of Technology (LYIT; Chinese: 蘭陽技術學院) is a private university in Toucheng Township, Yilan County, Taiwan. 1

History

LYIT was originally established as Fushin Junior College of Technology in March 1966. In 1983, it was renamed Fushin Institute of Technology. In 2001, the school was upgraded to Lan Yang Institute of Technology. 2

Transportation

The campus is accessible within walking distance northwest from Toucheng Station of the Taiwan Railways. 3
