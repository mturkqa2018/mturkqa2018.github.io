The 2016â€“17 Sydney Thunder WBBL season was the second in the team's history. Coached by Joanne Broadbent and captained by Alex Blackwell, the team competed in the WBBL|02 tournament. 1

At the conclusion of the group stage, the Thunder team was sixth on the table, and therefore did not qualify for the knockout phase. 2
