Casper Bouman (born 2 October 1985, in The Hague) is a sailor from the Netherlands. Bouman represented his country at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Qingdao. Bouman took 15th place on the Men's RS:X. 1

Nowadays Bouman is Sports coach and Professional Surfer. 2
