Sevil Yuldasheva (born 9 January 2002) is an Uzbekistani tennis player. 1

Yuldasheva has a career high ITF junior ranking of 575 achieved on 5 September 2016. 2

Yuldasheva made her WTA main draw debut at the 2017 Tashkent Open in the doubles draw partnering Olesya Kim. 3
