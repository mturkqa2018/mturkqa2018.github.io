The 1999 Ondrej Nepela Memorial was the Ondrej Nepela Memorial competition of the 1999â€“2000 season. It was the 7th Ondrej Nepela Memorial to be held. It is an annual senior-level international figure skating competition held in Bratislava, Slovakia. Skaters compete in four disciplines: men's singles, ladies' singles, pair skating, and ice dancing. It is usually one of the first senior internationals of the season. 1

The competition is named for 1972 Olympic gold medalist Ondrej Nepela. The 1999 competition was its 7th year. It took place between September 24 and 26, 1999. 2
