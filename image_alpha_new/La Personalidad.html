La Personalidad de Yolandita Monge is the final release under Patty Records by Puerto Rican singer Yolandita Monge and a re-issue of her second studio album A Star is Shining with different artwork and track listing. 1

The album was re-released in the early 90's by the label Disco Hit in cassette format. It is currently out of print in all media layouts. 2
