Kawage Station (æ²³èŠ¸é§…, Kawage-eki) is a railway station in Tsu, Mie Prefecture, Japan, operated by Ise Railway. The station is 16.4 rail kilometers from the terminus of the line at Kawarada Station. 1

History

Kawage Station opened on September 1, 1973 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR) Ise Line. The Ise Line was privatized on March 27, 1987, four days before the dissolution of the JNR on April 1, 1987. 2

Station layout

Kawage Station consists of two opposed side platforms connected by a level crossing. The station is unattended. 3
