2012 World Junior Championships in Athletics – Women's hammer throw

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The women's hammer throw at the 2012 World Junior Championships in Athletics was held at the Estadi Olímpic Lluís Companys on 12 and 14 July. 1

Records

Prior to the competition, the existing world junior and championship records were as follows. 2

Championship Record  Bianca Perie (ROU) 67.95 m Bydgoszcz, Poland 9 July 2008
World Junior Leading  Alexandra Tavernier (FRA) 68.44 m Forbach, France 27 May 2012
