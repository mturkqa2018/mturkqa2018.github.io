Roquessels (Languedocien: Ròcacèls) is a commune in the Hérault department in the Occitanie region in southern France. 1

Inhabitants of Roquessels are called Roquesselois 2

Winemaking

Roquessels is one of the seven communes which produces Faugères AOC wine. 3

Sights

Above the village stands the ruined mediaeval castle, the Château de Roquessels. The castle chapel still stands and is a listed historic site. 4
