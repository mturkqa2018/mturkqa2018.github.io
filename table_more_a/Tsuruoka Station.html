Lines

Tsuruoka Station is served by the Uetsu Main Line, and is located 139.4 kilometers from the starting point of the line at Niitsu Station. 2

Station layout

Tsuruoka Station has one side platform and one island platform connected to the station building by a footbridge. 3

The station has a Midori no Madoguchi staffed ticket office, a NewDays convenience store, and coin lockers. 4

History

Tsuruoka Station opened on 21 September 1918. With the privatization of Japanese National Railways (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East. 5
