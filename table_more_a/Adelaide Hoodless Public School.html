Adelaide Hoodless Public School is located at 71 Maplewood Avenue Hamilton, Ontario, and the school is a member of the Hamilton-Wentworth District School Board. Built in 1911, the school currently has 544 students enrolled in grades ranging from junior kindergarten to grade 8. 1

  • School Principal - Kelly Charles
  • Vice Principal - Kelly McCrory
  • Acting Superintendent of Education, Leadership & Learning -Jamie Nunn
  • Interim Director of Education - Pam Reinholdt
  • Trustee - Larry Pattison

Program highlights

Adelaide Hoodless takes part in the following programs: 2

  • Provincial Turnaround
  • School Boys Literacy
  • Smart Board Pilot school
  • Effective Teaching/Learning Cycles through SEF
