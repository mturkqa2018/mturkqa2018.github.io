Carlos Cadena Gaitan (born June 6, 1983, Medellin, Colombia) is a professor, research and urban activist. Cadena Gaitan holds a Ph.D from the University of Maastricht in the Netherlands, and is an affiliated researcher with the United Nations University (UNU-MERIT).
