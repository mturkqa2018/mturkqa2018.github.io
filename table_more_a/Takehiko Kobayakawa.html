Takehiko Kobayakawa (å°æ—©å· æ¯…å½¦, Kobayakawa Takehiko), (born November 15, 1961) is a former Japanese baseball player of the Hiroshima Toyo Carp and Yakult Swallows of Japan's Central League. 2

He was born in Aki-ku, Hiroshima, Hiroshima Prefecture. 3

He led his team into two Japan Series and winning one title (1984:Hiroshima and 1997:Yakult). 4
