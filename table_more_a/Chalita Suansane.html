Early life

Chalita was born in Nonthaburi, Thailand, but moved to Samut Prakan, Thailand as a child. Her father originally from Yasothon, Soranan Suansane is a company driver. Her mother, Chutikan Suansane, who is (Luk khrueng) half Thai-German originally from Nakhon Si Thammarat, is a King Power's employee. She currently lives in Bangkok, Thailand and has previously worked as a commercial model. 2

Pageantry

Miss Universe Thailand 2016

Chalita was crowned Miss Universe Thailand 2016 on 23 July 2016 at the Royal Paragon Hall in Bangkok by outgoing titleholder Aniporn Chalermburanawong. She was studying Microbiology when her sister sent an application for her in the Miss Universe Thailand competition last July 2016. This was Chalita's first ever beauty pageant. 3

Miss Universe 2016

She represented Thailand at the Miss Universe 2016 pageant in The Philippines where she won the fan vote and placed in the Top 6. She became the first person from Thailand to make the Top 6 since Porntip Nakhirunkanok in 1988. 4
