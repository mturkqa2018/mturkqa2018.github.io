Best Selection (Aimer album)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Best Selection is the title of two compilation albums released by Aimer on May 3, 2017. Each album was released in three versions: a regular CD edition, a limited CD + Blu-ray edition (Type-A), and a limited CD + DVD edition (Type-B). 1
