The men's standing high jump was one of six jumping events on the athletics at the 1908 Summer Olympics programme in London. The competition was held on July 23, 1908. 1

23 high jumpers from eleven nations competed. 2

Records

These were the standing world and Olympic records (in metres) prior to the 1908 Summer Olympics. 3
