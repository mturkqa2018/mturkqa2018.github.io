Tumpat Football Association (Malay: Persatuan Bola Sepak Jajahan Tumpat) commonly known as Tumpat FA is a football team based in Kota Bharu, Kelantan. They play in Malaysia FAM League. In 2012, the team was promoted to play in Malaysia FAM League. In 2014, Tumpat FA has withdrawn from competition because financial issue. Tumpat FA is not Kelantan FA second team. 2

Stadium

Sultan Muhammad IV Stadium in Kota Bharu, Kelantan is their ground. The capacity of the stadium is 30,000. They share the stadium with Kelantan FA and become their ground since 2013. 3
