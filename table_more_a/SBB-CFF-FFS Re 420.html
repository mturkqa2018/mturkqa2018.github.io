The Re 420, originally (and still widely called) Re 4/4, series are the most common electric locomotives of the Swiss Federal Railways. They are used for passenger services throughout Switzerland alone or in pairs. For freight services, they are sometimes paired with the Re 620, especially in mountainous regions. That pairing is referred to by the term Re 10/10. The Re 430, originally known as the Re 4/4, are a derivative of the Re 420 modified for higher traction but lower speed. 1

The Re 420 locomotives were produced over a period of 21 years, from 1964 to 1985. 2
