Bat Yam-Komemiyut railway station is a railway station on the Ra'anana Westâ€“Sderot line. It is located at the Komemiyut interchange on the Ayalon Highway in Holon next to the border with Bat Yam. 1

Train service

The station is situated on the Ra'ananaâ€“Be'er Sheva line. All trains in the line stop at this station. 2

On most weekday hours there is a train every 30 minutes each direction, All trains stops at all stations of the line. On rush hours there is a train every 15 minutes each direction, Some of the southbound trains terminate at Rishon LeZion Moshe Dayan or Ashkelon and will not continue to Be'er Sheva. 3

Public transport connections

The station is served by bus routes 2A, 6, 11, 14, 19, 26, 40, 85, 87, 99, 143, 240 and 425. 4
