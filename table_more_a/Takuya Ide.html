Biography

On 1993 Ide debuted as a model in Harajuku. 3

From April 2001 to March 2005 he appeared in 'Tensai TV-kun Series as a "TV Senshi". 4

On 2010 Ide became a member of Cocoa Otoko. The group later disbanded on March 2012. He later became a solo singer in 2013. Ide graduated from Aoyama Gakuin University in March 2015. On April 2016 he later became a member of the dance vocal group Ryoga. He left Ryoga on December 31st, 2017. 5
