The 1905 Western University of Pennsylvania football team was an American football team that represented Western University of Pennsylvania (later renamed the University of Pittsburgh) as an independent during the 1905 college football season. In its third season under head coach Arthur Mosse, the team compiled a 10â€“2 record, shut out nine of its twelve opponents, and outscored all opponents by a total of 405 to 46. The team played its home games at Exposition Park in Pittsburgh. 1
