The Apartment: Rising Stars Edition is the fifth season of reality show competition The Apartment which after six seasons is the longest running reality competition television show in Asia. The contestants are 12 up-and-coming interior designers competing to win $100,000. Its judges are Laurence Llewelyn-Bowen, Jamie Durie (who also hosts), and Genevieve Gorder. 1
