Eastern High School is a public high school in Winchester, Ohio, USA. It is the only high school in the Eastern Local School District. 1

Athletics

The school colors are red and white. The mascot is the Warrior. Eastern High School has golf team, girls' and boys' soccer teams, baseball, softball, girls' and boys' basketball, golf, track & field and cross country teams. 2
