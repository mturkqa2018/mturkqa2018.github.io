Ajay Sastry
Born (1974-07-26) July 26, 1974 (age44)
Hyderabad, Telangana, India
OccupationFilm director, screenwriter

Ajay Sastry is a Director/Writer who directed his maiden film called ' Nenu Meeku Telusa ' starring Manchu Manoj, Sneha Ullal and Riya Sen. 1

His journey started with a short film called "12"(Baarah) that was produced by KAD Movies And Rana Daggubati (of dum marro dum Fame). In the past, he played the role of Associate Director & Screenplay writer for movies such as Rakhi and Danger. 2

He had served as president of YOCS(Young Orators Club) for many years. He had been an active participant and winner at youth festivals in and around Hyderabad. 3

To add to this Ajay has been the lead singer of Rock bands like "Alter Egoz" and "Jekyll and Hyde". An orator and a poet, his poems have been published in the "American Anthology Of Poetry". 4

During his schooling, he had an opportunity through the YMCA to attend the 'World Championship Of Modern Western Dances' at London and ran a dance school for almost five years before he shifted to being an ad film maker. 5

His first movie as a director 'Nenu Meeku Telusa' hit the silver screen on October 10, 2008 6
