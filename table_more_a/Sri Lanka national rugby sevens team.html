Australia playing Sri Lanka at the 2014 Commonwealth Games.

The Sri Lanka national rugby sevens team has competed in the Hong Kong Sevens since the 1980s. In 1989, veteran rugby commentator Bill McLaren mentions them in an article on the Hong Kong Sevens, saying that their team had players such as Kothalawala, Ekanayake and Lakshantha. 1
