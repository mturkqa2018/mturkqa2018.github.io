Akbar Djuraev (born (1999-10-08)8 October 1999) is an Uzbekistani weightlifter competing in the 105Â kg category until 2018, and the 102Â kg and 109Â kg categories starting in 2018 after the International Weightlifting Federation reorganized the categories. 1

He is currently the junior world record holder in the snatch and total in the 102 kg division. 2

Career

At the 2018 Junior World Weightlifting Championships he competed in the 105 kg division, winning the silver medal in the snatch, clean & jerk, and total. 3

He competed at the 2018 World Weightlifting Championships in the 102 kg category, winning a gold medal in the snatch and setting the junior world records in the snatch and total. 4
