Tithorea harmonia, the Harmonia tiger-wing or Harmonia tiger, is a species of butterflies belonging to the family Nymphalidae. 2

Description

Tithorea harmonia has a wingspan reaching about 70 millimetres (2.8Â in). This toxic "tiger" butterfly has the usual pattern of black wings with bright orange bands. The forewings have a black tip. Also the antennae are orange. Larvae feed on Prestonia species. 3

Distribution

This widespread species can be found from Mexico to South America. 4
