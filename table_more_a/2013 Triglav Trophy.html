The 2013 Triglav Trophy was an international figure skating competition in Slovenia during the 2012–13 season. The competition was held on 27–31 March 2013 in the town of Jesenice. Skaters competed in the disciplines of men's and ladies' singles on the senior, junior, and novice levels. 1
