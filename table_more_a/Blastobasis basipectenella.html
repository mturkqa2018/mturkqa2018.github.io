Blastobasis basipectenella is a moth in the family Blastobasidae. It is found in Thailand. 2

The length of the forewings is 6â€“6.1Â mm. The forewings have greyish-brown and pale greyish-brown scales, each tipped with white. The hindwings are pale grey. 3

Etymology

The species name is derived from Latin basis (meaning base) and pecten (meaning comb) and refers to the comb-like process arising from the base of the lower part of the valva. 4
