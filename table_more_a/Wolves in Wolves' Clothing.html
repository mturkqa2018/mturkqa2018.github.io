Wolves in Wolves' Clothing is the tenth studio album by the American punk rock band NOFX. 1

Release

It was released on April 18, 2006 through Fat Wreck Chords. The band went on the 2006 edition of the Warped Tour. In January 2007, the band went on a tour of California alongside Strike Anywhere, Dead to Me and Love Equals Death. In November, the band went on a tour of Europe with support from The Loved Ones. In February and March 2008, the band headlined the first leg of the 2008 Fat Tour, alongside No Use for a Name, American Steel and the Flatliners. 2
