Reisdorf (Luxembourgish: Reisduerf) is a commune and small town in eastern Luxembourg, on the river Sauer. It is part of the canton of Diekirch. 1

In 2005, Reisdorf, which lies in the centre of the commune, had a population of 479. Other towns within the commune include Hoesdorf. 2
