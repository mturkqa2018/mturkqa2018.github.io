List of Sullivan & Son episodes

Sullivan & Son is a TBS sitcom created by Steve Byrne and Rob Long. The series premiered on July 19, 2012, and follows Steve Sullivan, who surprises his parents when he leaves his job behind as a corporate lawyer to take over a bar owned by his father. 1

On November 20, 2014, the show was cancelled after 33 episodes and its third season. 2
