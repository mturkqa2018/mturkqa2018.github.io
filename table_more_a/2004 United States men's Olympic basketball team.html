2004 United States men's Olympic basketball team

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 2004 United States men's Olympic basketball team represented the United States at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens, Greece. The team was coached by Basketball Hall of Famer Larry Brown. The team was noted for being one of the most disappointing Olympic teams, losing three games against their opponents (the most ever lost by a USA Olympic basketball team) as well as the biggest loss in a game in their history. This would be the second time that Team USA would win the bronze medal, having also done so at the 1988 Summer Olympics. 1
