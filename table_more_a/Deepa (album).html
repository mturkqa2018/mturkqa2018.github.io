Deepa is the third studio album by new jack swing group Troop released by Atlantic Records on June 2, 1992. The album includes the #1 R&B hit Sweet November written by Babyface. Also, It is the final album to feature all five members until six years later. 1

Deepa is largely a concept album, and includes somewhat racier subject matter than their previous two releases . 2
