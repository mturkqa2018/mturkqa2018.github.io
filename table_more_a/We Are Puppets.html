We Are Puppets is the dÃ©but studio album by the British band Tiger. It was released in 1996 and reached number one-hundred and eight on the UK Albums Chart. We Are Puppets includes the singles "Race", "My Puppet Pal" and "On the Rose", the latter of which was re-recorded for single release. 1

The album was produced by Daren Eskriett and the band-themselves, with the exception of "My Puppet Pal" which was produced by the band with Pete Briquette. Receiving mixed reviews the album was first released by the Island sub-label Trade 2 as a CD and LP in November 1996. 2
