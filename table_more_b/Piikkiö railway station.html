Piikkiön rautatieasema is a railway station in the municipality of Kaarina, Piikkiö, near Turku, Finland. It was opened in 1889. The station building, designed by a Finnish architect Bruno Granholm was built in 1888 and later expanded in 1922. The last train stopped at the station on May 26, 1979. 1

The station is located in the central area of the town of Piikkiö. 2

On September 2016, the railway station was sold to a private buyer. 3
