The company produces among others the FAMAE CT-30 carbine, FAMAE SAF submachine gun, FAMAE FD-200 sniper rifle, Rayo rocket launcher and FAMAE revolver. 1

History

Among its projects are the SIG SG 510-4 rifle, SIG SG 542 rifle, FAMAE SAF submachine gun, Rayo MLRS, among others. Responsible for modifying and maintaining the tanks, aircraft and other military devices that reach the Chilean Army. 3

FAMAE specializes in research projects and Weapon System Modernization. 4
