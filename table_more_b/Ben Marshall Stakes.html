The Ben Marshall Stakes is a Listed flat horse race in Great Britain open to horses aged three years or older. It is run on the Rowley Mile course at Newmarket over a distance of 1 mile (1,609 metres), and it is scheduled to take place each year in late October or early November. 1

The race is named in honour of the equine artist Ben Marshall (1768-1835). 2
