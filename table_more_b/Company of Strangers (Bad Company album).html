Company of Strangers is the eleventh studio album by the English blues rock band Bad Company, and their first with Robert Hart (in place of Brian Howe who had replaced Paul Rodgers in 1986 as lead vocalist). The album was released in June 1995. To date, it is the band's latest studio album of all-new material. 2
