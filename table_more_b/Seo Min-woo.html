2014: Military Service

On March 4, 2014, Seo enlisted in the military for his mandatory military service. He enlisted as an active-duty soldier. Seo was discharged after 21 months, on December 4, 2015. 6

Death

On March 25, 2018, Seo was found in his home in Gangnam in a state of cardiac arrest. Emergency responders declared him dead on arrival. Seo's funeral was held on March 27, 2018. 7
