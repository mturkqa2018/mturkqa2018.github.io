Marcellus Joannes Irma Selen (born 16 April 1932) is a Belgian painter. His early works were in the styles of cubism and expressionism and surrealism. During the seventies he painted in the styles of popart and hyperrealism. His recent works have been classified as (photo) negativism, negative realism, or short negarealism. 1
