Song information

It was written by Krista Cappan, Fabrice Nyiri and JoÃ«l Guidon and was released on March 13, 2008, in the Nord-Pas-de-Calais, then in the full France in April, under Nord-Way Productions label. 2

It was surprisingly a hit in France, probably through the film success: it entered the SNEP singles chart at number nine on 5 April 2008, with 2,475 sales, then jumped directly to number one, selling 7,643 units this week, then dropped slowly, totaling nine weeks in the top ten, 23 weeks in the top 50 and 38 weeks on the chart. In Belgium (Wallonia), the song debuted on the chart on 24 May 2008, peaked at number seven in its fourth week and remained on the chart for 12 weeks. 3

Ch'ti DJ toured in France to promote his song, particularly devoted to the discothÃ¨ques. The song is characterized by minimal lyrics with phrases taken from the film, and house and rap sonorities inspired by the tecktonik. The song was generally well received by the media, although the commercial aspect of the single was deemed obvious. It was also stated by Ozap that the song was "a little opportunist". 4
