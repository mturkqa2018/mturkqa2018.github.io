Far from Over is a studio album by American jazz musician Vijay Iyer. It was released in August 2017 under ECM Records. 1

Far from Over was generally well-regarded by professional music critics upon release. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album received an average score of 88, based on 5 reviews, indicating 'universal acclaim'. 2

In his review for AllMusic, Matt Collar stated that "What's particularly engaging about Far from Over is Iyer and his band's sense of danger and risk-taking. Ultimately, it's that balance of harmonically adventurous exploration and no-holds-barred blowing that make Far from Over nothing short of thrilling." 3
