Asad Sabetpour (born 1948 in Tehran) an Iranian born agriculturalist. 1

He left Iran in 1965 for higher studies in Pakistan and England where he received his BS and CDA in agricultural engineering. 2

After the regime change in 1979 he was appointed by secretary of defense Admiral Dr Madani as a managing director of cancelled Brown and root "Marina Cove" Chah Bahar submarine base project. while in Sisitan and Baluchistan he was asked to act as governor of the state. Later he was sent to Abadan as Mayor of that city due to serious issues shaping up in that region. He left Iran in 1981 and is currently living in U.S. 3
