Muela (full name Manufacturas Muela) is a Spanish knife manufacturer based in Ciudad Real with an annual output of about 350 000 pieces a year. The products include hunting, combat, luxury, outdoor and folding knives and knife accessories. 1

History

The company was founded by Eladio Muela who celebrated, in 2002, 50 years since he created his first knife, a folding model. 2

Materials

Most Muela knives are made of stainless steel alloys with vanadium, chromium and molybdenum content; few luxury items are made of pattern welded steel. 3
