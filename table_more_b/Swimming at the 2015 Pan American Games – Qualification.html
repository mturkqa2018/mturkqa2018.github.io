Qualification system

As with previous editions of the Games, A/B qualifying times will be used, with a target number of 276 swimmers. Times need to be swum in an approved meet sometime between January 1, 2014 and May 1, 2015. A total of 36 open water swimmers will also qualify (18 per gender). 1
