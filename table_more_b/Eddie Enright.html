Eddie Enright (born 1974 in Thurles, County Tipperary) is a retired Irish sportsperson. He played hurling with his local club Thurles Sarsfields and with the Tipperary senior inter-county team in the 1990s and 2000s. He also won 3 Fitzgibbon Cup medals with UCC, captaining the side in 1998. He is currently hurling with Watergrasshill GAA club in Cork. 1
