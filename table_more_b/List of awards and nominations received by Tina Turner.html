Guinness Book of World Records

Main article: Guinness Book of World Records

In January 1988 Turner set a Guinness World Record "for the largest paying rock concert attendance for a solo artist" by performing in front of approximately 180,000 people at Maracanã Stadium in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, which remained until 1997. Some sources claim that she's also listed in the Guinness Book of Records for selling more concert tickets than any solo performer in history. 10
