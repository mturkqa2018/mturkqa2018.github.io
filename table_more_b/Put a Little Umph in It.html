"Put a Little Umph in It" is the first single from Jagged Edge's studio album Baby Makin' Project. It features popâ€“R&B singer Ashanti. The song was their first single since being dropped from Columbia Records and getting signed to Island Def Jam. 1

Music video

The music video was shot by Norwegian director Ray Kay around the week of July 17, 2007. Jagged Edge members looked for girl video dancers with acting ability (a big plus for "Soprano's" style narrative). The video premiered on 106 & Park on August 17. 2

The video premiered on 106 & Park as the New Joint of the Day on August 15, 2007. Ashanti's verse was taken out of the song, and replaced with a snippet clip of "Whole Town Laughing." 3

Recently on HOT 97 in New York City, Jagged Edge reportedly said Ashanti was not featured in the music video due to a conflict disagreement with Irv Gotti. 4

Remix

There is a So So Def Remix of the track on the Japanese version of the album Baby Makin' Project. 5
