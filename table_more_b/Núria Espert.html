Family and early life

She went to Institut Maragall, a high school in Barcelona, and she started to study theatre while she was taking some courses of music and languages. 2

When she was 17 years old, she had to substitute the actress Elvira Noriega in the play Medea, that was her first great success. 3

When she was 19 years old, she married the actor Armando Moreno, who would later become her manager. They both founded a theatre company in 1959, which started in the Teatro Recoletos in Madrid. 4
