Hello Mister Zamindar is a 1965 Tamil romantic comedy film, directed and produced by K. J. Mahadevan under the banner of Sudharsanam Pictures. The film dialogue was written by Ve. Lakshmanan and the story was written by K. J. Mahadevan respectively. Music was by Viswanathan-Ramamoorthy. It stars Gemini Ganesan M. R. Radha and T. S. Balaiah in lead, with Savitri, V. Gopalakrishnan and K. S. Angamuthu in supporting roles. The film is a loose adaptation of Frank Capra's You Can't Take it With You (1938). 2
