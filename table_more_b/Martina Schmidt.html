Martina Schmidt (later Schwarz, born 1 September 1960) is a German former volleyball player who competed for East Germany in the 1980 Summer Olympics. 2

She was born in Parchim. 3

In 1980 she was part of the East German team which won the silver medal in the Olympic tournament. She played three matches. 4
