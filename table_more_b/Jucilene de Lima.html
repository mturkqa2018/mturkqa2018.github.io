Jucilene Sales de Lima (born 14 September 1990) is a Brazilian athlete specialising in the javelin throw. She represented her country at the 2013 World Championships without qualifying for the final. In addition, she has won multiple medals on regional level, including the bronze at the 2015 Pan American Games. 1

Her personal best in the event is 62.89 metres set in SÃ£o Paulo in 2014. This is the current national record. 2
