Bruzolo di Susa (Italian: Stazione di Bruzolo) is a railway station in Bruzolo. The station is located on the Turin-Modane railway. The train services are operated by Trenitalia. 1

Train services

The station is served by the following services: 2

  • Turin Metropolitan services (SFM3) Bardonecchia - Bussoleno - Turin
  • Turin Metropolitan services (SFM3) Susa - Bussoleno - Turin
