Club career

Varglien played club football with Juventus for most of his career, winning five Serie A championships, and also serving as the team's captain. 2

International career

At international level, Varglien was also part of the Italian national football team which won the 1934 FIFA World Cup. 3

Personal life

Mario's brother, Giovanni Varglien also played football in Italy and with the Italian national team; the two brothers played at Juventus together. 4
