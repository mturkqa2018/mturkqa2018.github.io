"In My Cabana" is a song recorded by Polish singer Margaret. It was written by Anderz Wrethov, Linnea Deb, Arash Labaf, and Robert Uhlmann. The song was released for digital download on 10 February 2018. 1

"In My Cabana" peaked at number three on the Polish Airplay Chart and number eight on the Swedish Singles Chart. Margaret participated with the song in Melodifestivalen 2018, and qualified to Andra Chansen from the second semi-final on 10 February 2018. She subsequently progressed to the final and finished in seventh place with 103 points. 2
