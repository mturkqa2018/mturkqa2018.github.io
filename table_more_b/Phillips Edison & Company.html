Phillips Edison & Company is an American owner, operator, acquirer and developer of shopping centers throughout the US. Founded in 1991, the Company is headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio and has offices in Salt Lake City, Utah, New York City, New York and Atlanta, Georgia. 1

In 2017, Phillips Edison & Company was ranked as one of the fastest growing private companies in Cincinnati by the Cincinnati Business Courier. Phillips Edison & Company has doubled in size every three years for the past 25 years. 2
