Zoe Hives (born 24 October 1996) is a professional Australian tennis player. 2

She has career-high WTA rankings of 178 in the singles and 290 in the doubles rankings, both achieved on 28 January 2019. Hives so far has won four singles and two doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. 3

In November 2017, Hives won the most important doubles career title to date at the 2017 Bendigo Women's International, partnering Alison Bai. This resulted in them being awarded a wildcard into the Australian Open. 4

Hives is coached by Michael Logarzo and based in Melbourne. 5

2019

In January, Hives was awarded a wildcard into the Australian Open and defeated Bethanie Mattek-Sands in round one - her first ever Grand Slam main-draw win. She lost to Caroline Garcia in the second round. 6
