Main article: Triathlon at the 2018 Asian Games

The women's triathlon was part of the Triathlon at the 2018 Asian Games program, was held in JSC Lake Jakabaring on 31 August 2018. The race was held over the "international distance" and consisted of 1,500 metres (0.93Â mi) swimming, 40 kilometres (25Â mi) road bicycle racing, and 10 kilometres (6.2Â mi) road running. 1

Japanese triathlete Yuko Takahashi clinched the gold medal with a time of 1:59:29. Zhong Mengying from China and Hoi Long from Macau captured the silver and bronze medal respectively. 2
