Atef Yassien ElSherif was the former chairman of the Egyptian Exchange (July 2013 â€“ August 2013), the former chairman counselors of the Egyptian Exchange and the founder and principal partner of ElSherif Law Firm & Consultants.ElSherif represents the Egyptian Exchange in many stock exchange conferences and International, African & Arabian stock market unions and contributes in preparation of many of legislations and Egyptian stock market rules since joining the Egyptian Exchange in 1994 to 2015. and also he contributes in establishing the Damascus Securities Exchange and the Libyan Stock Market. He also is the founder and board member of Egyptian investor relations association (EIRA). 1

Career

  • Founder and principal partner of ElSherif Law Firm & Consultants.
  • Chairman of the Egyptian Exchange in (July 2013 - August 2013).
  • Former counselor of the Egyptian Exchange.
  • Deputy of the head of legal department at the Egyptian Exchange.
  • ElSherif works at the legal department since 1994.
  • Representative of the Egyptian Exchange in many conferences that are related to Arabian stock markets in Egypt & abroad
  • Supervisor of the administrative office of the Egyptian Exchange's board of directors.
  • Member of the permanent committee for preparation and amendments the Egyptian Exchange's regulations.
  • Member of the trading committee of the Egyptian Exchange.
  • Member of the workers affairs committee of the Egyptian Exchange.
  • Pleading before the various courts since 1990.
  • Participating in many arbitration litigations.

Publications

ElSherif has various notable articles, He has published a book titled "Stock Exchange: Conflict between Bulls & Bears" and also published a book titled "Small & Medium-sized Enterprises". 2

Membership

  • Member of the BAR Association since 1989 to present, and also member of the Arab Lawyers Union since 1990 and is admitted before the court of cassation since 2010.
  • Member of the Board of Directors of the Arab organization of Young Lawyers (based in Morocco).
  • Founder and secretary-general of Egyptian investor relations association (EIRA) since 2010.
  • Vice-Chairman of the board of directors of the Mediterranean sea lawyers association (based in Tunisia).
  • Arbitrator at Arbitration Center, Ain Shams University.
  • Board member in EGID Company owned by Egyptian Exchange and Nasdaq-OMX.
  • Member of the Egyptian association for political economy and statistics and legislation.
