Neal Lee Coty is an American country music artist. A musician from an early age, Coty has recorded two studio albums, both on divisions of Mercury Records. He has also charted one single on the Billboard Hot Country Songs charts, in addition to co-writing singles for Mark Chesnutt ("She Was"), James Wesley ("Real"), Craig Morgan ("Every Friday Afternoon"), Blake Shelton ("Playboys of the Southwestern World"), Flynnville Train ("Last Good Time"), and Heartland ("Mustache"). 1
