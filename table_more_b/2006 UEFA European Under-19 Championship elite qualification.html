UEFA U-19 Championship 2006 (Elite Round) is the second round of qualifications for the Final Tournament of UEFA U-19 Championship 2006. Spain, England, and Czech Republic automatically qualify for this round. The winners of each group join hosts Poland at the Final Tournament. 1
