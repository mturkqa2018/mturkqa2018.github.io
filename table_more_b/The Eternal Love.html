Synopsis

An unforeseen event changes the life of Qu Tan'er (Liang Jie), after a suicide attempt over her love for Mo Yihuai (Wang Ruichang). She wakes up to find that another person has entered her body, namely Qu Xiaotan, who has time-traveled from the modern world. Both girls share a different personality, with Qu Tan'er being gentle and submissive while Qu Xiaotan is wild and uninhibited; whenever one of them tells a lie, the other takes over their shared body. Duty forces Qu Tan'er to marry Mo Liancheng (Xing Zhaolin), thus beginning an unexpected romance between two people thrown together by circumstance: infact, as Tan'er persists in her love for Yihuai, Liancheng falls in love with modern girl Xiaotan. 3

Production

Pre-production of the series began in November 2016. The production team from Tencent Penguin Pictures selected the novel due to several trending elements, such as "sweet and touching" romance and fantasy. 4

Shooting commenced in Hengdian World Studios on February 2, 2017 and wrapped up on March 27, 2017. 5

Prequel

In November 2017, it was announced via Tencent Media Conference that the series will have a second season, with the original leads returning. The series began filming in Guizhou on March 17, 2018. 6
