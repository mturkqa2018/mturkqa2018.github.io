The 2014-2015 cyclo-cross season consists of three international series conducted in the bicycle racing discipline of cyclo-cross: 1

  • World Cup
  • Superprestige
  • BPost Bank Trophy

The season began on 5 October with the Superprestige Gieten, won by Mathieu van der Poel. It ended on 14 February 2015. 2
