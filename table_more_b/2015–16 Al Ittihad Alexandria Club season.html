The 2015-16 season was the 102nd season since the founding of Al Ittihad Alexandria Club in 1914. The club plays in the Egyptian Premier League and the Egypt Cup. The club is one of the oldest Egyptian clubs, celebrating its centenary in 2014 in front of Sporting CP. 2
