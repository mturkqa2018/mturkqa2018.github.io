Pinna Nesbit (1896-1950) was a Canadian silent film actress. She was married three times and had an affair with King Edward VIII, when he was Prince of Wales, and not yet King. Her first husband, Harley Knoles, directed several of her films. 1

She was the daughter of the Captain William A. Nesbit, a British army officer stationed in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 2

She was married to the haberdasher, Freddie Cruger, at the time of her affair with the Prince of Wales. 3
