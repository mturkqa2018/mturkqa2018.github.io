"I'm in Love with a Capital "U"" is a song recorded by American country music artist Joe Diffie. It was released in May 1995 as the fourth single from the album Third Rock from the Sun. The song reached #21 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart. The song was written by Paul Nelson and Craig Wiseman. 1
