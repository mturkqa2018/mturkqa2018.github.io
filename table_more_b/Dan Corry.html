Dan Corry is a British was the head of the Number 10 Policy Unit for British prime minister Gordon Brown. He was employed as a special adviser. After leaving Number 10, he became director of the economics division of FTI Consulting then in 2011 head of the think tank New Philanthropy Capital. 3
