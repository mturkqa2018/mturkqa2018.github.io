Romania in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest
◄2005 • 2006 • 2007►

Romania selected their Junior Eurovision entry for 2007 through Selecţia Naţională, a national final consisting of 21 songs. The winners were New Star Music, with their song Povestea mea, receiving the maximum points from both the jury and the televoters. 1
