Sarah Gancher is an American playwright. 1

She has worked with ensembles including the Telluride Theatre in Telluride, Colorado, The Team in New York City, and the Blue Man Group. In addition, she has assisted on the television show The Colbert Report, and worked with the theater organizations The Big Apple Circus, and Stellapolaris in Norway. She is also a jazz violinist. 2
