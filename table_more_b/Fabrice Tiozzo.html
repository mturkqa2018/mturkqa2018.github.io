Fabrice Tiozzo (born May 8, 1969) is a French former boxer who fought at light heavyweight (175 lb). His record upon retiring was 48–2 (32 KOs). 1

Tiozzo was a two-time light heavyweight champion as well as a one time cruiserweight champion. 2

He retired in 2006 while still WBA light heavyweight champion. 3
