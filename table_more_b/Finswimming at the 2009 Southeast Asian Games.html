Finswimming at the 2009 Southeast Asian Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The finswimming events at the 2009 Southeast Asian Games were held from 15 December to 17 December in Vientiane, Laos. The Events were held at the Aquatic Center of the National Sports Complex. 1
