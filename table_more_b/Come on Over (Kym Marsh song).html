"Come on Over" is a song written by Deborah Andrews, Martin Harrington and Ash Howes for former Hear'say member Kym Marsh's debut album Standing Tall. It was released as second solo single on 7 July 2003 in the United Kingdom. It charted at a peak position of number ten in the UK Singles Chart. 1
