Geina sheppardi, or Sheppard's plume moth, is a moth of the family Pterophoridae. It known from western North America, including Mississippi, Ontario and Wisconsin. 2

The wingspan is about 15Â mm. Adults are on wing from May to June. There is one generation per year. 3

The larvae feed on Vitis species. They make ties in young leaves. Full-grown caterpillars are about 10Â mm long, pale green and hairy. 4
