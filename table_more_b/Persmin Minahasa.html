Persmin Minahasa is an Indonesian football club based in Minahasa. Persmin Minahasa plays in the Indonesian football league, Liga Nusantara. Its home stadium is Maesa Stadium. In 2005, Persmin Minahasa was promoted to the first division of Indonesian Football League. They have two group of supporters. They are called Manguni Fans Club and Persmin Mania. The team's homebase is in Tondano, (capital of Minahasa Regency), North Celebes, Indonesia. 1
