3s is the fifth full-length album by post-grunge band Smile Empty Soul. The album was released on May 22, 2012. "Afterlife" was chosen as the first single off the album along with an accompanying music video. Music videos were later released for the songs "Not Alike" & "Wrecking Ball". 1
