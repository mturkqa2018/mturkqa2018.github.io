The J. Colin English School (also known as the J. Colin English Elementary School) is a historic school in North Fort Myers, Florida, United States. It is located at 120 Pine Island Road. On July 8, 1999, it was added to the U.S. National Register of Historic Places. 1

This property is part of the Lee County Multiple Property Submission, a Multiple Property Submission to the National Register. 2
