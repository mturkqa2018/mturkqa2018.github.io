Leandro Guido Testa (born March 24, 1976 in General Belgrano, Buenos Aires) is a former footballer from Argentina who played as a defender. 1

Football career

Testa started out at Estudiantes de La Plata in 1996. He spent 6 years at the club, making 116 appearances during this period. In 2002, he chose to join Nueva Chicago where he indisputably earned a spot in the starting eleven since his arrival. In June 2004 he transferred to Arsenal de SarandÃ­, but returned to Chicago the following year after only 4 games played with el viaducto. In early 2007, Testa signed for Ferro Carril Oeste. 2
