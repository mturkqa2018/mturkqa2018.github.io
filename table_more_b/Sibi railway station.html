Sibi Junction railway station (Urdu: سبی جنکشن ریلوے اسٹیشن‎, Balochi: سبی جنکشن ریلوے اسٹیشن) is located in Sibi, Balochistan, Pakistan. It is staffed and has a booking office. The station served as the junction between the Rohri-Chaman Railway Line and Kandahar State Railway. 1
