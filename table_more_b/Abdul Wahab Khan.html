Abdul Wahab Khan was the 3rd speaker of the National Assembly of Pakistan. He was preceded and succeeded by Maulvi Tamizuddin Khan. He was born into a middle-class Muslim family in East Pakistan. In later years, he became a Bengali Nationalist. He is the grandfather of Salman Khan, founder of the Khan Academy. 1
