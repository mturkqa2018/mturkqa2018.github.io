Life Goes On is a studio album by Paul Williams, released in 1972. 1

Notable songs from the album include "I Won't Last a Day Without You" and "Out in the Country". 2

Jackson Browne and Linda Ronstadt sang background vocals on the title track. 3

Jimmy Webb arranged the strings on "Rose." 4

"Where Do I Go from Here" selected as ending title for "Thunderbolt and Lightfoot", and also recorded by Elvis Presley for album Elvis. 5
