Anthony "Bronco" McLoughlin (10 August 1938 - 26 March 2019) was an Irish actor, stuntman, and animal trainer. 1

He performed and co-ordinated stunts in more than 40 movies, including: 2

  • Being tied to a wooden cross that floats over the top of a waterfall in the opening scene of The Mission
  • A stormtrooper in Star Wars in 1977
  • Superman, 1978
  • A mujahideen in Rambo III, 1988
  • Several James Bond movies, including A View to a Kill, 1985
  • A customs officer in Total Recall, 1990

In the horror movie by Clive Barker, Hellbound: Hellraiser II, because of a neck accident, Kenneth Cranham only wore the Channard Cenobite makeup for one day, McLoughlin played the Channard Cenobite for the rest of the shoot. He also played the part of the assassin in Martin Scorsese's Gangs of New York. 3

On 26 March 2019, it was announced that McLoughlin had died. 4

Awards

He was inducted into the Stuntmen's Hall of Fame. 5
