Ignacio Diez de la Barrera y Bastida (1645–1709) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as Bishop of Durango (1701–1704). 1

Biography

Ignacio Diez de la Barrera y Bastida was born in Mexico in 1645. On 16 November 1705, he was appointed during the papacy of Pope Clement XI as Bishop of Durango. On 30 January 1707, he was consecrated bishop. He served as Bishop of Durango until his death on 20 September 1709. 2
