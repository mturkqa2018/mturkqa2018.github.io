Villacher Alpenarena is a ski jumping hill in Villach, Austria. 1

History

It was opened in 1937 and owned by SV Villach. It hosted six FIS Ski jumping World Cup individual events and one team event. David Unterberger holds the hill record. There are also K-15, K-30 and K-60 hills. 2
