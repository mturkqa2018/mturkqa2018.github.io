La Liga 1931-1932 season started November 22, 1931, and finished April 3, 1932. As the Spanish Second Republic all the clubs dropped the Royal patronage from their name and deleted the crowns from their shields. 1

Athletic Bilbao was the defending champion, but Madrid won its first title after finishing the season unbeaten. 2
