Kennedy Town & Mount Davis is one of the 15 constituencies in the Central and Western District 2

The constituency returns one district councillor to the Central and Western District Council, with an election every four years. The seat is currently held by Chan Hok-fung of the DAB. 3

Kennedy Town & Mount Davis constituency is loosely based on the Kennedy Town including Sai Wan Estate, Mount Davis and Green Island with estimated population of 17,270. 4
