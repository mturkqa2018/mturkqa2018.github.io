Neo Pornographia is a series of pornographic films directed by Michael Ninn and produced by his production company Ninn Worx. The full title of these movies are Neo Pornographia: The Secret Tapes of Michael Ninn. They represent Michael Ninn's more extreme and Gonzo expression of Pornography, while retaining his dark, moody atmosphere. Features very hardcore sex, including Double anal, and Double vaginal. 1

Five films were produced between 2005 and 2009. 2
