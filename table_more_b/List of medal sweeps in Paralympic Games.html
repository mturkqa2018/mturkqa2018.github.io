List of medal sweeps in Paralympic Games

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

A podium sweep is when one team wins all available medals in a single event in a sporting event. It is often a rare occurrence that often happens in athletics, cycling and swimming. Since the Paralympic Games started in 1960, there have been 44 podium sweeps (updated to the 2016 Summer Paralympics). 1
