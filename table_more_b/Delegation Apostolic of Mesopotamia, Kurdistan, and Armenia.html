Delegation Apostolic of Mesopotamia, Kurdistan, and Armenia

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Delegation Apostolic of Mesopotamia, Kurdistan, and Armenia was the Papal representative who administered the Latin Rite diocese of Baghdad and oversaw various Eastern Rite dioceses among the Catholic community. 1
