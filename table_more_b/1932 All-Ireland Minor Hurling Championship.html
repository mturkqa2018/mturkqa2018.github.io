The 1932 All-Ireland Minor Hurling Championship was the fifth staging of the All-Ireland Minor Hurling Championship since its establishment by the Gaelic Athletic Association in 1928. 1

Kilkenny entered the championship as the defending champions. 2

On 2 October 1932 Tipperary won the championship following an 8-6 to 5-1 defeat of Kilkenny in the All-Ireland final. This was their second All-Ireland title. 3
