Alon Livne is an Israeli fashion designer. Livne started his career at fashion houses Roberto Cavalli and Alexander McQueen (brand) before participating in the Israeli TV show Project Runway Israel, a variation on the American Project Runway. 1

Livne started his self-titled ready-to-wear line that was a global hit, gaining him a following of celebrity fashion trend-setters such as Lady Gaga, Naomi Campbell, Kim Kardashian and Paris Hilton. In 2013 Livne showed his work at Mercedes-Benz New York Fashion Week where he presented his Fall-Winter 2013 collection at NY Lincoln center, he's been showing his collections there up until the summer of 2016. 2

In May 2013, Beyonce commissioned Alon Livne to design Gowns for her world-tour The Mrs. Carter Show World Tour performing All over the globe. The dress was featured in magazines worldwide. 3

Livne's Spring-Summer 2017 collection was presented in Paris during the official schedule of Paris Fashion Week, making his brand the first Israeli brand to ever show on schedule. The collection was positively reviewed by international press 4

Alon Livne White

Alon Livne's bridal line, "Alon Livne White", is sold worldwide and is greatly praised by bridal fashion editors and magazines. A presentation of the collection is held bi-annually dring Bridal Fashion Week in New York City. Livne's 2017 bridal collection, shown in October 2017, was his most covered collection to date and was featured in a great range of publications such as Vogue Paris, Cosmopolitan (magazine), Brides Magazine and more... 5
