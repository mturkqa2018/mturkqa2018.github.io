Verteillac is a commune in the Dordogne department in Nouvelle-Aquitaine in south-western France. In 2014, the feast of Félibrée was held in Verteillac. 1

Shopping

Verteillac is a local shopping centre for the nearby villages. It has a general store (Merlaud, which sells everything from lettuce to lawn mowers), butchers, bakers, a brocante, a choice of cafes and restaurants. 3

Personalities

  • Martine Aurillac, députée for Paris, was member of the municipal council of Verteillac from 1971 until 1977.
