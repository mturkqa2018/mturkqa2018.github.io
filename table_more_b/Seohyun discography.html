Seohyun (born Seo Ju-hyun) is a South Korean singer, member of Girls' Generation and its subgroup Girls' Generation-TTS. Since debuting in 2007, she has collaborated with various artists and released several original soundtracks for South Korean TV series. Her career as a solo singer began in January 2017 with the release of her debut extended play Don't Say No. 1
