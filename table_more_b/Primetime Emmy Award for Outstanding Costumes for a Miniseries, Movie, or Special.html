Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Costumes for a Miniseries, Movie, or Special

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Costumes for a Miniseries, Movie, or Special is a retired award that was presented as part of the Primetime Emmy Awards. Until 1999, the category was divided to award costume designers and costume supervisors separately. 1

In 2015, this category and Outstanding Costumes for a Series were rearranged as Outstanding Costumes for a Period/Fantasy Series, Limited Series, or Movie and Outstanding Costumes for a Contemporary Series, Limited Series, or Movie. 2
