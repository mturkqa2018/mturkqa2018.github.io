Ithu Pathiramanal (English: Sands of Night) is a 2013 Malayalam action film directed by M. Padmakumar starring Unni Mukundan in the lead role with Jayasurya and Ramya Nambeeshan . The shooting locations are in Marayur, Pathiramanal and Alapuzha. 2

Plot

The story is about a private financer Eldho (Unni Mukundan), who lives in a hill station with his mother. His mother had become mentally challenged after she was raped by a police constable (Pradeep Rawat) during Eldho's childhood. His mother conceived from the rape and gave birth to a baby girl. Johnkutty (Jayasurya), Eldho's father is avenging his wife's rape by going after the police constable and unfortunately dies. 3

After Eldho has grown up, he reaches Pathiramanal, a place in Kuttanad, in search of the constable to take revenge. There he meets Sara. He realizes that Sara is the daughter of the constable. 4

Production

The shoot of the film started at Alappuzha in May. The shooting was stopped for a while because of lead actor Jayasurya's injury during Vaadhyar film action sequence. Jayasurya was doing both the role of father and son in the film. But due to his injury, the son's role was filled by Unni Mukundan. 5
