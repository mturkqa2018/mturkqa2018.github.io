The Midland Regional Hockey Association is the organising body for field hockey in the Midlands, England. It feeds teams into the Men's and Women's England Hockey Leagues and receives teams from regional and county leagues. 1

League structure

The region has separate men's league structures for 1st, 2nd and 3rd XIs, and an open Central League for 4th XIs and below. The champions of the Premier Division enter the Men's and Women's England Hockey League Conference play-offs. 2

The league covers the counties of Derbyshire, Leicestershire and Rutland, Northamptonshire, Nottinghamshire, Shropshire, Staffordshire, Warwickshire and Worcestershire. 3
