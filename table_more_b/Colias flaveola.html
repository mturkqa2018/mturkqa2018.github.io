Colias flaveola is a butterfly in the family Pieridae. It is found in the Tropical Andes subregion of the Neotropical ecozone. 2

Description

Colias flaveola is pale orange yellow in the female, in the rather broad dark distal margin of the forewing are placed four large yellowish-white subapical spots, the under surface has dull sulphur-yellow ground colour. Deeper orange-yellow coloured female specimens are common. 3
