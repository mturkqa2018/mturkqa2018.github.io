The Roman Catholic Diocese of Caxito (Latin: Dioecesis Caxitonsis) is a diocese located in the city of Caxito in the Ecclesiastical province of Luanda in Angola. 1

History

  • June 6, 2007: Established as Diocese of Caxito from the Metropolitan Archdiocese of Luanda

Special churches

The Cathedral of the diocese is Catedral Sant’Ana in Caxito. 2
