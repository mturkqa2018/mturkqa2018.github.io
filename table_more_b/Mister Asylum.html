Mister Asylum is the debut album by American rock trio Highly Suspect, released on July 17, 2015. It features the singles "Lydia" and "Bloodfeather". 1

Reception

The album debuted at No. 56 on Billboard 200, and No. 7 on the Top Rock Albums chart, selling 4,000 copies in its first week. It has sold 58,000 copies as of November 2016. 2
