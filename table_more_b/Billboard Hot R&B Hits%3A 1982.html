Billboard Hot R&B Hits: 1982 is a compilation album released by Rhino Records in 1996, featuring 10 hit rhythm and blues recordings from 1982. 1

All tracks on the album were hits on Billboard's Hot Black Singles chart. In addition, several of the songs were mainstream hits, charting on the Billboard Hot 100 during 1982 and early 1983. One of those mainstream hits reached No. 1 on the Hot 100: "I Can't Go for That (No Can Do)" by Hall & Oates. 2
