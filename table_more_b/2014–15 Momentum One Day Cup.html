The 2014â€“15 Momentum One Day Cup was a domestic one-day cricket championship in South Africa. It was the 34th time the championship was contested. The competition started on 10 October 2014 and the final took place on 13 February 2015. The Titans defeated the Cape Cobras in the final, which was played at Newlands Cricket Ground in Cape Town. 1
