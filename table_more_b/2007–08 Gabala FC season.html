The Gabala FC 2007-08 season was Gabala FC's second Azerbaijan Premier League season, and their second season under manager Ramiz Mammadov. They finished the season in 6th place, whilst they also took part in the 2007â€“08 Azerbaijan Cup, which they were knocked out of in the Semifinals by Khazar Lankaran. 2
