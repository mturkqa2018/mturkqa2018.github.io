Biography

Mos debuted at 16 as a model for a magazine by Poj Anon in 1990, but has become mainly known as an actor and a singer. He has a twin brother name "Aon Dettarn Pataweekarn". He became the rising star when he played as "Peerapol" in the high-rating "3 Num 3 Mum" sitcom (EXACT, GMM Grammy) together with Songsit Rungnopakunsi (Kob) and Saksit Tangtong (Tang) in 1991. A year later, he released his debut album, "Ur Hur". Mos' second album, "Mr. Mos" became his most successful album ever with the sale amount of 1.5 million copies. So far, he's released 8 albums (with another 10 special albums). He starred in Exact's musical for the first time in "Bangkok 2485". Afterthat, Mos did several dramas including the acclaimed "Hua Jai Chocolate", "Duay Rang Hang Ruk", "Ubattihet Hua Jai", etc. Latest he's been honourly given the leading role in "Fah Jarod Sai-The Musical". Now Mos plays the police officer in Exact-Scenario's new sitcom called "Poo Kong Jao Sa-nae", and he's also hosting the 3 Num 3 Mum Variety Show with Kob and Tang. 2
