The 2014 Women's U22 South American Volleyball Championship was the 1st edition of the tournament, organised by South America's governing volleyball body, the Confederación Sudamericana de Voleibol (CSV). The championship took place 20–24 August in Popayán, Colombia. The event served as a rating for the 2015 FIVB Women's U23 Volleyball World Championship. 1

Competition format

The 2014 Women's U22 South American Volleyball Championship consisted of a single round-robin pool between the six teams, the champion was determined from the ranking after the round. 3
