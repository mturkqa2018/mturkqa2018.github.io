Scott Thwaites (born 12 February 1990 in Burley in Wharfedale) is a British cyclist, who currently rides for UCI Continental team Vitus Pro Cycling Team. He represented England in the 2014 Commonwealth Games road race and won the bronze medal. He was named in the startlist for the 2016 Vuelta a EspaÃ±a. In September 2016 Team Dimension Data confirmed that Thwaites would join them for 2017. In June 2017, he was named in the startlist for the 2017 Tour de France. 3
