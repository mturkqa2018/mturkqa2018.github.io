We Will All Evolve is the second album by New Hampshire-based band, Our Last Night. It was released on Epitaph Records. It was released on May 4, 2010. On March 23, the band changed their MySpace layout to reveal the album cover and they also added a new song titled "Elephants" along with the lyrics. On April 7, a second song, titled "Across the Ocean", was released on AbsolutePunk. On April 13, the new album "We Will All Evolve" was leaked on to the Internet, which was then confirmed by Trevor Wentworth on Twitter. 1
