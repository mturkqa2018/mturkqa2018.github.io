Intrakat, one of the top five construction companies in Greece, established in 1987, with a steadily growing international presence, leverages its specialized knowhow and long experience in delivering complex large-scale construction projects, including civil engineering, infrastructure, telecom network, renewable energy, environmental and real estate development projects in both the public and private sectors. 1

Aiming to effectively compete in a highly challenging business environment, the company has adopted a growth plan focused on EU-funded and PPP financed national and regional public works, wind farm design and construction, telecommunications and gas networks, as well as new waste management and other environmental technology application projects. 2

Following the successful delivery of Paros and Karpathos airports (airport terminal, airside), Intrakat has been awarded in March 2017 by Fraport Greece as the contractor to design and built all the refurbishment, remodelling and expansion works in 14 peripheral airports in Greece. 3

Intrakat is a member of the Intracom Holdings Group, one of the largest multinational technology groups in S.E Europe. 4
