Olearia paniculata, commonly called akiraho, is a species of shrub or tree in the Asteraceae family, found only in New Zealand. The tree can grow to 7.5 metres high, and has yellow-green, oval-shaped leaves, with white undersides and wavy margins. 3

O. paniculata produces clusters of daisy flowers in late autumn. 4
