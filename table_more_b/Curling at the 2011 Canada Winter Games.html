Curling at the 2011 Canada Winter Games took place at the Mayflower Curling Club in Halifax, Nova Scotia. 1

The women's competition was held from February 13–18, ending with a win by British Columbia's Corryn Cecile Brown in a 3-1 victory over Alberta's Jocelyn Peterman. Ontario's Laura Horton won the bronze medal after defeating host Nova Scotia's Emily Dwyer. The men's competition, held from February 21–26, ended with a close 6-5 win by Ontario's Ben Bevan over Saskatchewan's Brady Scharback, while Manitoba's Kyle Doering secured a win over New Brunswick's Scott Babin. 2
