The 2015 Swiss Indoors was a tennis tournament played on indoor hard courts. It was the 46th edition of the event, and part of the 500 series of the 2015 ATP World Tour. It was held at the St. Jakobshalle in Basel, Switzerland, from October 26 through November 1, 2015. 1
