WLIB AM: King of the Wigflip is a studio album by American hip hop musician Madlib. It was released on BBE in CD format as part of their Beat Generation Series. 1

Critical reception

At Metacritic, which assigns a weighted average score out of 100 to reviews from mainstream critics, the album received an average score of 72% based on 13 reviews, indicating "generally favorable reviews". 2
