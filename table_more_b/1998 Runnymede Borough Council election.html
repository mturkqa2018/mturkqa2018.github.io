1998 Runnymede Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Elections to Runnymede Council were held on 7 May 1998. One third of the council was up for election and the Conservative party gained overall control of the council from no overall control. 1

After the election, the composition of the council was 2

  • Conservative 23
  • Labour 12
  • Independent 5
  • Liberal Democrat 1
  • Residents Association 1
