2006 Runnymede Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

1

Elections to Runnymede Council were held on 4 May 2006. One third of the council was up for election and the Conservative party stayed in overall control of the council. 2

After the election, the composition of the council was 3

  • Conservative 36
  • Runnymede Residents Association 6
