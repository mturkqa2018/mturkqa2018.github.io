The 2012 Omloop Het Nieuwsblad took place on 25 February 2012. It was the 67th edition of the international classic Omloop Het Nieuwsblad. This year's Omloop started and ended at St. Peter's Square in Ghent, Belgium and spanned 200Â km (124.3Â mi) in the province of East Flanders. The race was the first 1.HC event in the 2012 UCI Europe Tour. 1
