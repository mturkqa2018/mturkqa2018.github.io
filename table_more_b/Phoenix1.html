Phoenix1 was a professional esports organization owned by Rob Moore. Its League of Legends team competed in the North American League of Legends Championship Series (NA LCS). The organization was formed after the Moore's purchase of Team Impulse's LCS spot. On June 7, 2018, Phoenix1 rebranded to Sentinels. 2
