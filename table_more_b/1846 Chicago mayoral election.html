In the Chicago mayoral election of 1846 Whig candidate John P. Chapin defeated Democratic candidate Philo Carpenter and Liberty candidate John H. Kinzie by a 22 point margin. 3

Campaign

This section needs expansion. You can help by adding to it. (February 2019)

Follansbee failed to garner the support of Irish Democrats, an important constituency for the Democratic Party in Chicago. These voters rejected him because he was a champion of the "Native American Act", which would require a period of 21 years of residency before any immigrant could become a naturalized citizen. 4
