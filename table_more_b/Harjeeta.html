Harjeeta is a hockey based drama film, inspired from true stories Punjabi film directed by Vijay Kumar Arora and starring Ammy Virk and Pankaj Tripathi with a release date of 18 May 2018. 2

Plot

Raised in a poor, dysfunctional home, a young man Harjeet Singh (Ammy Virk) dreams of winning the World Cup in field hockey. 3
