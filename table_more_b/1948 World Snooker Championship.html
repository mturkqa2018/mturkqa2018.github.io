The 1948 World Snooker Championship was a professional snooker tournament. The final was held at the Leicester Square Hall in London, England from 19 April to 1 May. 1

For the second year running the final was contested by Fred Davis and Walter Donaldson. Davis won his first World title by defeating Donaldson 81â€“64 in the final, although he reached the winning margin already at 73â€“52. Davis also made the highest break of the tournament with 109. 2
