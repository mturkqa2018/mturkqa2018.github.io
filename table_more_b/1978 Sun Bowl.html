The 1978 Sun Bowl was a college football bowl game that featured the Texas Longhorns and the Maryland Terrapins. 1

Background

The Longhorns went from being one game away from a title the previous year to finishing second in the Southwest Conference. The Terrapins finished second in the Atlantic Coast Conference and made their sixth straight bowl appearance. This was the first Sun Bowl for both teams. 2

In the third quarter, a 16-yard punt by the Terrapins set up another Longhorn opportunity. A.J. Jones added in his second touchdown of the day to make it 35â€“0. A Johnnie Johnson interception gave the ball back to the Longhorns again. Ham Jones scored from 35 yards out to make it 42â€“0 two plays later. Aftermath

The Terps returned to the Sun Bowl in 1984, while the Longhorns returned in 1979. 4
