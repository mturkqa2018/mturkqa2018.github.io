Walking with Thee is the second studio album by British indie rock band Clinic. It was released on 25 February 2002 on Domino Records. 1

Walking with Thee was included in Rolling Stone's "50 Best Albums of 2002". It was nominated for the Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album in 2003, losing out to Coldplay's A Rush of Blood to the Head. 2
