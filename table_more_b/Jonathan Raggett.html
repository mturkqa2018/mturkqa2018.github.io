Jonathan Raggett (born 9 November 1992) is a British-Chinese actor. His notable roles include Wolfblood and The Lady (2011 film). 1

Career

He began his professional acting career in 2011 in a film called The Lady and with a guest-starring appearance in Dani's House. He and Amanda Donohoe were both cast in the 2013 film Trafficker. He was featured in a commercial for Nintendo DS. He gained fame for his role as Jimi in the series Wolfblood. He landed a starring role in 2015 horror film Strangers Within. 2
