Jude Weng (Chinese name: Weng Fei-fei/"ç¿è²è²") is a Taiwanese-born American television writer, producer, and director of multi and single-camera half-hour comedies, and one-hour dramas. She has directed a variety of shows on American broadcast networks, premium cable channels and streaming services such as Black-ish (ABC), The Good Place (NBC), iZombie (The CW), Crashing (HBO), and Unbreakable Kimmy Schmidt (Netflix), as well as multiple episodes of Fresh Off the Boat (ABC), Crazy Ex-Girlfriend (The CW), Life in Pieces (CBS), and Young Sheldon (CBS). 1
