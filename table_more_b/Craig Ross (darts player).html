Craig Ross (born 5 October 1970) is an professional darts player from New Zealand, Who currently competes in the Professional Darts Corporation events. His the nickname The Rock. 1

He qualified for the 2019 PDC World Darts Championship, replacing Tahuna Irwin, who had beaten him in the DPNZ Qualifier, but was unable to travel to the UK, owing to visa problems. Ross played Toni Alcinas of Spain in the first round, but only won one leg, and was easily defeated 3â€“0. 2
