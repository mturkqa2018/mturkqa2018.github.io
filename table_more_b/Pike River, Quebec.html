Pike River is a municipality in Brome-Missisquoi Regional County Municipality in the Montérégie region of Quebec, Canada. The population as of the Canada 2011 Census was 525. 1

Until May 5, 2012 it was known as Saint-Pierre-de-Véronne-à-Pike-River. 2
