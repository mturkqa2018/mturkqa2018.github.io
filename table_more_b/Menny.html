Manuel Alejandro Carrasco de la O, (b. January 21, 1983, Chihuahua, Mexico) commonly known as Menny, is a Mexican singer / songwriter, and former contestant of the popular Mexican reality television series La Academia and Segunda Oportunidad. 2
