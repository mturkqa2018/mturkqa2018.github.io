Liga Deportiva Universitaria de Cuenca is a football club based in Cuenca, Ecuador currently in the Second Category Ecuadorian football Championship. 1

Honors

  • Championship Champion Provincial Azuay (4): 2004, 2005, 2007, 2010
  • Serie B (1): 1977 -E1
  • Runner up of the Series B of Ecuador (1): 1980 -E1
  • Runner up Second Category (2): 1986, 1988
