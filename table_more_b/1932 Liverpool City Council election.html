1932 Liverpool City Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

1

Elections to Liverpool City Council were held on 1 November 1932. One third of the council seats were up for election, the term of office of each councillor being three years. 2

Six of the forty seats up for election were uncontested. 3
