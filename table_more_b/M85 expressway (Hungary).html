The M85 expressway (Hungarian: M85-ös autóút) is a Hungarian expressway connecting Győr to Sopron. It will eventually connect M1 motorway to the Austrian border. 2

Municipalities

The M85 expressway runs through the following municipalities: 3

  • Ikrény, Rábapatona, Enese, Bezi, Kóny, Barbacs, Csorna, Farád

Openings timeline

  • Győr; M1 – Enese (8 km): 2015.06.16.
  • Enese bypass (7 km): 2011.12.21.
  • Enese – Kóny (3 km): 2015.06.16.
  • Kóny – Csorna (6 km): 2015.09.09.
  • Csorna bypass; I.section (east) (5 km): 2015.09.09.; (this section was extended 2x3 lane)
  • Csorna bypass; II.section (west) (4.5 km): 2017.12.15.
