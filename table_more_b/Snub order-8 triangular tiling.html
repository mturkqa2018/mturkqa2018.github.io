In geometry, the snub tritetratrigonal tiling or snub order-8 triangular tiling is a uniform tiling of the hyperbolic plane. It has Schläfli symbols of s{(3,4,3)} and s{3,8}. 1

Symmetry

The alternated construction from the truncated order-8 triangular tiling has 2 colors of triangles and achiral symmetry. It has Schläfli symbol of s{3,8}. 3
