Joshua Thomas "Josh" Vanlandingham (born January 22, 1984) is an American-born Filipino-German professional basketball player. He last played for the Alaska Aces of the Philippine Basketball Association (PBA). Born in Snohomish, Washington, he was drafted fifth overall in the 2010 PBA Draft. 1
