History

1

The Miss Russia crown

The pageant has been running since 1992; however, there was no Miss Russia 1994, 2000 or 2008. 2

Since 2007, the winner of Miss Russia represents her country in both Miss World and Miss Universe. However, if she is unable to participate, a runner-up is sent instead. 3

Russia has won three major international titles: Miss World 1992, Miss Universe 2002, although later dethroned, and Miss World 2008. 4

It is estimated that every Miss Russia auditions over 80,000-100,000 applicants are being processed annually. 5

The following women have represented Russia under Miss Russia Organization in the following international beauty pageants: Miss Universe and Miss World. Traditionally, winning Miss Russia gives titleholders the right to represent Russia at both Miss World and Miss Universe. However, since 2015, the winner of Miss Russia has been sent to Miss World, while the first runner-up has been sent to Miss Universe due to the dates of the two pageants often conflicting with each other. 20
