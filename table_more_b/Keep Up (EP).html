Keep Up is the debut extended play by English YouTube personality and rapper KSI. It was released on 8 January 2016, through Island Records. The EP features guest appearances by Jack Pook, Tiggs da Author, Sway, Randolph, and others. The EP's title track was brought forward to 13 November 2015 due to its appearance on the compilation album Now 92. The EP peaked at number one on the UK R&B Albums Chart, number 13 on the UK Albums Chart, and number 15 on the Scottish Albums Chart. 1
