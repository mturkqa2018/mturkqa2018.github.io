The Oats Park Grammar School, at 167 E. Park St. in Fallon, Nevada, is a historic school built in 1914. It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1990. 1

It was designed in 1914 by noted Nevada architect Frederick J. DeLongchamps, who in 1920 designed an addition as well; the school and addition were built in 1915 and 1921. It was deemed significant for its association with education in the state and for association with DeLongchamps. 2

It has also been known as Oats Park School and as Fallon Grammar School. 3
