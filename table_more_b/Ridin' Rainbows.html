Ridin' Rainbows was the seventh album by Tanya Tucker. Hit singles released from the album, and their Billboard Country Singles positions were, in chronological order: "Ridin' Rainbows" at #12, "It's a Cowboy Lovin' Night" at #7, and "Dancing the Night Away" at #16. The album was also at #16 on the Country Albums chart. 1
