Federal Route 105, or Jalan Lapangan Terbang Langkawi 1, is a major federal road in Langkawi Island, Kedah, Malaysia. 2

Features

At most sections, the Federal Route 105 was built under the JKR R5 road standard, allowing maximum speed limit of up to 90Â km/h. 3
