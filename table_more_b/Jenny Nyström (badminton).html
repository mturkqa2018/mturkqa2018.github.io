Jenny NystrÃ¶m (born 2 February 1994) is a Finnish female badminton player, specializing in doubles play. She started playing badminton at 6 years old, then in 2009, she joined Finnish national badminton team. 1

In 2013, she won Estonian International and Irish International tournaments in mixed doubles event with her partner Anton Kaisti. In 2015, she became the runner-up of Hellas International in women's doubles event with Mathilda Lindholm. In 2016, she won Hellas International in women's doubles and runner-up in mixed doubles. 2
