Maurice I (German: Moritz I.; c. 1145 â€“ c. 1211) was Count of Oldenburg from 1169 through 1211. Count Maurice of Oldenburg was the son of Count Christian I of Oldenburg and his wife Kunigunde. 2

He married Salome, the daughter of Otto II, Count of Wickrath. He is the male-line progenitor of Margrethe II of Denmark, Harald V of Norway, Constantine II of Greece and Charles, Prince of Wales. 3

Issue

  • Christian II (Count of Oldenburg 1211-1233)
  • Otto I (Count of Oldenburg 1209-1251)
