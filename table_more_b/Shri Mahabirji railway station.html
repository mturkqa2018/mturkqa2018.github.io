Shri Mahabirji (SMBJ) is a railway station on the West Central railway network at Hindaun Tehsil in India. It comes under the Kota Railway division of West Central Railway zone. This is a C-Grade station on the Delhi-Mumbai route. It serves Hindaun city. The station consists of three platforms. The platforms are not well sheltered. It lacks many facilities including water and sanitation. 2
