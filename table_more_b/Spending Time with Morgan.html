Spending Time with Morgan is the debut studio album by Norwegian singer-songwriter Ane Brun, released on 25 May 2003. Morgan is the nickname of Ane's guitar at the time, though he has since been retired - according to Ane herself on stage in Paris on 19 April 2008. 1
