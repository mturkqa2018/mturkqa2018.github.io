History

Ogoso Station was opened on June 22, 1922 as a station on the Mie Railway. The station was closed in January 1944, and reopened in May 1959 as a station on the Mie Kotsu, which later became the Mie Electric Railway, and which merged with Kintetsu on April 1, 1965. 3
