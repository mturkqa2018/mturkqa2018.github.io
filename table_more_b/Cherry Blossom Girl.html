"Cherry Blossom Girl" is a song by French electronic duo Air. It was released in February 2004 by record label Virgin/Astralwerks as the first single for their 2004 album Talkie Walkie. 1

Content

The song is the album's second track. Written in C-sharp minor, it opens up with bowed vibraphone, drum machines and fingerpicked acoustic guitar, which are soon joined by synthesizers and flute. The lyrics are sung by Jean-BenoÃ®t Dunckel. 2

The music video was directed by adult film director Kris Kramski. 3
