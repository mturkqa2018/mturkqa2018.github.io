Jang Hyung-Seok (born 7 July 1972) is a South Korean football player. 1

He played for several clubs, including Ulsan Hyundai Horang-i, Anyang LG Cheetahs and Bucheon SK. 2

He played for the South Korea national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup. 3
