The Battle for the Brice-Cowell Musket is the name of the rivalry between the Maine Black Bears and the New Hampshire Wildcats. Both schools are members of the Colonial Athletic Association. The two teams have met 108 times on the football field, with New Hampshire currently holding a 56–44–8 edge in the all-time series. 1
