Honorific and titles

When the son of one of the consorts ascended the throne she became Valide Hatun (Mother of Sultan). 2

Consequently, the title "Valide Hatun" also turned into "Valide Sultan". In this time, the title "Haseki Sultan" was created and used for the legal wife or Chief Consort of the Ottoman Sultan.
