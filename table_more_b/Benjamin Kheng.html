Benjamin Kheng (born August 15, 1990) is a Singaporean actor and singer. He is one of the members of Singaporean band The Sam Willows. 1

Career

Benjamin is the keyboardist, rhythm guitarist and vocalist for the Sam Willows. 2

Personal life

He was a former national youth swimmer, having trained since he was 6. He attended Anglo-Chinese School (Primary), Anglo-Chinese School (Independent), Singapore Sports School and has a diploma in Arts and Theatre Management from Republic Polytechnic. Benjamin is the older brother of The Sam Willow's Narelle Kheng. 3
