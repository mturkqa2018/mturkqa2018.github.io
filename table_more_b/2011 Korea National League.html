The 2011 Korea National League season was the ninth season of the Korea National League. The postseason playoff's teams were expanded by 6 teams and the playoffs were operated in the same format as the K-League Championship. In the 2011 season, Yesan FC withdrew from the league due to financial difficulties. 1

Awards

Most Valuable Player

  • The Most Valuable Player award was won by Cho Sung-Won (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin)

Top Scorer

  • The Top Scorer award was won by Danilo Teixeira (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin)

Top Assistor

  • The Top Assistor award was won by Lee Sang-Woo (Goyang KB)

Best XI

  • Goalkeeper: Woo Je-Myeong (Incheon Korail)
  • Defence: Kim Hyo-Joon, Lee Sang-Woo (both Goyang KB), Ahn Seon-Tae (Daejeon KHNP), Cho Sung-Won (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin)
  • Midfield: Kim Won-Min (Gimhae City), Park Seung-Min (Busan Transportation Corporation), Jeong Seon-Ho (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin), Lim Jong-Wook (Changwon City)
  • Attack: Hong Hyung-Gi (Daejeon KHNP), Alex (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin)

Manager of the Year

  • The Manager of the Year award was won by Cho Min-Kook (Ulsan Hyundai Mipo Dolphin)

Club of the Year

  • The Club of the Year award was won by Suwon City

Fair Play Award

  • The Fair Play award was won by Incheon Korail
