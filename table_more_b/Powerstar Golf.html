Gameplay

Powerstar Golf plays similar to other video game golf games although the game does offer a few unique features: Every character in the game has a unique special move that enables him or her to perform better than they normally would. The game also features a caddy system, and caddies also have unique move sets that players can use to their advantage. The game also uses an RPG-like experience points system where players can earn points to level up their characters by performing small challenges on the course. The game also has an asynchronous multiplayer mode that allows players to play against ghost versions of players on the leaderboard and players can earn additional XP if their "ghost" beats another player who challenges them. 2

The accompanying image provides a list of all the clubs and balls available in the game. Different type clubs and balls with the same name form a set which, when complete, provide an additional stat bonus when equipped. These sets can be made up of clubs and balls from different tiers. 3

The next image lists the six caddy perks available in the game. Each perk has three possible levels and are acquired from card packs. A caddy has three perk slots which can be unlocked and can have any three unique perks assigned to them. The two available caddies can have the same perks assigned at the same time.5

Once you have gotten all the clubs, balls, caddie perks, and skins available in a particular class (amateur, pro, elite, extreme), the in-store card pack will show a red banner which reads "Boosters Only". 6
