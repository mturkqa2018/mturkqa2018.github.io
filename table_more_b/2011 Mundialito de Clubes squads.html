These are the rosters of the ten participating teams at the inaugural Mundialito de Clubes (or Club World Cup) beach soccer tournament that will take place at a temporary beach soccer stadium at Represa de Guarapiranga, a reservoir located in São Paulo, Brazil from 19–26 March 2011. The first nine were drafted at the Official Draft, with the tenth player added by Beach Soccer Worldwide 48 hours later. 1
