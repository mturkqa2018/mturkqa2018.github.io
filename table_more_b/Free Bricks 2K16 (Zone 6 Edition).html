Free Bricks 2: Zone 6 Edition is a collaborative EP by American rappers Gucci Mane and Future. It was released on November 14, 2016. The EP features production by Southside, London on da Track, Metro Boomin and Zaytoven. It is a follow up to their 2011 mixtape Free Bricks. 1
