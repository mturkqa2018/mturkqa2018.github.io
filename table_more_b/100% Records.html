100% Records is a British, London-based, independent record label and product management business founded by Toby Harris, formerly of Back Yard Recordings. 3

100% have released studio albums by Orchestral Manoeuvres in the Dark, Killa Kela, Matisyahu, Monarchy, Skunk Anansie, Izabo, Sam Roberts and We Are Scientists. 4
