Aitor Iturrioz (born Aitor Antonio Iturrioz Ortiz on November 12, 1970) is a s a well-known Mexican actor. He is best known for his roles in the Mexican telenovela Clase 406 as Max Brouer, in Rebelde as Esteban Nolasco, and in Al Diablo con los Guapos as the antagonist Mateo. 1

Aitor Iturrioz is of Basque descent, Mother From Madrid, Father From Bilbao. 2
