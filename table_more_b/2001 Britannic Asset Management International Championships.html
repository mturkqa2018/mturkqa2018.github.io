The 2001 Britannic Asset Management International Championships was a women's tennis tournament played on grass courts at the Eastbourne Tennis Centre in Eastbourne in the United Kingdom that was part of Tier II of the 2001 WTA Tour. It was the 27th edition of the tournament and was held from 18 June through 24 June 2001. Lindsay Davenport won the singles title. 1
