Prince Antoine of Bourbon-Two Sicilies (French: Antoine Marie Joseph Alphonse Adam et omnes sancti; born 20 January 1929) is a member of the House of Bourbon-Two Sicilies which ruled the defunct Kingdom of the Two Sicilies until 1860. By profession he is an engineer specializing in radar technology. 2

Family

Antoine was the only child of Prince Gabriel of the Two Sicilies by his first wife, Princess Malgorzata Izabella Czartoryska. Antoine is a male-line great-grandson of King Ferdinand II of the Two Sicilies and an elder half-brother of Princes Giovanni and Casimir of Bourbon-Two Sicilies. 3

Although his children were born in Germany, he later practiced his career in France, maintaining a family home in Seine-et-Marne. 4
