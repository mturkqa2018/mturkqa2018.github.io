1988 World Junior Championships in Athletics – Men's 200 metres

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The men's 200 metres event at the 1988 World Junior Championships in Athletics was held in Sudbury, Ontario, Canada, at Laurentian University Stadium on 29 and 30 July. 1
