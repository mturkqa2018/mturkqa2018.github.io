Nabiollah "Nabi" Sorouri (1933â€“2002) was an Iranian welterweight freestyle wrestler. He was a member of the national team between 1953 and 1960. He won the world title in 1957 and placed fourth at the 1956 Summer Olympics. At the 1965 FILA Wrestling World Championships Sorouri was the head coach of Iran national wrestling team. 2
