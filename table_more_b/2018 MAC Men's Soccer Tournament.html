The 2018 MAC Men's Soccer Tournament, was the 25th edition of the tournament. It determined the Mid-American Conference's automatic berth into the 2018 NCAA Division I Men's Soccer Championship. The tournament began on November 6 and concluded on November 11. 1

Akron won the MAC Championship, 3â€“0 against Western Michigan, making it a rematch of last year's championship. Akron entered the tournament as the six-time defending champions, and the fourth overall seed. 2

The tournament was expanded this year from four teams to six teams. 3
