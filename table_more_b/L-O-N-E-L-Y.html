"L-O-N-E-L-Y" is a song written and sung by Bobby Vinton, which he released in 1965. The song spent 8 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100 chart, peaking at No. 22, while reaching No. 7 on Billboard's Pop-Standards Singles chart, and No. 1 on Canada's "RPM Play Sheet". 1
