Big Brother Panam√° is the Panamanian version of the reality television franchise Big Brother. It is the first season of the show, and was started broadcasting on 31 August 2016 on TVN-2. Rolando Sterling and Gaby Garrido hosting the main shows. This season the grand prize is $25,000. 1

The house to be used for the first season is the Gran Hermano Argentina house. 2
