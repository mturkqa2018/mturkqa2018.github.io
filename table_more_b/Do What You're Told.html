"Do What You're Told" is the debut single from Swedish pop/rock and alternative rock artist Sebastian, released from his debut album "Sebastian" on February 1, 2006. The song was written by Peter Wallevik, Mikael Jakobsson and Tina Harris. It was a hit on Swedish radio and successful in the Swedish charts, staying in the #1 position for four weeks. 1

The song was released three months after the Idol 2005 final, where Sebastian came in second place after Agnes Carlsson. 2
