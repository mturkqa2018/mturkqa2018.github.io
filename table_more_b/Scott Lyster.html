Career

In 2010 he played Randy Jackson, sheriff's son, in Psych episode Dual Spires. In 2012, he starred as Richie in the indie film Locked in a Garage Band and in 2016 played Nelson in psychological horror film The Unseen. 2

He also portrayed Perry Dell in Hallmark Movies & Mysteries TV-films series The Aurora Teagarden Mysteries, based on Charlaine Harris crime novels. 3
