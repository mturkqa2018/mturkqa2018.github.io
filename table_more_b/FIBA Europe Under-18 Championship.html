The FIBA Europe Under-18 Championship, originally known as the European Championship for Juniors, is a youth men's basketball competition that was inaugurated in 1964. It was held biennially until 2002. From 2004 onward, it has been held every year. The current champion is Serbia. It was originally an Under-20-age tournament, but is now an Under-18-age tournament. 1
