Death

Cano died on December 7, 1983, in an airplane accident in Madrid, Spain. Her flight was an Aviaco DC-9 which was about to take off for Santander at the same time as an Iberia Airlines Boeing 727 was leaving to Rome. The aircraft collided with each other on the tarmac resulting in 93 deaths. She was killed in the fire after the crash. 2
