Jhimpir railway station (Urdu: جھمپیر ریلوے اسٹیشن‎, Sindhi: جھمپیر ریلوے اسٹیشن‎) is located at Jhimpir village, Thatta district of Sindh province, Pakistan, 114 km (71 mi) from Karachi. Jhimpir Wind Power Plant is Pakistan's first wind power plant. 1
