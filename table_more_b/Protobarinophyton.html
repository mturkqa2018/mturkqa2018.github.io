Protobarinophyton was a genus of Silu-Devonian land plant with branching axes. 2

A cladogram published in 2004 by Crane et al. places Protobarinophyton in the core of a paraphyletic stem group of broadly defined "zosterophylls", basal to the lycopsids (living and extinct clubmosses and relatives). 3
