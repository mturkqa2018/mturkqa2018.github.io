Cal is a soundtrack album by British singer-songwriter and guitarist Mark Knopfler, released on 24 August 1984 by Vertigo Records. The album contains music composed for the 1984 film Cal, produced by David Puttnam and directed by Pat O'Connor. Puttnam also produced the film Local Hero (1983). 1

Critical reception

Professional ratings
Review scores
SourceRating
AllMusic

In a contemporary review in The New York Times, Janet Maslin called the album "an exceptionally lovely and haunting score." 2

In a retrospective review for AllMusic, Steven McDonald gave the album four out of five stars and called it a "quiet, reflective set of cues that eschew false dramatics in favor of supporting the story." 3
