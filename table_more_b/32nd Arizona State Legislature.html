Sessions

The Legislature met for two regular sessions at the State Capitol in Phoenix. The first opened on January 13, 1975, and adjourned on June 13; while the second convened on January 12, 1976, and adjourned on June 24. There was a single Special Session, convened on January 12, 1976, and adjourned on March 1, 1976. 2
