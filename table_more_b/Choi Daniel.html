Choi Daniel (born 22 February 1986) is a South Korean actor, DJ and television anchorman. He is best known for his roles in Jugglers as Nam Chi Won, High Kick Through the Roof, Cyrano Agency, Baby Faced Beauty and School 2013. He is also an anchorman on KBS World Radio since 2011 and a DJ on KBS 2FM since 2013. 1
