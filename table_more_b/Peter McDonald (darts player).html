Peter McDonald (born 11 February 1965) is a retired Scottish professional darts player. 2

Darts Career

McDonald first qualified for the Embassy World Professional Darts Championship in 1987, losing in the first round 3-1 to Sweden's Lars Erik Karlsson. He returned two years later in 1989 but again lost in the first round, 3-0 to Mike Gregory. 3
