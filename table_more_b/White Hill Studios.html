White Hill Studios is a film studio in the Punjabi industry. The studio's activities range over production, creative development, distribution and marketing of films in Punjab and worldwide. 2

History

White Hill Productions was founded in 2012 by Gunbir Singh Sidhu and Manmord Sidhu with the release of Jatt & Juliet. After several more successful films, White Hill Productions opened their doors to their own music label called White Hill Music in October 2016 leading to the formation of White Hill Studios. 3

White Hill went on to produce Carry on Jatta 2, and has received the National Film Award in 2015 for Punjab 1984, a tale of a mother and her son during the time of turmoil in Punjab. 4
