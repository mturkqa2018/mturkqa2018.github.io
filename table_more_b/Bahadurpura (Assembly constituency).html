Bahadurpura Assembly constituency is a constituency of Telangana Legislative Assembly, India. It is one of 15 constituencies in Capital city of Hyderabad. It is part Hyderabad Lok Sabha constituency. Bahadur Pura was derived from the name Sarwar Ul Milk Bahadur who was the master of Salar Jung 1

Mohd. Moazam Khan of All India Majlis-e-Ittehadul Muslimeen won the seat for the second time with record majority of 95,045 votes. 2
