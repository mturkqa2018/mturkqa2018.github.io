Bangor University Football Club are a Welsh football club based in Bangor, Gwynedd. They currently compete in the Gwynedd League. 1

The club also competes in the British Universities and Colleges Sport (BUCS) competitions, and currently fields a First, second, third & Women's team in this competition. The first team compete in the BUCS Mars Northern 3A League after winning the 4A league in the 2012â€“13 season. The second & third team both compete in the BUCS Mars Northern 6C League. The Women's team now compete in the BUCS Mars Women's 3A league after winning the 3B league in the 2012â€“2013 season. 2

The club is the largest in the University's Athletic Union. 3

The club's crest and colours are those of the university 4
