The Croatia women's national under-18 volleyball team represents Croatia in international women's volleyball competitions and friendly matches under the age 18 and it is ruled by the Croatian Volleyball Federation That is an affiliate of Federation of International Volleyball FIVB and also a part of European Volleyball Confederation CEV. 1
