Kickboxing career

In 2004 GajoviÄ‡ was called to represent Serbia and Montenegro at the W.A.K.O. European championships held in his home country. He won the gold medal in the Full-Contact kickboxing heavyweight category (91 kg/200.2 lbs), defeating Anatoly Nossyrev in the final by majority decision. In 2006 he went to European Championships in Lisbon, Portugal making the semi finals and earning a bronze medal for his efforts. A year later he return to Portugal for the world championships, reaching the final but having to make do with a silver medal. 3
