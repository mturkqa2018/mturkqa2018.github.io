The seniors' division of the UAAP Season 76 volleyball tournaments opened December 1, 2013. Tournaments are hosted by Adamson University. Tournament games are held at the Filoil Flying V Arena in San Juan City, the Mall of Asia Arena in Pasay City and the Smart Araneta Coliseum in Quezon City. 1
