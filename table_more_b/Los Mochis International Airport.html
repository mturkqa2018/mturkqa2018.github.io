Valle del Fuerte Federal International Airport (Spanish: Aeropuerto Internacional Federal del Valle del Fuerte, IATA: LMM, ICAO: MMLM), commonly named Los Mochis International Airport (Aeropuerto Internacional de Los Mochis), is the airport serving Los Mochis, Sinaloa, Mexico. It comprises a terminal building with basic services and three commercial aircraft parking spaces. 1

It handled 280,166 passengers in 2017, and 273,068 passengers in 2018. 2
