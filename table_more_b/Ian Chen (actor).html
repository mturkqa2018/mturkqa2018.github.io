Ian Chen (born September 7, 2006) is an American actor. In 2014, he was cast as a series regular in the ABC comedy series Fresh Off the Boat. 1

Appearances

He is a lead character on the ABC series Fresh Off The Boat. 2

In November 2016, Chen opened the "FASO Goes Pops!" orchestra concert with a performance of the young Michael Jackson hit, "Ben." 3

In June 2016, he starred as the main character in Eric Nam's music video "Into You". In 2019, he co-starred in the live-action film Shazam!. 4

Personal life

Ian Chen has a younger brother, named Max, and both are fluent in Mandarin. He has shown enthusiasm for the sandbox videogame Minecraft, and also sings and plays guitar in a band. 5
