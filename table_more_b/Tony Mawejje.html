Anthony Mawejje Jr. (born 15 December 1990) is a Ugandan international footballer who plays as a midfielder for Tirana. 1

Club career

Mawejje previously signed a two-year contract with the Norwegian Tippeligaen club Haugesund ahead of the 2014 season. In July 2014, after only three appearances for Haugesund, Mawejje moved to Valur on loan. On 27 January 2015, Mawejje became a free agent. 2

Tirana

In July Mawejje joined Tirana in Albania and made his debut on 6 September 2017 in a win against FK KukÃ«si where he played the entire match. Tony scored his first goal for Tirana on 16 September 2017 against KS Iliria; Tirana won 1-0. 3
