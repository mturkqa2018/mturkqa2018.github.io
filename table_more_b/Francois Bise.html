Francois Bise is a French restaurateur. He elevated his wife's restaurant, three Michelin-starred French restaurant, Auberge du PÃ¨re Bise to a legendary status. 1

Life

He trained under the father of modern French cuisine, Fernand Point. 2

Auberge

Auberge began as a one-room restaurant in 1901 by his wife, Charlyne. He joined the restaurant later and elevated it to its legendary status. 3

He is married to Charlyne, who ran the restaurant after his death in 1983 from cancer. His daughter, Sophie Bise, is the patron chef of Auberge du PÃ¨re Bise. She won back the three star michelin status after his death, at a young age of 21. 4
