Kerakat is a constituency of the Uttar Pradesh Legislative Assembly covering the city of Kerakat in the Jaunpur district of Uttar Pradesh, India. 2

Kerakat is one of five assembly constituencies in the Machhlishahr (Lok Sabha constituency). Since 2008, this assembly constituency is numbered 372 amongst 403 constituencies. 3

As of now, this seat belongs to Bharatiya Janta Party candidate Dinesh Choudhary who won in 2017 Uttar Pradesh Legislative Elections by defeating Samajwadi Party candidate Sanjai Kumar Saroj by a margin of 15,259 votes. 4
