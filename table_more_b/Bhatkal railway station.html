Bhatkal railway station is a railway station in coastal Karnataka in South India. Its four-letter code is BTJL. 1

Background

Konkan Railway Academy(KRA) is situated here providing training to entire konkan railway employees. 2

Infrastructure

This station has two platforms and four tracks. 3

Lines

There is a single diesel broad gauge track. 4

Location

It lies off the Kanyakumari-Panvel Road, in Bhatkal, PIN Code 581320. The Mangalore Bajpe Airport (Code: IXE) is 125 km away. The station is located at an elevation of 15 metres above sea level. It lies within the KR/Konkan Zone under the Karwar Division. 5

Other stations

It falls under Karwar railway division of Konkan Railway zone, a subsidiary zone of Indian Railways. under the jurisdiction of Konkan Railway. Bhatkal has 3 stations viz Bhatkal, Chitrapur Halt, Murdeshwar 6
