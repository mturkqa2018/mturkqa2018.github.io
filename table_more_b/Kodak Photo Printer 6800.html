The Kodak Photo Printer 6800 is a model of computer printer manufactured by Eastman Kodak whose purpose can be inferred to be for printing 6-inch-by-8-inch photographs. According to Kodak's news release (as cited in the "References" section), the 6800 takes advantage of "thermal" printing technology. As of the 2004 Photo Marketing Association Annual Convention and Trade Show, the 6800 would be released in the second quarter of 2004, and carry a suggested list price in the United States of approximately 3,000 USD. 1
