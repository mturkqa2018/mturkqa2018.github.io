1987 Australian Football Championships

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 1987 Australian Football Championships was an Australian rules football series between representative teams of the three major football states. Games involving Victoria were played under State of Origin rules, whilst the match between Western Australia and South Australia involved players based in their respective states at the time. The competition was won by South Australia. 1
