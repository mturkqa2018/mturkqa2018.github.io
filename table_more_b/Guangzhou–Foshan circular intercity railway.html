Guangzhouâ€“Foshan circular intercity railway, also known as the Guangfo ring intercity railway, is a regional railway forming a ring around Guangzhou, Guangdong, China. It is not a separate project with no new trackage, but a collection of upgraded existing sections that are part of other railways surrounding Guangzhou. 1

Route

The western section of this ring railway runs from Guangzhou South to Guangzhou North along the southernmost section of the Wuhanâ€“Guangzhou high-speed railway. The northern section of this railway runs from Guangzhou North to Airport North along the Airport Branch of the Guangzhouâ€“Qingyuan intercity railway. The eastern section of this railway is from Airport North to Wanghong in Dongguan along the Guangzhouâ€“Dongguanâ€“Shenzhen intercity railway. The southern section completes this railway, connecting Wanghong back to Guangzhou South along the Foshanâ€“Dongguan intercity railway. 2

Stations

  • The line shares tracks with the Guangfozhao & Foguan intercity railways from Guangzhou South to Foshan West.
  • The line shares tracks with Suiguanshen intercity railway from Guangzhou North to Zhuliao.
  • The line shares tracks with Guangqing intercity railway from Guangzhou North to Guangzhou Airport Terminal 1.
