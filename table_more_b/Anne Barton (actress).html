Anne Barton (March 20, 1924 â€“ November 27, 2000) was an American actress. She appeared in the films Destination 60,000 (1957), Pawnee (1957), The Green-Eyed Blonde (1957), The Left Handed Gun (1958), The Comancheros (1961), What Ever Happened to Baby Jane? (1962), The Way West (1967) and The Great Northfield Minnesota Raid (1967), among others. 1

TV appearances included Leave It to Beaver, The Twilight Zone, Thriller, Perry Mason, and Death Valley Days. 2

She died on November 27, 2000, in Los Angeles, California at age 72. 3
