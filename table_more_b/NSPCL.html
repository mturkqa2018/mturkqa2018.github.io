NSPCL (NTPC-SAIL Power Company Limited) is a 50:50 joint venture company of NTPC Limited and SAIL, engaged in power generation primarily to meet the captive power requirement of various steel plants of SAIL throughout India. It is one of the institutional category III profit making Indian PSEs. 2

History

NTPC and SAIL joined forces in March 2001 and took over a captive power plant (consisting of 2x60MW generators) located at the Durgapur Steel Plant and another (also 2x60Â MW) at the Rourkela Steel Plant. 3

NTPC formed another joint venture company with SAIL on in March 2002 in the name of Bhilai Electric Supply Company Ltd. (BESCL). BESCL took over a captive power plant (comprising 2x 30Â MW generators and one 14MW back-power turbo generator) located at the Bhilai Steel Plant from SAIL. Effective 11 September 2006, BESCL became part of NSPCL. 4

Awards and recognition

  • NSPCL has been conferred with prestigious Global HR Excellence Awards for Best Corporate Social Responsibility Practices, Leading HR Practices in Quality Work-Life and Best Talent Management Award at the World HRD Congress.
  • NSPCL has also won the prestigious "SCOPE Award for Excellence and Outstanding Contribution to the Public Sector Management- Institutional Category-III for the year 2011-12.
  • NSPCL got Commendation Certificate for Institutional Category III (Other Profit Making PSEs) for the year 2013-14 at "SCOPE Award for Excellence and Outstanding Contribution to the Public Sector Management.
  • NSPCL has been ranked 81stamong India's Best Companies to Work For-Top 100 in Great Place to Work - 2013 Study.
  • NSPCL was recognized among 25 Best Companies to Work (up to 1000 employees category) for 2012 as per study by The Economic Times & Great Place to Work and Ranked 3rd in the Category of Manufacturing and Production sector.
  • NSPCL ranked 73rd in the Great Place to Work Study in the year 2011.
  • NSPCL was conferred Award for 'Outstanding & Noteworthy Accomplishments' in 9th India Power Award 2016 ceremony organised by Council of Power Utilities (CPU) on 25.11.2016 at New Delhi.
  • NSPCL got Special Recognition for a comprehensive and timely implementation of SAP Solution at the 4th Public Services ACE Awards held on 13th Aug'14.
  • NSPCL has been conferred the award for "Most Admired Organisation in Power Sector" by Asia Oil and Gas Congress under The National Energy Excellence Awards 2013. These awards recognize excellence and achievement across the electricity, oil, gas and petroleum industries.
