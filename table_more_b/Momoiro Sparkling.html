Background

The single was intended as a summer tune. It was released in three versions: Regular Edition (catalog number EPCE-5790), and Limited Editions A (EPCE-5786) and B (EPCE-5788) that included a bonus DVD. Limited Edition A DVD contained the "Momoiro Sparkling (Dance Shot Ver.)" music video, and Limited Edition B had "Momoiro Sparkling (Close-up Ver.)". The Single V appeared on June 1. 2
