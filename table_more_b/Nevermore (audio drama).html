Nevermore is an audio drama based on the British science fiction television series Doctor Who. This audio drama was produced by Big Finish Productions. 3

Plot

The Doctor and his new companion are dragged down to the planet Nevermore, a world themed after the works of Edgar Allan Poe and devised as a prison for one woman. 4

Cast Notes

  • Fenella Woolgar played Agatha Christie in the TV story The Unicorn and the Wasp. She was also in the audio story The Company of Friends.
  • Eric Loren played Mr Diagoras in the TV story Daleks in Manhattan / Evolution of the Daleks. He was also in the audio story Assassin in the Limelight.

Continuity

The identity of the renegade "strange little man" who set these events in motion is revealed in the next story, The Book of Kells. 5

Broadcast

Nevermore was broadcast on digital radio station BBC Radio 4 Extra on 9 Jan 2013 as a single hour-long episode. 6
