Biography

  • From June to August 2011 Miyazaki applied for the S/mileage Member BoshÅ« Audition and the Morning Musume Genki Jirushi Audition but were both failed. Her audition for S/mileage was left until its final review.
  • Miyazaki won the "Encouragement/Samantha Thavasa Award" in the 2nd Forest Award New Face Audition in March 2012. During the audition she played a piano cover of Ai Otsuka's "Planetarium". After winning the award Miyazaki received lessons in Tokyo every two weeks, and heard that she will debut on May.
  • In October 2012 she participated on the planning of the television series Hello! Satoyama Life. Miyazaki debuted as a member of the "Satoyama movement" group Green Fields. The group had performed at Morning Musume's concert on the 28 of the same month. This was Miyazaki's first stage performance.
  • It was announced that she and five Hello Pro Kenshusei members are members of a new group on the 3 February 2013, later on the 25 of the same month the group's name was revealed as Juice=Juice. Miyazaki is the oldest member of the group.
  • On 13 June 2013 she became the leader of Juice=Juice.
  • On December 21, she announced that she would graduate from Juice=Juice and Hello! Project at the end of Juice=Juice's 2019 spring tour.
