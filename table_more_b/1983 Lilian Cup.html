The 1983 Lilian Cup was the 2nd season of the competition. The four top placed teams for the previous season took part in the competition. 1

The competition was held in two stages. First, the four teams played a round-robin tournament, after which the two top teams played for the cup, while the bottom teams played for the third place. The competition was held between 3 September and 13 September 1983. 2

The competition was won by Maccabi Netanya, who had beaten Hapoel Be'er Sheva 3â€“2 in the final. 3
