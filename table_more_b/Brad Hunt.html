Brad Hunt is an American actor , singer and songwriter, born in Moberly, Missouri. 1

Filmography

  • Just Add Water (2008) as Denny
  • The Tripper (2006) as Hank
  • The Plague (2006) as Sam Raynor
  • The Darwin Awards (2006) as Stan
  • Lucky 13 (2005) as Zach Bader
  • Hart's War (2002) as Pvt. G.H. 'Cookie' Bell
  • Cherish (2002) as D.J.
  • The Journeyman (2001) as The Morphinist
  • Blow (2001) as GG
  • Magnolia (1999) as Craig Hansen
  • Dream with the Fishes (1997) as Nick
  • Fire Down Below (1997) as Orin Hanner Jr.
  • Mulholland Falls (1996) as Guard
  • Ed (1996) as Carnie
