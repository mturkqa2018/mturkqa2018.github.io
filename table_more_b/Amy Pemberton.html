Amy Louise Pemberton is a British actress whose career began in 2004. 2

She may be best known for voicing Gideon in the television series Legends of Tomorrow and Elaena Glenmore in the Game of Thrones video game. She also voiced Slone from Titanfall 2 (2016). She also played Private Sally Morgan, a companion of the Seventh Doctor, in Big Finish Productions' Doctor Who audio plays. 3

Personal life

Pemberton is from Stowmarket, Suffolk. Her father died in 2009. 4
