1982 Waltham Forest London Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

1

The 1982 Waltham Forest Council election took place on 6 May 1982 to elect members of Waltham Forest London Borough Council in London, England. The whole council was up for election and the Labour party stayed in overall control of the council. 2
