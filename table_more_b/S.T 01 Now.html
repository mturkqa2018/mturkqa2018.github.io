S.T 01 Now is South Korean boy band SS501's first full-length studio album, released on November 10, 2006 by DSP Media. 1

The album title was named after the combination of "S" from SS501, "T" from Triple S (SS501's official fanclub), and "01 NOW" which, when combined altogether, means "SS501 and Triple S united as one from now until forever". 2

Their first single track from the album is "Unlock", followed by the funky hip-hop rhythm "Four Chance", and bright and lively pop song "Coward". 3
