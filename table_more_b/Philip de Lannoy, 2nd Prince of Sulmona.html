Philip de Lannoy, 2nd Prince of Sulmona (4 November 1544 - 4 November 1561) was an Italian military leader in Spanish service. At the Battle of Ceresole, he commanded the Neapolitan light cavalry. 1

He was the son of Charles de Lannoy, 1st Prince of Sulmona. 2
