Xinrong (simplified Chinese: æ–°è£; traditional Chinese: æ–°æ¦®) is a station on Line 1 of Wuhan Metro, opened along with the completion of Line 1, Phase 2 on July 29, 2010. It is an elevated station situated on Jiefang Avenue and located at the intersection of Jiefang Avenue and Hanhuang Road, with easy access to Xinrongcun Long Distance Bus Terminal and downtown bus transfers. The station has two side platforms serving trains from each direction. 1

Exits

There are currently three exits in service: 3

  • Exit A: Northwest side of Jiefang Avenue. Accessible to Xinrongcun Long Distance Bus Terminal.
  • Exit B: Northwest side of Jiefang Avenue.
  • Exit D: Southeast side of Jiefang Avenue.

Transfers

Bus transfers to Route 3, 4, 211, 212, 232, 234, 509, 577, 615, 727 and 809 are available at Xinrong Station. 4
