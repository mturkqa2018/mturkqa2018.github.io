Julie Matteau (born April 10, 1977) is a Canadian ski mountaineer and member of the national selection. She lives in Revelstoke, British Columbia. 1

Selected results

  • 2010:
    • 7th, World Championship relay race (together with Melanie Bernier and Billie Velisek)
    • 9th, World Championship team race (together with Melanie Bernier)
  • 2011:
    • 9th, World Championship team race (together with Melanie Bernier)
  • 2012:
    • 5th, North American Championship, sprint
    • 6th, North American Championship, sprint
    • 6th, North American Championship, total ranking
