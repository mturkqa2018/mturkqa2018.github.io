Bijukuttan (born 24 December 1976) is an Indian film actor and comedian appearing in Malayalam films. 2

Personal life

Bijukuttan is born to Anandan and Chandrika at North Paravur, Ernakulam district. He had his primary education at S. N. M. High School, Moothakunnam, N. Paravur. He dropped studies at his tenth grade. Biju is married to Subitha. The couple have two daughtersLakshmi and Parvathi. 3

Career

In 2007, he acted in a supporting role alongside Mammootty in the Malayalam film Pothen Vava. It was a comedic role. In the same year, he acted in another full-length comedy role in the Mohanlal starrer Chotta Mumbai. 4
