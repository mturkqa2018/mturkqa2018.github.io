Jhutha Sach is a 1984 Bollywood film directed by Esmayeel Shroff and starring Dharmendra and Rekha. The film was remade in Telugu as Kodetrachu. It was the second movie for child actor Jugal Hansraj, after Masoom. 2

Cast

  • Dharmendra as Vijay/Tiger
  • Rekha as Alka
  • Amrish Puri as Kolga
  • Jugal Hansraj as Bhishan "Binny"
  • Asrani as Bhajanlal
  • Lalita Pawar as Governess
  • Aruna Irani as Bahar
  • Viju Khote as Custom Officer (song "Loot Gayi")
  • Manik Irani as Dharhu
  • Prema Narayan as Mrs. Prema Bhajanlal
