Martin Bergmann (born February 15, 1983) is a Model and TV personality born as one of two children near Munich, Germany . 1

Modeling

Bergmann is best known for his Lanvin advertisements in Europe as well as in Asia. He has also modeled for Chanel, Levi's, Louis Vuitton, Uniqlo, Theory, Guess, Gucci, Sisley and Henry Cotton's. He is signed to NEXT Model Management in New York, MGM Marilyn Paris, Modelwerk Germany, Fashion in Milano, Italy, Elite Model Management in Sweden, Elite Models in Copenhagen, Bravo Models in Tokyo, FORD in Brasil and METRO Models in Switzerland. 2

Other work

Bergmann is as well graduated in Communications Design and Fashion Photography. He as well practices this all around the world. 3
