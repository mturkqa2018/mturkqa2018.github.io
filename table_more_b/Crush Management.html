Crush Management is a New York City-based musician management company that manages Skylar Grey, Sia, Green Day, Lorde, Train, Weezer, Fall Out Boy, Panic! at the Disco, and Travie McCoy/Gym Class Heroes, amongst others. The company was founded by Jonathan Daniel, formerly of Electric Angels and several other glam rock bands, and Bob McLynn, formerly of The Step Kings. 1
