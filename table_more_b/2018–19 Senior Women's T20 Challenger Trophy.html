2018â€“19 Senior Women's T20 Challenger Trophy was the first edition of the women's T20 tournament in India. It was played from 14 August to 21 August 2018. The participating teams were India Blue Women, India Green Women and India Red Women. It was played in double Round-robin format followed by a final. India Blue Women defeated India Red women by 4 runs to win the inaugural trophy. 1
