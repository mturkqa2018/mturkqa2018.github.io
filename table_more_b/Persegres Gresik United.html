Persegres Gresik is an Indonesian football club based in Gresik, East Java. Having relegated from Liga 2, They currently play in Liga 3. 1

History

It was founded on 2 December 2005 as a merger between two clubs from Gresik, which were Petrokimia Putra and Persegres Gresik. 2
