Mantri Prasad Naithani is an Educational and Minister of Water Resources in Uttarakhand, India. He was former Cabinet Minister in the cabinet of N. D. Tiwari (Former Chief Minister of Uttarakhand) in the year 2002. Again he was voted in the Devprayag Legislative election and deputed for the Cabinet Minister in Uttarakhand. The address of his Legislative area is Village Sankro (Chauraas), Vikaskhand Kirtinagar, Tehri Garhwal. 2

Early life

Naithani was born on 20 July 1958 in family of Late Naag Dutt Naithani and Late Bhuma Devi in Village Koti, Post Banjiyaal Gaon, Patti Gyarahgaon Hindao, Vikaskhand Bhilangana, District Tehri in state Uttarakhand. He got the bachelor's degree in Arts (B.A) and then he did the bachelor's degree in Law as LLB. After getting the education he got married with Smt. Roshni Devi. At Present He is resident at R-09, Yamuna Colony In Dehradun District of Uttarakhand. 3

  7. Elected through the students council in Tehri PG College in 1987.
  8. Elected with huge votes from Devprayag Legislative Assembly for UP Legislative elections in 1989.
