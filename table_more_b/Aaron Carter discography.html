American pop singer Aaron Carter has released four studio albums, one extended play (EP), three compilation albums, twenty-one singles, and five video albums. He has sold more than 4 millions albums in the United States, as of 2013, and more than 10 million worldwide. Aaron Carter released his debut single Crush on You in 1997, which was a Top 10 in Australia, Germany and the United Kingdom. His first album was released in the same year in Europe, and sold more than 1 million copies worldwide. In 2000 Aaron signed with Jive Records and released three more studio albums, the first of them Aaron's Party (Come Get It), was his most successful album, and sold more than three million copies in United States alone, being certified 3Ã— platinum by RIAA.
