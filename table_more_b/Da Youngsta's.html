Da Youngsta's are a rap group from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania that consisted of brothers Taji "Taj Mahal" Goodman (born February 22, 1977) and Qu'ran "Q-Ball" Goodman (born March 24, 1978); and their cousin Tarik "Reek Geez" Dawson (born February 23, 1976). 1

The group released four albums in four years, experiencing its greatest success with 1993's The Aftermath. 2
