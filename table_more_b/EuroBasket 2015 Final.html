The EuroBasket 2015 Final was the championship game of the EuroBasket 2015 tournament. The game was played on 20 September 2015 at the Stade Pierre-Mauroy in Lille, France. 2

Spain won their third title by defeating Lithuania 80â€“63. The attendance of 27,372 was an all-time record. Both teams qualified for the 2016 Summer Olympics. 3
