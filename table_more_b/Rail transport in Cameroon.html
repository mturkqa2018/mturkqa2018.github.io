Extensions of the rail network to Maroua and Yokadouma to promote the forestry industry have also been recommended. 3

Separate from the metre gauge mainlines were narrow gauge plantation railways, especially in the Tiko area. These served cocoa and sugar plantations. 4

In December 2010, it was reported that a South Korean consortium planned to build new railways in Cameroon. 5
