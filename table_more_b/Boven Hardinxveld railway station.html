Boven-Hardinxveld is a railway station, in Boven-Hardinxveld, Netherlands. 1

History

The station opened on 16 Jul 1885 as Buldersteeg. It was renamed Boven-Hardinxveld on 15 May 1927. The station was closed on 15 May 1934, but reopened on 16 April 2012. The station lies on the MerwedeLingelijn (Dordrecht - Geldermalsen). The station is primarily for Hardinxveld and small settlements in the area. Trains can pass each other here, the station has a double track . Train services are operated by Qbuzz. 2
