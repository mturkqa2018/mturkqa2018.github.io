Leinster Senior Cup (cricket)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Leinster Senior Cup is the most important provincial cricket knock-out cup of the Leinster jurisdiction in Ireland. The competition began in 1919. 1

The Cup is open to teams playing in top two divisions of the Leinster Senior League. It is sponsored by IBI Corporate Finance and marketed as the IBI Corporate Finance Leinster Senior Cup. 2
