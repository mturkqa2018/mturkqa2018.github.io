Shake the Shudder is the seventh studio album by American dance-punk group !!!, released on May 19, 2017, on Warp Records. The band described the album as a "fancy way of saying 'shake it off and dance your cares away.'" 1

Shake the Shudder was met with mixed to positive reviews. At Metacritic, which assigns a normalized rating out of 100 to reviews from music critics, the album has received an average score of 64, indicating "generally favorable reviews", based on 12 reviews. Stuart Berman of Pitchfork said thatÂ !!! "[have not] lost their flair for infusing peak-hour hysterics with sobering morning-after rumination." However, in a far more critical review, Josh Gray of Clash described the album as "a terrible party bag: previous experience suggests that it should be full to the brim with party-poppers, balloons and dangerously sugary sweets but, in reality, all it contains is a few loose crayons and a small bar of vegan chocolate." 2
