Mary Elizabeth Twist (born July 1956) is a British Labour politician. She was elected as an MP for Blaydon in 2017. 2

Career

Twist is head of health in the North-East for the trade union UNISON. Since March 2019, she has been a member of the Commons Select Committee on Standards. 3

Personal life

Twist lives in Ryton. 4
