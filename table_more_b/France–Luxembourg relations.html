France–Luxembourg relations are the bilateral relations between the French Republic and the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg. 1

France has an embassy in Luxembourg and Luxembourg has an embassy in Paris. The French ambassador to Luxembourg is Guy Yelda. The Luxembourg ambassador to France is Paul Dühr. 2

France and Luxembourg share a land border. 3
