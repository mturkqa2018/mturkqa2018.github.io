Early life and career

Wannarot Sonthichai, was born on 10 April 1989 in Bangkok, Thailand. 2

Vill Wannarot's family consists of her father, mother, Kate Sonthichai, and younger sister. 3

As a child, Vill Wannarot's parents enrolled her in piano lessons and dancing classes. 4

Vill Wannarot entered the Thai entertainment industry alongside Son Songpaisarn when they both auditioned together at Exact. Son Songpaisarn and Vill Wannarot were later paired up and debuted as leading roles in the lakorn, Kaew Lorm Petch. It was a success and achieved a rating of 17 for its highest rating for an episode. Yuke Songpaisan and Vill Wannarot quickly became one of the most popular "koojins" (golden couples) during that era. 5
