Patrick Schliwa (born 8 April 1985) is a German international rugby union player, playing for the Heidelberger RK in the Rugby-Bundesliga and the German national rugby union team. 1

Schliwa played in the 2009, 2010, 2011 and 2012 German championship final for Heidelberger RK, losing the first one and winning the following three. Schliwa scored a try in the lost 2009 final, the only one for HRK in the game. 2

He made his debut for Germany in a friendly against Hong Kong on 12 December 2009. 3
