Miyakojima Station (都島駅, Miyakojima-eki) is a railway station on the Osaka Metro Tanimachi Line in Miyakojima-ku, Osaka, Japan. It is numbered "T17". 2

Layout

The station has an island platform serving two tracks on the second basement ("B2F") level. Around half of the trains return for Fuminosato, Kire-Uriwari and Yaominami at this station. 3
