Dream No. 7 is the second studio album by the German band Reamonn. It was recorded in 2001 and released on November 19, 2001. 2

The album title comes from the number of the band's former home and rehearsal room in Munzingen, a suburb of Freiburg. 3

With the single "Weep", Reamonn had a guest appearance in the German film Mondscheintarif. 4
