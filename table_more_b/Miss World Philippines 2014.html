Miss World Philippines 2014 is the 4th edition of CQGQI's (Cory Quirino Global Quest, Inc.) Miss World Philippines Pageant, held on 12 October 2014< in Mall of Asia Arena, Pasay City. 2

Miss World Philippines 2013 and Miss World 2013 Megan Lynne Talde Young crowned her successor, Valerie Clacio Weigmann at the end of the event. 3

She represented the Philippines in Miss World 2014 pageant held in London and she made it to the Top 25. 4

Contestants

Alexa Rae Kirby was the first official delegate to be announced after winning Miss Philippines Quest USA on 12 July at Symphony Space in New York City. Then the next batch of 20 official candidates were announced on 10 September from a 2-day screening at the Raven Club Boutique in Bonifacio Global City. The remaining six (6) were from provincial screenings which was determined on 12 September at Miss World Philippines headquarters in Makati. 7

Crossovers

  • Candidate #4 Priscilla Kimberley dela Cruz was Miss Bikini Philippines 2012 and Mutya ng Pilipinas 2012 candidate
  • Candidate #7 Rachel Peters was later crowned Miss Universe Philippines 2017 at Binibining Pilipinas 2017
  • Candidate #9 Ranielah Marie Oval was Miss Bikini Philippines 2013 Top 12
  • Candidate #14 Nicole Kim Donesa was Binibining Pilipinas 2013 GOLD candidate
  • Candidate #16 Ina Dominica Guerrero was Miss Philippines Earth 2012 candidate and Binibining Pilipinas 2015 candidate
  • Candidate #18 Ruffa Nava was Binibining Pilipinas 2017 Top 25
  • Candidate #20 Nelda Ibe was Mutya ng Pilipinas 2013 candidate and later crowned as Miss Globe Philippines 2017 at Binibining Pilipinas 2017

Post-Pageant Notes

  • Miss World Philippines 2014, Valerie Weigmann competed at Miss World 2014 on December 14, 2014 at London, UK where she placed Top 25 breaking the trend of non placement of the national successors of the reigning Miss World since 2003.
