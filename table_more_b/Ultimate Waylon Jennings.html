Ultimate Waylon Jennings is a compilation album by American country music artist Waylon Jennings. It was released by RCA Records on March 23, 2004. The album peaked at number 16 on the Billboard Top Country Albums chart. The album has sold 667,900 copies in the United States as of November 2017. 1
