Kevin Scott (born May 19, 1969) is a former professional American football cornerback in the National Football League. He played four seasons for the Detroit Lions. He attended Stanford University where he was an All-Pac 10 1st team selection as a Defensive Back. He was chosen by the Detroit Lions with the 91st pick in the 4th rd. He played 4 seasons with the Lions. His career included stops in Denver,Chicago, NFL Europe-Scotland Claymores,XFL-Las Vegas Outlaws,AFL-Detroit Fury and CFL-Toronto Argonauts. 1
