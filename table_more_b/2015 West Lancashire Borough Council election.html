2015 West Lancashire Borough Council election

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

1

The 2015 West Lancashire Borough Council election took place on 7 May 2015, to elect members of West Lancashire Borough Council in Lancashire, England. One third of the council participated in the election. The UK general election was also on 7 May 2015. 2
