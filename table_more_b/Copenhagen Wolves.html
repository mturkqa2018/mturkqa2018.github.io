The Copenhagen Wolves were a professional esports organization which had players competing in Counter-Strike: Global Offensive, Hearthstone, and League of Legends. The organization ceased all operations on June 6, 2016, citing the responsibilities of its owners in other organizations, namely Astralis and Ninjas in Pyjamas. 1
