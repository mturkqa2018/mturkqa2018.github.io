"Mou Les" (Greek script: "ÎœÎ¿Ï… Î»ÎµÏ‚", English translation: "You Tell Me") was the Greek entry in the Eurovision Song Contest 1983, performed in Greek by Kristi Stassinopoulou. 1

The song was performed tenth on the night (following Finland's Ami Aspelund with "Fantasiaa" and preceding the Netherlands' Bernadette with "Sing Me a Song"). At the close of voting, it had received 32 points, placing 14th in a field of 20. 2

The song is a ballad, with Stassinopolou telling her lover about how she feels when he tells her things. 3

Greece did not enter the 1984 Contest. Thus, the song was succeeded as Greek representative at the 1985 Contest by Takis Biniaris with "Miazoume". 4
