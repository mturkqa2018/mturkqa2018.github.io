Eric Eugene Everett (born July 13, 1966) is a former professional American football cornerback in the National Football League. He played for five seasons with the Philadelphia Eagles, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, the Kansas City Chiefs, and the Minnesota Vikings. He was a 5th round selection (122nd overall pick) in the 1988 NFL Draft out of Texas Tech University. He is now a gym coach at DeWitt Perry Middle School in Carrollton, Texas. 1
