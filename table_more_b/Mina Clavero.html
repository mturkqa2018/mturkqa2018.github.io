Mina Clavero is a municipality in San Alberto Department in CÃ³rdoba Province, Argentina. It is characterized by its natural landscapes, beaches and nightlife. 1

Attractions

1

The final course of the Mina Clavero river.

The area is popular tourist site with its 14 miles of sandy beach and rocks, small waterfalls, spas and natural river basins. There are also cultural options such as the Museo de las Campanas (with a collection of over 600 bells from around the world) or the minerals museum. 2

The area is used as a special stage for Rally Argentina. 3
