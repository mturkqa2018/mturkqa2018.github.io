The following outline is provided as an overview of and topical guide to Mexico: 1

The United Mexican States, commonly known as Mexico, is a federal constitutional republic located in North America. Mexico is bound on the north by the United States; on the south and west by the North Pacific Ocean; on the southeast by Guatemala, Belize, and the Caribbean Sea; and on the east by the Gulf of Mexico. 2

  • Official English country name: United Mexican States

  • Etymology: Name of Mexico
