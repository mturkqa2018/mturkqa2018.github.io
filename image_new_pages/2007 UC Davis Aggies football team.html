The 2007 UC Davis football team represented the University of California, Davis in the 2007 NCAA Division I FCS football season. They competed in the Great West Football Conference (GWFC). 1

The 2007 Aggies were led by head coach Bob Biggs in his fifteenth year and played their home games at newly opened Aggie Stadium.
