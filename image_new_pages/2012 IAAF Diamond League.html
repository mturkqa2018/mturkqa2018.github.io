The 2012 IAAF Diamond League (also known as the 2012 Samsung Diamond League for sponsorship purposes) is the third edition of the Diamond League, an annual series of fourteen one-day track and field meetings. 1

The series began on 11 May in Doha, Qatar and ended on 7 September in Brussels, Belgium. 2
