Tsuruoka Station (鶴岡駅, Tsuruoka-eki) is a railway station on the Uetsu Main Line in Tsuruoka, Yamagata, Japan, operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East).

Lines[edit]

Tsuruoka Station is served by the Uetsu Main Line, and is located 139.4 kilometers from the starting point of the line at Niitsu Station.

Station layout[edit]

Tsuruoka Station has one side platform and one island platform connected to the station building by a footbridge.

The station has a Midori no Madoguchi staffed ticket office, a NewDays convenience store, and coin lockers.

History[edit]

Tsuruoka Station opened on 21 September 1918. With the privatization of Japanese National Railways (JNR) on 1 April 1987, the station came under the control of JR East.
