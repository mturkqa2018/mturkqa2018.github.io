Victoria Bulitko (Ukrainian: Булітко Вікторія Сергіївна) is a Ukrainian film, television and theater actress.

Biography[edit]

Victoria was born in Zaporizhya, Ukrainian SSR, USSR. Graduated from the Zaporizhzhya National University theater department, specialty – film and theater actress. From 2009, the actress of Kiev Academic Drama Theater on Podol [ru]. She has appeared in over 30 films and television shows. Also well known as resident of "Diezel Studio".
