2004 Oceania Handball Championship

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 2004 Oceania Handball Championship took place in Sydney, Australia from 7-9 June. Entered nations were Australia, Cook Islands and the New Zealand. Australia won the right to play in the 2005 World Men's Handball Championship in Tunisia.
