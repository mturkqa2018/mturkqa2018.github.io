St. Matthias Episcopal Church is a parish of the Episcopal Church in Waukesha, Wisconsin, in the Diocese of Milwaukee.

Its historic parish church was built in 1851. On October 28, 1983, the church was added to the National Register of Historic Places for its architectural significance.
