Xu Jiujing (born 13 July 1995) is a Chinese female volleyball player. She is part of the China women's national volleyball team. She participated in the 2015 FIVB U23 Volleyball Women's World Championship, and the 2015 Montreux Volley Masters. On club level she played for Shanghai women's volleyball team.
