Rubina Dilaik is an Indian actress and fitness spokesperson. She gained recognition by playing Radhika, the main character in the TV series Choti Bahu, and was playing the same role in its sequel Choti Bahu 2. In 2016, Dilaik was ranked No. 11 in the Top 50 Sexiest Asian Women List by Eastern Eye. Also in 2017, she was ranked No. 10 in the Top 50 Sexiest Asian Women by Eastern Eye. Currently, she is playing the role of Soumya in the series Shakti — Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki on Colors TV.

Career[edit]

Rubina first got recognition from Playing Radhika in ZeeTVs Choti Bahu season one & two. She portrayed the character of Simran "Smiley" Gill in the show Saas Bina Sasural on Sony Entertainment Television.

She played the role of Divya in Punar Vivah - Ek Nayi Umeed. She played the role of Sita in the Life OK's faithological show Devon Ke Dev...Mahadev and the role of Jeannie in Jeannie Aur Juju. She also participated in Nachle Ve with Saroj Khan. Currently she is playing the role of Soumya, a transgender woman in Colors TV's Shakti — Astitva Ke Ehsaas Ki.

Personal life[edit]

Rubina married her longtime boyfriend TV actor Abhinav Shukla on June 21, 2018.
