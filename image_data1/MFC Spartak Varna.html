MFC Spartak Varna (Bulgarian: МФК Спартак Варна) is a Bulgarian beach soccer team based in Varna. The club was founded in 2012. Spartak have won a 3 Bulgarian Championship titles.

Honours[edit]

Bulgarian Championship
  • Winners (3): 2008, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2016, 2017
  • Runners-up (2): 2009, 2010, 2014, 2015
Varna International Beach Soccer Cup
  • Winners: 2017
Beach Soccer International Cup West Dewa
  • Runners-up: 2017
Euro Winners Cup
  • 12th place: 2018
