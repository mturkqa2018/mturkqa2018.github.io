Alonso Valderrama Airport (IATA: CTD, ICAO: MPCE) is an airport serving Chitré, a city in the Herrera Province of Panama. The airport is 2 kilometres (1.2 mi) northeast of the city, and 1.6 kilometres (1 mi) inland from the Gulf of Panama.

The Alonso Valderrama non-directional beacon (Ident: CHE) is located on the field. The Santiago VOR-DME (Ident: STG) is located 32.1 nautical miles (59 km) west of the airport.
