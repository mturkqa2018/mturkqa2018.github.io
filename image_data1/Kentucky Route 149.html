Kentucky Route 149 (KY 149) is a 10.640-mile-long (17.123 km) state highway in Clay County, Kentucky. It runs from U.S. Route 421 (US 421) and KY 80 southeast of Manchester to KY 66 in rural Clay County south of Oneida.

Major intersections[edit]

The entire route is in Clay County.
