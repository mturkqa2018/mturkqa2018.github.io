Ukrainian Federation of Rhythmic Gymnastics is a national governing body of rhythmic gymnastics in Ukraine.

In Ukraine the sport is known rather as Artistic Gymnastics (Ukrainian: Художня гімнастика).

The major training center is the Deriugins School of Gymnastics in Kiev.
