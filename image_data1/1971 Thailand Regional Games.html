The 5th Thailand National Games (Thai:กีฬาเขตแห่งประเทศไทย ครั้งที่ 5) also known (1971 National Games,1971 Inter-Provincial Games) held in Nakhon Sawan, Thailand during 2 to 9 December 1971 ,Representing were 13 sports. This games was the qualification Thai athletes of 1971 Southeast Asian Peninsular Games held in Kualalumpur, Malaysia.

Sports[edit]

The 1971 Thailand National Games featured 10 Olympic sports that will be contested at the 1971 Southeast Asian Peninsular Games, 1974 Asian Games and 1972 Summer Olympics. In addition, four non-Olympic sports was featured: Badminton, Sepak takraw, Table tennis and Tennis.
