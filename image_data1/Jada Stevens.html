Candace Jackson (born July 4, 1988) is an American pornographic actress, who goes by the stage name Jada Stevens.

Early life[edit]

Stevens was born in Snellville, Georgia. She is of Cherokee and Irish descent. Jada identifies as bisexual and her first sexual experience was with a girl. She worked at an Eckerds pharmacy in Atlanta prior to her porn career and aspired to become a veterinarian.

Career[edit]

Stevens was introduced to the adult film industry by pornographic actress Bailey Brooks. She had blonde hair early in her career but she is now a brunette. In 2013, she appeared on the Vice magazine web show Fresh Off The Boat where she toured Miami with host Eddie Huang focusing on food and porn. She also starred in Reality Kings 'Dong Riders', alongside Juelz Ventura.

In 2011, Complex ranked Stevens forty-third on their list of "The 50 Hottest White Girls With Ass". She was also placed on CNBC's list of "The Dirty Dozen: Porn's biggest stars" in 2015.

Stevens signed a one-year exclusive performing contract with the studio ArchAngel Productions, which began on November 1, 2014.
