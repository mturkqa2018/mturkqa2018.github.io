Miletus zinckenii is a butterfly in the family Lycaenidae. It is found on Java and Borneo.

Subspecies[edit]

  • Miletus zinckenii zinckenii (Java)
  • Miletus zinckenii improbus (H. H. Druce, 1896) (Borneo)
