The 1958 Washington Huskies football team was an American football team that represented the University of Washington during the 1958 NCAA University Division football season. In its second season under head coach Jim Owens, the team compiled a 3–7 record, finished in eighth place in the Pacific Coast Conference, and was outscored by its opponents by a combined total of 146 to 102.
