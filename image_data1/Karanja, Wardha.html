Karanja Ghadge is a town and a tehesil in Arvi subdivision of Wardha district in the Indian state of Maharashtra.

Karanja was famous for Gonghadi in past time. Its situated on national Highway no 53. Its 76 km away from Nagpur and 79 km away from Amravati. And Wardha district away from 80 km. Near about 60 grampanchayat and 102 villages come under the taluka. Waghoda is the smallest village while Karanja is the biggest village. In year 2015 Karanja becomes a Municipality. It Connected to NH53.

Weather and Climate of Karanja Taluka:-[edit]

It is too Hot in summer. Karanja summer highest day temperature is in between 34 °C to 46 °C .

Average temperatures of January is 24 °C , February is 27 °C , March is 31 °C , April is 35 °C , May is 40 °C
