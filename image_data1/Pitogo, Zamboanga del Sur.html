Pitogo, officially the Municipality of Pitogo, is a 4th class municipality in the province of Zamboanga del Sur, Philippines. According to the 2015 census, it has a population of 27,057 people.

Barangays[edit]

Pitogo is politically subdivided into 15 barangays.

  • Balabawan
  • Balong-balong
  • Colojo
  • Liasan
  • Liguac
  • Limbayan
  • Lower Paniki-an
  • Matin-ao
  • Panubigan
  • Poblacion (Pitogo)
  • Punta Flecha
  • Sugbay Dos
  • Tongao
  • Upper Paniki-an
  • San Isidro
