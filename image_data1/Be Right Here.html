"Be Right Here" is a song by French DJ Kungs and Norwegian production duo Stargate, featuring American vocalist Goldn. The song was released by record label Barclay, on 22 June 2018, through digital download and streaming formats. The song was written by Kungs, Stargate, Camila Cabello, Charli XCX, Noonie Bao, Sasha Sloan, and Throttle. "Be Right Here" was produced by Kungs, Stargate, Greg Cerrone, Kevin Calame, Tim Blacksmith, Danny D, Tyran "Ty Ty" Smith and Jay Brown.

The song received minor success on the French charts, peaking at 77.
