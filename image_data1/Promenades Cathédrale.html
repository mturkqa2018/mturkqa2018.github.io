Promenades Cathédrale

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Promenades Cathédrale is a 135,495-square-foot retail complex on Saint Catherine Street in downtown Montreal, Quebec, Canada.

The complex is located beneath Montreal's Anglican Christ Church Cathedral. Constructed in 1987, the mall is integrated into the underground city.

The complex is connected to Henry Morgan Building across the street along Avenue Union and home to Hudson's Bay Company Montreal store.
