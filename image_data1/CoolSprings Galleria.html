CoolSprings Galleria is an enclosed regional shopping mall in the Cool Springs commercial and residential corridor between Franklin and Brentwood, Tennessee, 15 miles (24 km) south of Nashville. It opened in 1991, and has 4 department stores and over 165 stores on two floors totaling 1,117,689 square feet (103,836.7 m), including the 500-seat Oasis Food Court. CoolSprings Crossings is an adjacent power center.

In May 2011, it was announced that TIAA-CREF would receive 50% ownership of the mall and several other CBL malls in an attempt to reduce CBL's debt. Sears closed its store there in 2013 and sold it to CBL, who plans to redevelop the space.
