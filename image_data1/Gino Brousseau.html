Gino Brousseau (born 4 September 1966) is a former Canadian volleyball player, a member of Canada men's national volleyball team from 1984 to 2000, and a participant in the 1992 Olympic Games. He is the current head coach of Laval Rouge et Or men's volleyball team and the Canada men's junior national volleyball team.

Personal life[edit]

Gino Brousseau was born in Quebec City, Canada. He went to the Université Laval for 5 years, being names student-athlete of the year in both 1988 and 1990.
