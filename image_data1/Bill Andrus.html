William Morgan "Bill" Andrus (July 25, 1907 – March 12, 1982), nicknamed "Andy", was a Major League Baseball player. Andrus played in 1931 for the Washington Senators, and in 1937 for the Philadelphia Phillies, playing 3 games for each team. He was born in Beaumont, Texas and died in Washington, D.C..
