The 2013–14 Burundi Premier League or, as it is also known, Amstel Ligue is the 48th season of the highest football division of Burundi. The season began on 21 December 2013. Flambeau de l’Est the champions from the 2012-13 season, were stripped of their title due to match fixing, and LLB Académic were awarded the title.
