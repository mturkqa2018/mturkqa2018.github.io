Seaman 1st Class is the fifth album and fifteenth Bravo stand-up comedy special by stand-up comedian Kathy Griffin, and her seventeenth overall. It was televised live from the Terrace Theater in Long Beach, California on August 20, 2012 (2012-08-20) on Bravo.
