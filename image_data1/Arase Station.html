Arase Station (荒瀬駅, Arase-eki) is a railway station located in Kitaakita, Akita Prefecture, Japan.

Lines[edit]

Arase Station is served by the Akita Nairiku Jūkan Railway Akita Nairiku Line, and is located 35.4 km from the terminus of the line at Takanosu Station.

Station layout[edit]

Arase Station consists of a single side platform serving bi-directional traffic. The station is unattended.

History[edit]

Arase Station opened on October 15, 1963 as a station on the Japan National Railways (JNR) serving the town of Ani, Akita. The line was privatized on November 1, 1986, becoming the Akita Nairiku Jūkan Railway.
