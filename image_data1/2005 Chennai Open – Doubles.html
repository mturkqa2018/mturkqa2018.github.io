Main article: 2005 Chennai Open

Rafael Nadal and Tommy Robredo were the defending champions, but did not participate this year.

Rainer Schüttler and Yen-Hsun Lu won in the final 7–5, 4–6, 7–6, against Mahesh Bhupathi and Jonas Björkman.
