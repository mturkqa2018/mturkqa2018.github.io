Jigarthandaa (transl. Cold-hearted) is a 2016 Indian Kannada satirical black comedy film directed by Shiva Ganesh, written by Karthik Subbaraj and produced by Kichcha Creations and SRV Productions. The film was a remake of 2014 Tamil film of same name. It features Rahul and Samyukta Hornad in lead roles along with P. Ravi Shankar, Chikkanna, Dharma, K. Manju, Guruprasad, Sadhu Kokila and Veena Sundar in supporting roles. Arjun Janya composed film's score and soundtrack. It was released on 24 June 2016.

Cast[edit]

  • Rahul as Rahul, an aspiring Film Director
  • Samyukta Hornad as Lakshmi
  • P. Ravi Shankar as Assault Armuga, a dreaded goon
  • Chikkanna as Raahul's friend
  • K. Manju as Film producer
  • Sadhu Kokila as Acting Trainer
  • H. G. Dattatreya
  • Dharma as Police Inspector
  • Veena Sundar
