Marvin Grégory Edward Torvic (born 5 January 1988) is a French Guianan footballer. He currently plays for the French Guiana national team.

Career[edit]

Torvic is a graduate of South Aiken High School and Newberry College, South Carolina. He has previously played for FC Lorient Reserves in France and US Sinnamary in the French Guiana Championnat National. February 2013 Torvic was signed by PS Kemi. In July 2014 he signed for Bayernliga club TSV 1860 Rosenheim, playing in the German fifth division.

National team[edit]

Marvin Torvic has capped 9 times for the French Guiana national team. He was a member of the French Guiana 2012 Caribbean Cup squad.
