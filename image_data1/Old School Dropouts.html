Old School Dropouts is the eighth studio album by the American pop/rock band The Connells, released in 2001. It was released by the band themselves on their Black Park Records label after parting ways with TVT. It is also the last album to date that the band has released. The record was recorded in Raleigh, NC with the band recording in different ways.
