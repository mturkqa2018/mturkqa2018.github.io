Rock & Roll Rebels is an album released by John Kay and Steppenwolf in 1987, under the label Qwil Catalog # NU 1563. LP distributed by 'Dominion Entertainment, Inc.', CD distributed by 'K-tel International (USA), Inc.' It was reissued in a remixed form with two new songs in 1996 as Feed the Fire on the Winter Harvest label.
