Inta Omri (Arabic: إنت عمري) is a song by Egyptian woman singer Umm Kulthum. It was composed by Mohammed Abdel Wahab with lyrics by Ahmad Sahafiq Kamel. Inta Omri has been sung by many singers including Amal Maher and Israeli singer Sarit Hadad.
