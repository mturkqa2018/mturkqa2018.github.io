Seeds[edit]

  1. Svetlana Kuznetsova (Quarterfinals)
  2. Caroline Wozniacki (Champion)
  3. Flavia Pennetta (Semifinals)
  4. Nadia Petrova (First Round)
  5. Agnieszka Radwańska (Second Round; retired due to right hand injury)
  6. Marion Bartoli (Second Round; retired due to a left adductor strain)
  7. Dominika Cibulková (withdrew due to right rib injury)
  8. Amélie Mauresmo (Semifinals)
  9. Samantha Stosur (Second Round)
