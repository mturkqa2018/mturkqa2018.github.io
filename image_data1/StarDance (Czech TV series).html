StarDance ...když hvězdy tančí (English: StarDance ...when stars dance) is the Czech version of Dancing with the Stars. Eight seasons were broadcast by Česká televize (1st in the autumn of 2006), hosted by Marek Eben and Tereza Kostková.

A ninth season was announced in summer of 2018, with 10 celebrities competing.
