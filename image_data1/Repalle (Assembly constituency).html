Repalle (Assembly constituency) is a constituency of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly in India. It is one of 17 constituencies in the Guntur district of Andhra Pradesh. As per the Delimitation Orders (2008), the constituency covers Nizampatnam, Nagaram, Cherukupalle and Repalle mandals. It is also a part of Bapatla (SC) (Lok Sabha constituency).

Members of Legislative Assembly[edit]

Anagani Satya Prasad is the present MLA of the constituency, representing the Telugu Desam Party. He has won the 2014 Andhra Pradesh Assembly Elections by defeating Mopidevi Venkata Ramana Rao of YSR Congress Party.
