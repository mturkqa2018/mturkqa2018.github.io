Sarah Trevis is an English casting director and member of the Casting Directors' Guild and also the Casting Society of America.

She is a graduate of the University of Manchester. Her credits include Shallow Grave (1993), Black Beauty (1994), The Ghost and the Darkness with Mary Selway (1996), Flight of the Phoenix (2003) and the series Larkrise to Candleford (2007) for BBC and "Harley and the Davidsons" (2016) for Discovery Channel. Most recently the feature films A United Kingdom (2016), Phantom Thread (2017) and Mary Poppins Returns (2018).

She is the youngest sister of Di Trevis, an English theatre director, Derek Trevis, former footballer for Aston Villa and Graham Trevis, recipient of the Queen's Police Medal.

She was married to actor Dougray Scott from 2000 to 2005.
