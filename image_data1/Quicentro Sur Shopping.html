Quicentro Sur is a shopping mall located south of Quito that opened in August 2010, and is the largest commercial center in Quito and Ecuador. It features 350 shops and 3,000 parking spaces (2,200 and 800 covered outdoor), an entertainment area, a food court, 10 cinema screens, a large plaza for events, and an ice rink in a construction area of 165,000 square meters over a total area of 10 hectares.
