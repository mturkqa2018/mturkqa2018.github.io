Main article: 2014 Wimbledon Championships

Vasek Pospisil and Jack Sock defeated the defending champions Bob and Mike Bryan in the final, 7–6, 6–7, 6–4, 3–6, 7–5 to win the Gentlemen's Doubles title at the 2014 Wimbledon Championships.
