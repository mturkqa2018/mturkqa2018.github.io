Ma Ke (Chinese: 马可, born 9 February 1990) is a Chinese actor. He first appeared in the television series Sun Zhongshan (2001), playing young Pu Yi. Ma is best known for his breakthrough role as "Sha Jiejie" in the fantasy romance drama The Journey of Flower (2015), which propelled him to fame in China. He is also known for his role as Qu Yuan in the historical drama Song of Phoenix (2017).
