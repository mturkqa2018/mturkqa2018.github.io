Carlos Alberto Castillo Ortíz (born September 30, 1975 in Barbacoas, Nariño) is a Colombian football forward, who last played for Deportivo Pasto in the Copa Mustang.

Career[edit]

Born in Barbacoas, Nariño, Castillo began playing youth football with Estrella Azul. He became a professional with Deportivo Cali.
