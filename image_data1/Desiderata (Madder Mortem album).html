Desiderata is an album by Madder Mortem, released on April 24, 2006 (except for Norway; it was released on March 27, 2006). It was their first CD release on Peaceville Records. The album spawned a digital single, "My Name Is Silence". A video was also shot for the single.

Desiderata is the Latin word for “those things worthy of desire”.
