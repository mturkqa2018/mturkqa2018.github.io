Saad El-Din Samir Saad Ali, simply known as Saad Samir, (Arabic: سعد الدين سمير‎  born 1 April 1989) is an Egyptian footballer who plays as a defender for Al-Ahly. He has competed at the 2012 Summer Olympics.

In May 2018 he was named in Egypt's preliminary squad for the 2018 FIFA World Cup in Russia.
