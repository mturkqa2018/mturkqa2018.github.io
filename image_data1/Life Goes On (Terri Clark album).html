Life Goes On is the sixth studio album by the Canadian country music singer Terri Clark. The album was released in 2005 as her final album for Mercury Records and produced three singles in "She Didn't Have Time", "Damn Right" and "Slow News Day". After this album's release, Terri Clark left Mercury Records.

The album was originally to be called Honky Tonk Songs, with the first single being "The World Needs a Drink".
