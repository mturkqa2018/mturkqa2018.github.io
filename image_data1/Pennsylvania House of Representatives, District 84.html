Pennsylvania House of Representatives, District 84

Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 84th Pennsylvania House of Representatives District is located in Lycoming County and Union County and includes the following areas:

  • Lycoming County
    • Anthony Township
    • Bastress Township
    • Brady Township
    • Brown Township
    • Cascade Township
    • Cogan House Township
    • Cummings Township
    • Eldred Township
    • Fairfield Township
    • Franklin Township
    • Gamble Township
    • Hepburn Township
    • Hughesville
    • Jackson Township
    • Jersey Shore
    • Jordan Township
    • Lewis Township
    • Limestone Township
    • McHenry Township
    • McIntyre Township
    • McNett Township
    • Mifflin Township
  • Lycoming County (continued)
    • Mill Creek Township
    • Montoursville
    • Moreland Township
    • Muncy
    • Muncy Township
    • Muncy Creek Township
    • Nippenose Township
    • Penn Township
    • Piatt Township
    • Picture Rocks
    • Pine Township
    • Plunketts Creek Township
    • Porter Township
    • Salladasburg
    • Shrewsbury Township
    • Upper Fairfield Township
    • Washington Township
    • Watson Township
    • Wolf Township
  • Union County
    • Gregg Township
    • White Deer Township

Representatives[edit]
