Micki Nielsen (born 26 February 1993) is a Danish professional boxer. He is fighting for the german Sauerland Promotion

As a professional Nielsen has won the WBC Youth and the WBC International Cruiserweight Titles.

He is the younger brother of Patrick Nielsen, who is also a professional boxer.
