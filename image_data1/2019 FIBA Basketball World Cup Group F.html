2019 FIBA Basketball World Cup Group F

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Group F of the 2019 FIBA Basketball World Cup will be the group stage of the 2019 FIBA Basketball World Cup for the Greece, New Zealand, Brazil and Montenegro. Each team will play each other once, for a total of three games per team, with all games played at Nanjing Youth Olympic Sports Park Gymnasium, Nanjing. After all of the games are played, the top two teams with the best records qualify for the Second round and the bottom two teams will play in the Classification Round.
