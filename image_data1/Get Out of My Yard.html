Get Out of My Yard is a 2006 album by guitarist Paul Gilbert. The only lyrics the album features are "Get out of my yard" which are yelled through the guitar pick-ups at the end of the first track, as well as "1, 2, 3, 4!" at the beginning of the eighth track.
