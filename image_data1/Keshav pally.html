Keshav pally, also known as Keshava pally, is a small village in Nawabpet, Ranga Reddy District, in Telangana, India. Its postal code is 501111. Its population is about two thousand people. Hazrat Syed Shah Ali Hussaini's dargah shareef is located in Keshav pally. One of its muthavally is Syed Madina Hussaini. Every year in the month of March, the URS will be held at this dargah.
