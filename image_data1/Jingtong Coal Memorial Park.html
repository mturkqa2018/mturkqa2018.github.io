The Jingtong Coal Memorial Park (Chinese: 菁桐煤礦紀念公園; pinyin: Jīngtóng Méikuàng Jìniàn Gōngyuán) is a memorial park on coal mining in Jingtong, Pingxi District, New Taipei, Taiwan.

History[edit]

The area used to be the underground coal mining site.

Transportation[edit]

The memorial park is accessible within walking distance north of Jingtong Station of Taiwan Railways Administration.
