Tan Chi-chung (Chinese: 唐啟中; pinyin: Táng Qǐzhōng; born February 24, 1990) is a Taiwanese male weightlifter, competing in the 56 kg category and representing Chinese Taipei at international competitions. He participated in the men's 56 kg event at the 2015 World Championships, and at the 2016 Summer Olympics. He won the bronze medal at the 2011 Summer Universiade.
