Nosálov is a village and municipality in Mělník District in the Central Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic.

History[edit]

The first written mention of the village comes from the 1324.

Sightseeing[edit]

  • Chapel of the Holy Trinity in the southeastern part of the village
  • Sellnerovo fort, archaeological site
