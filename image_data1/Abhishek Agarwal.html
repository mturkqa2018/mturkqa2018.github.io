Abhishek Agarwal (born March 11, 1981) in Hyderabad, India is an Indian Film Producer. He has co-produced Kirrak Party, Sita, Gopichand Untitled Film and produced Goodachari, Goodachari 2. He is also producing Tiger Nageswara Rao and Dr. Abdul Kalam.
