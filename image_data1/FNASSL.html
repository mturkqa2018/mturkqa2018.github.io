The National Academy of Sciences of Sri Lanka (NASSL), established in 1976 and incorporated by an act of Parliament, is an exclusive body of eminent scientists. Members are scientists who have achieved excellence in their respective fields. Membership is by election after evaluation by the Academy. The number of fellows of the academy (FNASSL) is 125. The current president is Professor Vijaya Kumar, an eminent chemist.
