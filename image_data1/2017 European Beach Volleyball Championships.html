2017 European Beach Volleyball Championships

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The 2017 European Beach Volleyball Championships was held from August 16 to August 20, 2017 in Jurmala, Latvia. The draw consisted of 32 men's & 32 women's teams, with 100,000 EUR prize money per gender. A 2,800 stadium was purpose built on Majori beach, with near capacity for most games. EuroBeachVolley awarded as “Sports Event of the Year” from hand Raimonds Vējonis.
