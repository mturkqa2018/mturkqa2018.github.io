The men's 5x5 basketball tournament at the 2018 Asian Games was held in Jakarta, Indonesia from 14 August to 1 September 2018.

Draw[edit]

The official draw for both the men's basketball events were held on 5 July 2018 in Jakarta. The Philippines and Palestine withdrew after the draw. On 5 August, the Philippines, through a hastily-made press conference by their national basketball federation, decided to field a team and would play in the Games.

Squads[edit]

Main article: Basketball at the 2018 Asian Games – Men's team rosters

Each NOC was limited to one team per tournament. Each team had a roster of twelve players, one of which could be a naturalized player.
