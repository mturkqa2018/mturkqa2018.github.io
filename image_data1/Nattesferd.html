Nattesferd is the third studio album by Norwegian heavy metal band Kvelertak, released on May 13, 2016. The album was recorded in Oslo, Norway. It is the band's last album with vocalist Erlend Hjelvik.

Background[edit]

Kvelertak released the first song "1985" on March 8, 2016. Nattesferd is the band's first album recorded in Norway. The band released music videos for the songs "Nattesferd" and "Svartmesse".
