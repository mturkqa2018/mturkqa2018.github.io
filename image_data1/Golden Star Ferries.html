Golden Star Ferries is a Greek ferry company operating from the Greek mainland to the Cyclades islands in the Aegean Sea. Golden Star Ferries was founded in 2011 by brothers Giorgos and Dimitris Stephanou, who also own the Bright Navigation shipping freight company.

Fleet[edit]

Golden Star Ferries currently operates a fleet of four ships, two of which are high-speed craft. One more high-speed vessel was acquired in fall 2017, namely Supercat (ex Karolin) and is expected to enter service in 2019. HSC Express, yet another high-speed vessel, was acquired in cooperation with Fast Ferries in October 2018.
