Arturo Freeman (born October 27, 1976) is a former American football player who played safety for the NFL's Miami Dolphins and New England Patriots. He attended the University of South Carolina for college. Freeman was drafted in the 5th round of the 2000 NFL Draft by the Dolphins. He also signed with the Green Bay Packers briefly.
