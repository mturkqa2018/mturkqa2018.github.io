The Usinskoye Oil Field is an oil field located in Komi Republic of Russia. It was discovered in 1977 and developed by Lukoil. The oil field is operated and owned by Lukoil. The total proven reserves of the Usinskoye oil field are around 430 million barrels (57.7×10tonnes), and production is centered on 10,000 barrels per day (1,600 m/d).
