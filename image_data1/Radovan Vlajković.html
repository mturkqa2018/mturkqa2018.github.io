Radovan Vlajkovic (Serbian Cyrillic: Радован Влајковић; 8 November 1924 – 12 November 2001) was a Yugoslav politician who served as Chairman of the Collective Presidency of Yugoslavia from 1985 until 1986. He was also Speaker of the Assembly of Vojvodina 1963–1967.
