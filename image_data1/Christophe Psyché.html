Christophe Charles Steven René Psyché (born 28 July 1988) is a French footballer who plays as a defender for Norwegian Eliteserien side Kristiansund. He has previously played for Baník Ostrava, Sogndal, HamKam, Kristiansund, Løv-Ham and Oslo City.

Career[edit]

Psyché was born in Cannes and he started his career with Oslo City.

Psyché joined Sogndal in 2014. He made his debut for Sogndal in a 1-3 defeat against Odd.

On 18 January 2018, Psyché signed a two-year deal with Czech team FC Baník Ostrava.
