Beuzeville-la-Grenier is a commune in the Seine-Maritime department in the Normandy region in northern France.

Geography[edit]

A farming village situated in the Pays de Caux, some 13 miles (21 km) northeast of Le Havre, at the junction of the D910 and the D112 roads. Junction 7 of the A29 autoroute forms the commune’s southern border.

Heraldry[edit]

 The arms of Beuzeville-la-Grenier are blazoned :
Vert, a tower argent overall 2 leopards gules.
