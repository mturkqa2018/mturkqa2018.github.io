Live On XM Satellite Radio is a live album by rock band Wishbone Ash.

Track listings[edit]

  1. "King Will Come"
  2. "Warrior"
  3. "Throw Down The Sword"
  4. "Faith Hope and Love"
  5. "F.U.B.B."
  6. "Almighty Blues"
  7. "Living Proof"
  8. "Blowin' Free"
  9. "Ballad Of The Beacon"

Personnel[edit]

  • Andy Powell - guitar, vocals
  • Muddy Manninen - guitar
  • Bob Skeat - bass
  • Ray Weston - drums
