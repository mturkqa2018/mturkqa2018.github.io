Acevedo is the fourth station of the Medellín Metro from north to south on line A and the first station on line K. It is located in the northernmost part of the municipality of Medellín, close to the natural boundary with Bello.

The transfer station is line K leading to the Santo Domingo Savio neighborhood (northeast of the city) by the Metro Cable.
