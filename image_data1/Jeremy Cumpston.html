Jeremy Cumpston (born 10 January 1967, Darwin, Northern Territory) is an Australian actor most known for his work as Connor Costello on All Saints, an Australian hospital drama.

Cumpston has four children: Hal (born 1999), Joseph (born 2005), River (born 2007), and Camille (born 2008).
