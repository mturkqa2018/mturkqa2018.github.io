The 1988 Stockholm Open was a tennis tournament played on hard courts and part of the 1988 Nabisco Grand Prix and took place in Stockholm, Sweden. The tournament was held from October 31 through November 6, 1988.

Finals[edit]

Singles[edit]

Main article: 1988 Stockholm Open – Singles

Doubles[edit]

Main article: 1988 Stockholm Open – Doubles

Kevin Curren / Jim Grabb defeated Paul Annacone / John Fitzgerald, 7–5, 6–4
