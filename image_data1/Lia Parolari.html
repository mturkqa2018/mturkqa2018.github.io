Lia Parolari is an Italian artistic gymnast who represented Italy at the 2008 Summer Olympics. She finished fourteenth in the individual all-around final at the 2008 Summer Olympics. Parolari was a member of the Italian team that won a gold medal at the 2006 European Championships.
