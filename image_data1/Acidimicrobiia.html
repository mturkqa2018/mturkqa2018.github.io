The Acidimicrobiia are a class of Actinobacteria, in which three families, eight genera, and nine species have been described, Acidimicrobium ferrooxidans is the type species of the order.

Taxonomy[edit]

The currently accepted taxonomy is based on the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature (LPSN) and National Center for Biotechnology Information (NCBI).

  • Class Acidimicrobiia Norris 2013 [Acidimicrobiidae Stackebrandt, Rainey & Ward-Rainey 1997 emend. Zhi, Li & Stackebrandt 2009]
    • Order Acidimicrobiales Stackebrandt, Rainey & Ward-Rainey 1997 emend. Zhi, Li & Stackebrandt 2009 ["Acidimicrobineae" Garrity, Bell & Lilburn 2003]
      • Family "Candidatus Microthrixaceae" Joseph et al. 2003
        • Genus Candidatus Microthrix" Blackall et al. 1996
          • Species "Ca. M. calida" ♠ Levantesi et al. 2006
          • Species "Ca. M. parvicella" ♠ Blackall et al. 1996
      • Family Iamiaceae Kurahashi et al. 2009
        • Genus Aquihabitans Jin et al. 2013
          • Species Aquihabitans daechungensis Jin et al. 2013
        • Genus Iamia Kurahashi et al. 2009
          • Species Iamia majanohamensis Kurahashi et al. 2009
      • Family Acidimicrobiaceae Stackebrandt et al. 1997 emend. Zhi et al. 2009
        • Genus Aciditerrimonas Itoh et al. 2011
          • Species Aciditerrimonas ferrireducens Itoh et al. 2011
        • Genus Acidimicrobium Clark and Norris 1996
          • Species Acidimicrobium ferrooxidans Clark & Norris 1996
        • Genus "Acidithiomicrobium" ♠ Davis-Belmar & Norris 2009
        • Genus "Acidithrix" ♠ Kay et al. 2013
