List of Spokane Transit Authority bus routes

A Spokane Transit bus on route 24 passes through Downtown Spokane.

This is a list of current routes operated by the mass transit agency Spokane Transit Authority in Spokane, Washington. Routes are organized by route number. Discontinued routes and former versions of existing routes are not listed.

These lists reflect the September 2018 service change.
