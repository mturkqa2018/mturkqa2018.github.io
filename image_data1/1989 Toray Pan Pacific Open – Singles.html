Pam Shriver was the defending champion but did not compete that year.

First-seeded Martina Navratilova won in the final 6–7, 6–3, 7–6 against Lori McNeil.

Seeds[edit]

A champion seed is indicated in bold text while text in italics indicates the round in which that seed was eliminated. The top four seeds received a bye to the second round.

  1. Martina Navratilova (Champion)
  2. Chris Evert (Quarterfinals)
  3. Gabriela Sabatini (Quarterfinals)
  4. Natasha Zvereva (Second Round)
  5. Zina Garrison (Semifinals)
  6. Claudia Kohde-Kilsch (Quarterfinals)
  7. Lori McNeil (Final)
  8. Mary Joe Fernández (Semifinals)
