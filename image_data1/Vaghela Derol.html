Vaghela Derol is a village situated in Khedbrahma Taluka of Sabarkantha District in Gujarat state.

It is an ancient village, which was earlier known as Devpurinagari. The village has many ancient Jain temples of historical significance. 18,000 dasha humad samaj is originally from Derol.
