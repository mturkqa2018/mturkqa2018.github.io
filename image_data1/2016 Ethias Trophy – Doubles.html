Main article: 2016 Ethias Trophy

Ruben Bemelmans and Philipp Petzschner were the defending champions but chose not to defend their title.

Julian Knowle and Jürgen Melzer won the title after defeating Sander Arends and Wesley Koolhof 7–6, 7–6 in the final.
