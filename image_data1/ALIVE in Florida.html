aLIVE in Florida is the second live album by Rebecca St. James. It was released on March 20, 2007 as a CD/DVD combination, and features live versions of fan favorites.

Track listing[edit]

  1. "Intro"
  2. "God Help Me"
  3. "Lamb of God"
  4. "Wait For Me"
  5. "You Are Loved" (acoustic)
  6. "Beautiful Stranger"
  7. "Reborn"
  8. "Thank You" (with Joel)
  9. "Without Love" (performed by Joel & Luke)
  10. "Lion"
  11. "Take All of Me"
  12. "Blessed Be Your Name"
  13. "Forgive Me"
  14. "Alive"
  15. "You Are Loved (hisboyelroy's funk house mix)" bonus studio track
