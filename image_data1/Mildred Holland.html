Mildred Holland (April 9, 1869 – January 27, 1944) was an American actress. She appeared on stage for more than 35 years, making her first appearance on September 24, 1890 at Hermann's Bleeker Hall in New York. Her first film appearance was in 1912, in the short Two Old Pals with Otto Breitkreutz.

Her spouse was Edward C. White.
