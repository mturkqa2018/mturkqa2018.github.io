Ian Greenidge, better known by his stage name Donae'o (/ˈdoʊneɪ.oʊ/ DOH-nay-oh; meaning Gift From God), is an English singer and rapper from North-West London.

Career[edit]

The single I'm fly, released by Donae'o in 2009, reached position 129 on the UK singles chart. His studio album Party Hard was released in the same year.
