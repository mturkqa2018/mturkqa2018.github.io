The 2013–14 season is Hartlepool United's 93rd competitive season and their first consecutive season in League Two since 2006-07. Along with competing in League Two, the club will also participate in the FA Cup, League Cup and League Trophy. The season covers the period from 1 July 2013 to 30 June 2014.
