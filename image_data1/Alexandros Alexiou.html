Alexandros "Alexis" Alexiou (born 8 September 1963) is a Greek former footballer. He played for Apollon Kalamarias, Olympiacos, and PAOK, as well as for the national side. He competed at the 1994 FIFA World Cup and he played in 2–0 defeat against Nigeria on 30 June.
