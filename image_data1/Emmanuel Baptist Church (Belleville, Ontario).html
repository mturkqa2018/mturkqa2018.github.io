Emmanuel Baptist Church is a Baptist church in the east end of Belleville, Ontario. The church is located on Rollins Drive at the intersection of Victoria Avenue. Emmanuel Baptist Church conducts worship services once every week usually held from 10:30am to 11:30am. As well as standard worship service every Sunday, the church also conducts Bible studies, prayer meetings, and provides services to the community and social gatherings. The current pastor is Reverend Ray Dickens.
