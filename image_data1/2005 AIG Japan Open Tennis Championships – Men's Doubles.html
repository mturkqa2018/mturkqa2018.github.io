Main article: 2005 AIG Japan Open Tennis Championships

Jared Palmer and Pavel Vízner were the defending champions, but none of them participate this year.

Satoshi Iwabuchi and Takao Suzuki won the title by defeating Simon Aspelin and Todd Perry 5–4, 5–4 in the final.
