Košarkaški klub Zemun (Serbian Cyrillic: Кошаркашки клуб Земун), commonly referred to as KK Zemun Fitofarmacija for sponsorship reasons, is a men's professional basketball club based in Belgrade, Serbia. They are currently competing in the Second Men's League of Serbia (2nd-tier).

Since 2015, the club has been organizing the Dado Trophy of Zemun in a honor of their former player and coach Vladimir "Dado" Arnautović.
