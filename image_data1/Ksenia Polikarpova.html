Ksenia Polikarpova (Russian: Ксения Олеговна Поликарпова; born 11 March 1990) is a Russian born badminton player. In 2009, she won a bronze medal at the European Junior Championships in Milan, Italy. She also won bronze medals at the European Mixed Team Badminton Championships in 2011, 2013, and 2015 with Russian national badminton team. Polikarpova became an Israeli citizen in May 2017 and won a gold medal in 2017 Maccabiah Games.
