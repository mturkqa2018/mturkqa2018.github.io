Fédérale 2

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Fédérale 2 is a rugby union club championship division in France. It is the fourth division of rugby above Fédérale 3. Teams can earn promotion to Fédérale 1, and subsequently, the professional leagues such as Rugby Pro D2 and the Top 14.
