Little Fighter is a single and multiplayer beat 'em up game designed and developed by Marti Wong. It was released in 1995.

Game play[edit]

In this game, players can choose to play as one of 11 characters. Each character has exactly 3 special moves. Players participate in a battle in three different modes. The game allows for a one on one match, team matches or elimination tournament matches. There can be up to 8 fighters simultaneously on screen. There can be up to 4 human players on the same game. Playing with a joystick is also an available option, making the game more versatile.

Game Modes Menu[edit]

There are 3 different game modes:

Free For All (FFA)[edit]

In FFA, players can choose how many players they want; from between 2-8 players. Each player chooses a character in the character selection screen and which stage in which to fight on. Players fight until only one player remains alive.

Team Play[edit]

In Team Play mode, players can pick their team, in the character selection menu. After character selection, players are placed in the chosen stage, and the battle begins.

Battle Mode[edit]

In Battle Mode, players can choose how many characters play in the tournament. It is an elimination-type game.

Character Selection[edit]

In the character selection, human players choose one of the 11 characters. After every player has chosen a character, players can then select if they want a computer-controlled player(s) to participate in the battle.

Characters[edit]

Each character has 3 special moves. There are 11 playable characters in the game, and no two player can choose the same character:

  • Davis: A melee fighter with a powerful flying uppercut.
Special Moves:
Ball - Shoots an energy ball
Grab - Runs forward and grabs an enemy
Uppercut - Launches vertically with a powerful punch
  • Dennis: Similar to Davis, but uses a multihit spinkick instead of an uppercut.
Special Moves:
Ball - Shoots an energy ball
Grab - Runs forward and grabs an enemy
Spinkick - Flies horizontally while hitting enemies in the way multiple times.
  • Deep: Can summon a sword usable only by Deep, and is required for 2 of his special moves.
Special Moves:
Fireball - Attacks produce short ranged energy balls. Only usable with a Sword.
Charge - Charge forward and stab enemies in the way. Only usable with any non-heavy/throw-only weapon.
Bomb - Creates an explosion directly in front of Deep.
  • Firen: His special moves can set the enemy on fire, torching others and preventing escape.
Special Moves:
Fireball - Shoots a fireball, igniting enemies.
Explode - Unleashes an explosion.
Charge - Run forward while covered in a cloak of flames.
  • Freeze: Not only can he freeze his enemies, he can also convert enemy projectiles into iceballs.
Special Moves:
Iceball - Shoots an iceball, freezing enemies and iceballs.
Sword - Creates an ice sword. This weapon can be stolen by other players.
Cyclone - Move horizontally while spinning to summon a whirlwind.
  • Henry: Can summon a bow usable only be Henry, allowing him to shoot enemies at range.
Special Moves:
Arrow - Shoots an arrow. Only usable with a Bow.
Blast - Unleash a powerful shockwave that trumps all other projectiles.
Flute - Play the flute, causing enemies to be lifted into the air.
  • John: A magician with good zoning spells and the ability to heal himself rapidly.
Special Moves:
Ball - Shoots an energy ball.
Disk - Carries a homing energy disk which can be thrown at will like a heavy weapon.
Healing - Heal yourself rapidly.
  • Louis: A fighter whose attacks are relatively slow but powerful.
Special Moves:
Air Punch - Performs a diagonal energy punch. Must be in mid-air.
Air Kick - Flies horizontally while hitting enemies in the way multiple times.
Blast - Unleash shockwaves in both directions.
  • Rudolf: A ninja who can harass his foes at any range.
Special Moves:
Teleport - Teleports behind an enemy.
Shuriken - Unleash a flurry of shurikens. Notably cannot be frozen by iceballs.
Invisible - Become invulnerable and only visible as a shadow.
  • Woody: A fighter who can easily close the distance to his enemies.
Special Moves:
Ball - Shoots an energy ball.
Teleport - Teleports behind an enemy. Slightly faster than Rudolf's teleport.
Launch - Launch forward in a powerful knee attack.
  • Julian: A secret character with overwhelmingly powerful attacks.
Special Moves
Soul Punch - Run forward with an energy fist.
Homing Skulls - Unleash a barrage of homing skulls.
Big Bang - Unleash a massive explosion.

Cheat[edit]

Input the commands to activate the cheats:

F1 = Unlocks Julian

F1 + '0' = Changes the background

F1 + '[' = Full HP for player 1

F1 + ']' = Full HP for player 2

F1 + '-' = Drops random weapons

F1 + '=' = Deletes weapon

F2 + 'Character Number' = Controls the character corresponding to the number

F10 = Ends the battle

Sequels[edit]

Little Fighter 2 is the sequel to Little Fighter. It was created by Marti Wong and Starsky Wong in 1999. After the release, it had several updates. In 2005, Marti Wong collaborated with Oscar Chu, to release the MMO sequel Little Fighter Online.
