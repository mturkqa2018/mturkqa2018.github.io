Curiously Cinnamon (formerly known as Cinnamon Grahams) is a breakfast cereal produced by Cereal Partners under the Nestl√© brand. It is sold throughout The United Kingdom and Republic of Ireland.

About[edit]

This section may be confusing or unclear to readers. (March 2019) (Learn how and when to remove this template message)

Curiously Cinnamon was first introduced to the United Kingdom and Ireland, several decades ago by Cereal Partners, as Cinnamon Toast Crunch, the same name as the popular North American cereal brand. The name was later changed to Cinnamon Grahams, similar to Golden Grahams, another Nestl√© product. Several years ago, the name was once again changed to its current name of Curiously Cinnamon.

Curiously Cinnamon consists of wholegrain wheat and rice squares about 1.37cm in size, which are then coated with a blend of cinnamon and sugar, and fortified with various vitamins and minerals.
