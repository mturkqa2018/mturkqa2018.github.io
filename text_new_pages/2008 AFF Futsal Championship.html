The 2008 AFF Futsal Championship was held in Bangkok, Thailand from 27 August to 31 August 2008. 1

Hosts Thailand have sent their under-21 team for this tournament as their senior team were preparing for the FIFA Futsal World Cup. While Laos are making their debut in this competition. 2
